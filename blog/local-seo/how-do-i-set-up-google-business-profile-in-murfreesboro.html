Creating a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to making your Google Business Profile stand out:

  • Claim your profile: Start by claiming your business on Google. Just search for your business name and follow the prompts. If you're a new business in Murfreesboro, consider mentioning your proximity to popular spots like the Middle Tennessee State University or the Murfreesboro Center for the Arts to help with local SEO.
  • Accurate info: Make sure your name, address, and phone number (NAP) are correct. For instance, if you are located near The Avenue Murfreesboro shopping center, include that in your address for easy reference.
  • Categories: Choose the right categories that reflect your business. This helps Google understand what you do. If you run a café, consider categories like "Café" or "Coffee Shop."
  • Photos: Add high-quality images of your business, products, and services. Pictures can say a thousand words! Including photos of popular local dishes or your store's ambiance can attract attention from foodies and shoppers alike.
  • Hours of operation: Clearly list when you’re open. Trust me on this one—people want to know when they can visit you, especially during local events like the Murfreesboro Christmas Parade or the Farmers Market.
  • Engage with customers: Respond to reviews, answer questions, and post updates. This builds trust and shows you care. Highlight your involvement in community events, like local festivals or charity drives, to connect with your audience.
  • Utilize posts: Share promotions or news about your business. This keeps your profile fresh and engaging. Inform locals about upcoming events or specials to draw them in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my business on Google?

Simply search for your business name on Google, and if it appears, click on it. You’ll see an option to claim it. Follow the instructions to get started.

Q: Can I change my business hours?

Yes! You can update your hours anytime from your Google Business Profile dashboard. If you're adjusting hours for local events or holidays, be sure to communicate that to your customers.

Q: What kind of photos should I upload?

Upload photos that showcase your business, like your storefront, products, team, and even happy customers (with their permission). Good visuals attract attention! Consider including photos from local events or markets to highlight your community engagement.
