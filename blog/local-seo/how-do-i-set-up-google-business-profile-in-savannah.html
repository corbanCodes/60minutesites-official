What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret: a well-optimized Google Business Profile makes your business stand out in a city filled with unique offerings. Trust me on this one, it’s not just about filling in the basics. To make it shine, focus on these key elements:

  • Business Name: Use your official business name for consistency, especially since Savannah's locals appreciate authenticity.
  • Address: Ensure your address is accurate and matches your website. Being located in a well-known area, like the Historic District, can enhance visibility.
  • Phone Number: List a local phone number so customers can easily reach you. This builds trust and encourages engagement.
  • Business Hours: Keep them updated, especially during local events and holidays like the Savannah Music Festival or St. Patrick's Day celebrations.
  • Categories: Choose the most relevant categories that define your business, such as 'Restaurant', 'Art Gallery', or 'Home Repair'.
  • Photos: Add high-quality images that showcase what you offer, like your signature dishes or the interior of your business, capturing Savannah's unique aesthetic.
  • Reviews: Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews, as they build trust with potential clients and reflect Savannah’s strong community spirit.

How Savannah Locals Search

In Savannah, locals love supporting small businesses, and they often search online before heading out. Here's how it typically goes:

  • People might type in ‘best coffee shop in Savannah’ or ‘local plumber near me’ when looking for services. They often seek out establishments in the bustling areas like Forsyth Park or River Street.
  • Word-of-mouth recommendations are huge here, but many folks will still check online reviews and Google Maps to confirm a business’s reputation.
  • The community has a strong sense of pride, so highlighting your local roots in your profile can resonate well. Mentioning local events or collaborations can enhance this connection.
  • Residents often look for businesses that are nearby and easy to reach, making your accurate location and contact info crucial. Remember, Savannah's charm lies in its small businesses, so being visible online is key!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a Google Business Profile?

Simply go to the Google Business Profile website and follow the prompts to claim or create your business listing. Be prepared to enter specific information about your Savannah location.

Q: Can I manage my profile from my phone?

Yes! You can download the Google My Business app to manage your profile on-the-go, making it easy to update information while enjoying a stroll through Savannah’s picturesque squares.

Q: How often should I update my profile?

Regularly! Update it whenever there are changes to hours, services, or if you have special promotions. Given Savannah’s dynamic tourism season, timely updates can attract both locals and visitors.
