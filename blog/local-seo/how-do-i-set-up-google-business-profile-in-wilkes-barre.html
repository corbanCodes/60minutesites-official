Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is not just a formality; it’s a powerful tool that can significantly impact your local business's success. Follow these detailed steps to set it up effectively:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile website and clicking on 'Manage now.' This is your gateway to creating an impactful online presence.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure to input your business name exactly as it appears on your storefront and ensure your address matches your official documentation. This consistency helps with your visibility in local searches.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best represents your offerings. For instance, if you run a cozy bakery, select 'Bakery' or 'Coffee Shop' to help customers find you more easily.
  • Input your contact information: Include a reliable phone number and a link to your website. This ensures potential customers can reach you easily and provides them with more information about your services or products.
  • Verify your business: Verification can be completed through a phone call, postcard, or email, depending on what you prefer. This step is crucial as it confirms your business’s legitimacy to Google and potential customers.
  • Add engaging photos: Include high-quality images of your storefront, products, and team members. Eye-catching visuals can draw in local customers—think of including images of your best-selling items or events you’ve hosted.
  • Write a compelling business description: This is your chance to tell your story. Highlight what makes your business unique, whether it’s a family recipe, locally sourced ingredients, or your commitment to community service.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Ask satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, and make sure to respond to them. Engaging with your audience shows that you value their opinions and can foster loyalty.

How Locals in Wilkes-Barre Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Wilkes-Barre search for businesses can help you tailor your Google Business Profile for maximum impact. Here are some key insights:

  • Many locals rely on Google Maps: Residents often use Google Maps for directions and to find nearby services. Ensure your address is accurate to improve your chances of appearing in local search results.
  • Keywords matter: Incorporate relevant keywords into your business description and posts. For example, if you own a florist, using terms like 'Wilkes-Barre flower delivery' can help attract local customers searching for those services.
  • Local landmarks influence searches: Being located near popular spots like Kirby Park or the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton International Airport can boost visibility. Mention proximity to these locations in your description to attract visitors who may be looking for local services.
  • Optimize for mobile: With so many people searching on their smartphones, ensure that your website is mobile-friendly and loads quickly. A seamless experience can make the difference between gaining a customer or losing one.
  • Leverage word of mouth: Wilkes-Barre has a strong community culture, and locals often seek recommendations through social media groups or community boards. Maintain an active online presence to enhance discoverability and engagement.

By setting up an optimized Google Business Profile and understanding local search behaviors, you can significantly increase your business's visibility in Wilkes-Barre.
