Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting started: it’s easier than you might think! Just follow these simple steps:

  • Claim your business: Go to Google Business Profile and verify that you own your business. Ensure your business is listed accurately under the right location, particularly as Lexington is home to numerous attractions like the Kentucky Horse Park and the Mary Todd Lincoln House.
  • Fill out your profile completely: Add your business name, address, phone number, and website. Make sure your address is precise, as Lexington-Fayette is a sprawling area with distinct neighborhoods like Chevy Chase and the historic downtown.
  • Choose the right categories: Pick categories that accurately represent your business to help Google show you to the right customers. For example, if you run a local restaurant, consider categories like “American Restaurant” or “Farm-to-Table.”
  • Add photos: Upload high-quality images of your business, products, or services—this draws people in! Given Lexington-Fayette’s picturesque landscapes and vibrant arts scene, consider showcasing your business against local landmarks.
  • Keep your hours updated: Make sure to list your regular hours and any special holiday hours. Many locals enjoy dining out or shopping during events like the Lexington Farmers Market or the Great American Brass Band Festival, so align your hours with community activities.

Trust me on this one, a complete profile can really make a difference in how visible you are to potential customers.

How Lexington-Fayette Locals Search

In Lexington-Fayette, locals are all about supporting their community. When they search for businesses, they often look for nearby options, which is where your Google Business Profile shines! Here’s how it works:

  • Locals frequently use phrases like "best coffee near me" or "top-rated restaurants in Lexington" when searching online. With the city’s emphasis on local dining experiences, keywords related to “Lexington eateries” can be beneficial.
  • Word-of-mouth is still super strong here; people love to ask friends for recommendations, but they also jump online to find what’s nearby. Events like the Lexington Art Walk encourage community engagement and sharing of local business favorites.
  • Many residents check Google reviews before trying a new place, so having good reviews on your profile can really boost your chances of being chosen. Lexington-Fayette hosts numerous review platforms, and locals take pride in supporting businesses with good reputations.

The best part is, your profile can help bridge that gap between online discovery and local support. When you make it easy for people to find you, they’re more likely to come through your doors!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take for my profile to show up on Google?

Once you complete your profile and verify it, it can take a few days to a week for it to start showing up in searches. The speed can vary based on location and business type, but Lexington-Fayette’s busy local economy may expedite visibility.

Q: Can I update my profile anytime?

Absolutely! You can update your business information, photos, and posts whenever you need to. Regular updates can be particularly beneficial during popular events like the Lexington Bluegrass Festival.

Q: What if my business hours change?

Just log in to your profile and update your hours. Keeping this information accurate helps customers know when to visit. Consider adjusting your hours for special events at landmarks like Rupp Arena or during the Kentucky Derby season.
