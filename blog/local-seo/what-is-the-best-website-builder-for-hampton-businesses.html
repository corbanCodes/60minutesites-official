Life and Business in Hampton

Hampton is a city rich with history, known for its deep maritime roots and the historic Fort Monroe. Small businesses play a crucial role in this community, ranging from cozy seafood restaurants that capture the essence of the Chesapeake Bay to salons that provide top-notch haircuts.
