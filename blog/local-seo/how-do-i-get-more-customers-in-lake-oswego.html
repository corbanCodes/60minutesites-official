How Do I Get More Customers in Lake Oswego?

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

I've seen this time and time again: small businesses in Lake Oswego are brimming with potential, yet they often struggle to attract the customers they deserve. With its charming waterfront, serene parks, and vibrant community, Lake Oswego presents a unique landscape for businesses to thrive. There are countless effective ways to stand out and connect with your audience, both online and offline, ensuring that your business not only survives but flourishes in this picturesque setting.

Effective Online Strategies

Having a solid online presence is essential for small businesses in Lake Oswego. Here are some proven strategies that can help you reach more customers:

  • Create an engaging website: Your website should be visually appealing and easy to navigate. Include high-quality images of your products or services, and ensure that your contact information is readily available. Use clear calls to action, such as 'Shop Now' or 'Contact Us,' to guide visitors toward taking the next step.
  • Utilize local SEO techniques: Implement keywords that resonate with the Lake Oswego community in your website content. For example, if you run a coffee shop, use phrases like 'best coffee in Lake Oswego' or 'Lake Oswego coffee shops.' This will help boost your visibility in local search results.
  • Engage on social media: Platforms like Facebook and Instagram are fantastic for showcasing your products or services. Share stories about your business, post behind-the-scenes content, or highlight customer testimonials. Create posts that resonate with local events, such as the Lake Oswego Farmers' Market, to engage with your community.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Positive reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp can significantly enhance your credibility. Consider incentivizing customers to leave reviews by offering a small discount on their next purchase or entering them into a monthly giveaway.
  • Run targeted online ads: Use Facebook and Google Ads to reach potential customers in the Lake Oswego area. Tailor your ads to highlight special promotions, new products, or community involvement, which can attract local shoppers looking to support businesses in their neighborhood.

Offline Strategies That Work

Don’t underestimate the power of face-to-face interactions. Here are some effective offline strategies to attract more customers:

  • Participate in local events: Engage with the community by setting up a booth at local events and festivals, such as the Lake Oswego Festival of the Arts or the annual Christmas tree lighting. This not only increases your visibility but allows you to meet potential customers in a relaxed environment.
  • Network with local business owners: Building relationships with other entrepreneurs can lead to cross-promotion opportunities. For example, if you own a bakery, consider collaborating with a local café to offer a special discount on pastries with coffee purchases.
  • Offer promotions or discounts: Attract first-time customers by offering an introductory discount. For example, if you run a fitness studio, consider offering a free first class or a 'bring a friend' promotion to encourage trial.
  • Create eye-catching signage: Invest in signs that reflect your brand identity and are easily readable from a distance. A well-placed sign near a busy intersection can catch the attention of passersby and draw them into your store.
  • Join community organizations: Becoming a member of the Lake Oswego Chamber of Commerce can significantly enhance your business visibility. Attend meetings and events to connect with other local business owners and participate in community initiatives.

Embracing the Lake Oswego Business Community

The Lake Oswego business community is vibrant and supportive, making it easier for you to find your niche. Here’s how you can leverage the local culture:

  • Collaborate with other businesses: Partner with other local businesses for joint marketing campaigns. For instance, if you own a florist, you could team up with a wedding planner to create a special package for couples planning their big day.
  • Engage in community service: Elevate your brand presence by sponsoring local events, such as youth sports teams or school fundraisers. This not only enhances your visibility but also shows that you care about the community.
  • Attend local business meetups: Join local business networking events to exchange ideas and gain insights from other entrepreneurs. This can lead to valuable connections and collaborations to help grow your customer base.
  • Utilize community resources: Take advantage of workshops and training programs offered by local organizations. These resources can help you improve your business skills in areas like marketing, customer service, and financial management.
  • Support local causes: Align your business with local charities or initiatives that resonate with your customer base. This not only builds brand loyalty but also strengthens your connection with the community.

Building a customer base in Lake Oswego is all about creativity, community, and connection. By implementing these thoughtful strategies, your small business can thrive and become a beloved part of the local landscape.
