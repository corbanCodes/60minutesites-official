Understanding Local SEO in Boston

I understand that running a business comes with a lot of responsibilities, but focusing on local SEO can significantly enhance your visibility in Boston. Here are some practical steps:

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing to highlight your proximity to key locations like Fenway Park or Boston University.
  • Use local keywords in your website content, such as "North End Italian restaurants" or "Boston Common events."
  • Encourage customer reviews and respond to them promptly to build trust and engagement.
  • Create location-based content, including blog posts about local events like the Boston Marathon or First Night celebrations.
  • Ensure your NAP (Name, Address, Phone number) is consistent across all platforms, including local directories like Yelp and Yellow Pages.
