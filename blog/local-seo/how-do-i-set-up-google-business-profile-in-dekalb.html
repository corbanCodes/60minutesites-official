Why Google Business Profile Matters

In DeKalb, where community ties run deep and local businesses play a vital role in the economy, having an online presence isn't just beneficial—it's essential. A Google Business Profile helps you stand out when locals search for services or products you offer. Here’s why it’s important:

  • Increases visibility in local searches: When someone searches for a service like 'best coffee in DeKalb' or 'emergency plumbing,' your business can show up in search results or Google Maps, making it easier for potential customers to find you.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile displays key details such as your business hours, location, and contact information, ensuring that customers can easily reach you.
  • Allows customers to leave reviews: Positive reviews can enhance your credibility and attract more customers. Engaging with these reviews also shows that you value customer feedback.
  • Offers insights on how customers find you: Google provides analytics that show how customers are searching for your business, which can help you tailor your marketing strategies.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be done in a few simple steps. Each step is crucial for ensuring that your business is accurately represented online:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by visiting the Google Business Profile website and clicking on 'Manage now.' This is where your journey begins.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure the address is accurate and reflects your location in DeKalb. This helps locals find you easily, especially if you’re near popular spots like the DeKalb Farmers' Market or the historic Egyptian Theatre.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best describes your business. For example, if you run a bakery, select 'Bakery' to help Google connect you with relevant searches.
  • Add your business phone number and website link: Ensure that both are correct, as this makes it easier for customers to contact you directly or explore your offerings online.
  • Verify your business: Google will typically send a postcard to your address with a verification code. This step is essential as it confirms that your business is legitimate and serves the local community.
  • Once verified, fill out your profile: Add engaging photos of your products or services, set your business hours, and write a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. Don't forget to include any promotions or special events that might attract local customers.

How Locals in DeKalb Search for Businesses

Understanding how people in DeKalb search for businesses can help you tailor your profile effectively. Here’s what you should know about local search behavior:

  • Many residents rely on Google Maps: For urgent needs like a plumber or takeout, locals often turn to Google Maps to find nearby services. Ensure that your location is accurately pinned to make it easy for them to navigate to you.
  • Local events and landmarks influence search behavior: Events like the DeKalb Farmers' Market, held every Saturday, or activities at Northern Illinois University often lead residents to search for businesses related to these events. Consider aligning your marketing efforts with these local happenings.
  • People value reviews and recommendations: Many locals look to Google reviews or social media for recommendations from friends or family before making a decision. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews and engage with them to build a loyal customer base.
  • Seasonal fluctuations affect searches: Be aware that seasonal changes can impact the types of searches. For example, winter may see an increase in searches for home repairs or snow removal services. Tailor your offerings or promotions to meet these seasonal demands.
