How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and sign in with your Google account.
  • Click on 'Manage now' and enter your business name.
  • Select your business category to help Google understand what services you offer.
  • Add your business location by entering your address. Ensure it's accurate for local searchers.
  • Include your contact information, including your phone number and website URL.
  • Verify your business through the mail or phone call from Google.
  • Once verified, optimize your profile by adding photos, hours of operation, and a description of your business.
