How Do I Get More Customers in Monterey?

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

I've seen this time and time again: small businesses in Monterey face unique challenges, but they also have fantastic opportunities to attract more customers. With the stunning coastline, rich history, and vibrant local culture, the right strategies can transform casual visitors into loyal patrons. Whether you're running a quaint café in Cannery Row or a boutique on Alvarado Street, understanding how to effectively connect with your audience is key to success. Let's explore actionable strategies that can help you boost your customer base and thrive in this beautiful coastal town.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

To connect with potential customers online, focus on these essential tactics:

  • Optimize your website for local search: Include keywords like 'Monterey dining,' 'Monterey shopping,' or 'things to do in Monterey' throughout your website content. Ensure your Google My Business profile is complete and accurate, including your hours, location, and services. This helps local customers find you easily when searching online.
  • Utilize social media platforms: Platforms like Instagram and Facebook are perfect for showcasing your products and services. Post high-quality images of your offerings, share customer testimonials, and announce any events or special promotions. Engage with your followers by responding to comments and messages promptly. Consider using Instagram Stories to highlight daily specials or behind-the-scenes glimpses of your business.
  • Create engaging content: Blogs or videos can be powerful tools to tell your story and share your passion. Write about the history of your business, the sourcing of your products, or fun facts about Monterey. Creating a video tour of your shop or a tutorial related to your services can also draw interest and encourage shares.
  • Encourage online reviews: Positive feedback can significantly influence new customers. Send follow-up emails after a purchase, asking satisfied customers to leave a review on Google or Yelp. Consider offering a small discount on their next purchase as a thank you for their feedback.
  • Run targeted online ads: Use platforms like Facebook and Google Ads to create targeted campaigns that highlight seasonal promotions or unique offerings. Tailor your ads to reach locals and tourists who have recently searched for activities in Monterey.

Offline Strategies to Increase Foot Traffic

Don't underestimate the power of community engagement and traditional marketing methods:

  • Participate in local events: Join in on local happenings like the Monterey County Fair or the annual Monterey Jazz Festival. Set up a booth to showcase your products and engage directly with potential customers. These events often attract large crowds, providing a great opportunity to increase visibility.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses: Team up with nearby businesses for joint promotions or events. For instance, a local bakery could partner with a coffee shop for a 'breakfast special' that encourages customers to visit both establishments.
  • Utilize flyers and brochures: Place promotional materials in strategic locations like hotels, tourist information centers, and popular tourist attractions. Ensure your materials highlight what makes your business unique and include a call to action, such as a special offer.
  • Host workshops or classes: Attract customers by hosting events that highlight your expertise. For example, if you own a craft store, consider offering a DIY workshop. This not only draws people in but also fosters a sense of community around your brand.
  • Offer special discounts for first-time visitors: Encourage people to step into your store by providing a special discount or a free item with their first purchase. This can turn a casual visitor into a repeat customer.

The Monterey Business Community

Monterey is home to a diverse and supportive business community that can be a valuable resource for growth:

  • Join local chambers of commerce or business associations: These organizations offer networking opportunities that can help you connect with other local business owners and gain access to resources that can aid your growth.
  • Collaborate on community projects: Engage in community initiatives and projects, such as beach clean-ups or local charity events. This not only enhances your visibility but also builds a positive reputation for your brand.
  • Participate in local farmer's markets or art walks: These events are a fantastic platform to showcase your products and meet potential customers face-to-face. Set up a booth where you can engage with the community and share your story.
  • Attend meetups: Look for local entrepreneurial meetups or workshops focused on business development. These gatherings are perfect for learning from fellow entrepreneurs and sharing experiences that can inform your own business strategies.
  • Leverage Monterey's tourism: With millions of visitors flocking to see attractions like the Monterey Bay Aquarium and Cannery Row, consider offering special deals or partnerships that attract tourists to your business. Collaborate with hotels or tour operators to create package deals that include your services.

Attracting more customers in Monterey is about blending online savvy with community spirit. By implementing these practical strategies, you can effectively reach out to both locals and visitors who are eager to experience what you have to offer.
