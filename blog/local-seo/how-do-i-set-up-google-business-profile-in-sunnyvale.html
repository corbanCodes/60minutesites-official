Getting Started with Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is simpler than you might think. Here are key steps to get you started:

  • Claim your business: Go to Google Business Profile and claim your business. Make sure to verify your listing, especially since Sunnyvale has a high density of tech-savvy residents who frequently use Google to find services.
  • Fill out all details: Include your business name, address, phone number, and website. Consistency is key, particularly in a city where local searches are paramount.
  • Choose the right categories: Select categories that accurately describe what you do. This helps Google show your business in relevant searches. For example, if you run a café or a tech repair shop in Sunnyvale, ensure you choose categories that highlight your niche.
  • Add photos: High-quality images of your products or services can attract more customers. Given Sunnyvale's scenic parks and family-friendly atmosphere, consider including images of your business in local settings.
  • Write a compelling description: Tell people what makes your business unique and why they should choose you. Highlight any local partnerships, community involvement, or special offers that resonate with the Sunnyvale community.
  • Keep it updated: Regularly update your profile with new photos, posts, and any changes in your business hours, especially during local events or holidays.

Q: What if my business hours change?
