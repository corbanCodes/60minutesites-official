Understanding Local SEO in Seattle

Look, I get it – SEO can feel overwhelming. But don't sweat it. Here’s how to tackle local SEO specifically for Seattle:

  • Claim your Google My Business listing. Make sure it’s complete with accurate info including your business hours, location, and services. This helps you appear in local searches and Google Maps.
  • Use local keywords. Think about terms like "Seattle coffee shops" or "Ballard restaurants." Incorporate these phrases naturally into your website content to improve relevance.
  • Get listed in local directories. Sites like Yelp, Yellow Pages, and local Seattle business directories like Seattle Weekly can significantly boost your visibility among local consumers.
  • Gather reviews from local customers. Encourage happy customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp. Positive feedback can improve your local ranking and attract new customers.
  • Optimize for mobile users. Many Seattleites search on their phones, especially around busy areas like Westlake Center or during events such as Seafair. Make sure your website is mobile-friendly to capture those on-the-go searches.

Q: How long does it take to see results from local SEO?
