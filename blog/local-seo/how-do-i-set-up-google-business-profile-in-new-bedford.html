Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is not just a formality; it's a strategic move that can significantly enhance your online presence. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Go to Google Business Profile: Start by visiting the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Start Now'. This initiates the setup process.
  • Enter Your Business Name: Carefully input your business name. Ensure it matches the name you use in the real world, as consistency is key for searchability and brand recognition.
  • Select Your Category: Choose the most appropriate category for your business. This helps Google understand what services you offer and makes it easier for potential customers to find you. For example, if you run a seafood restaurant, make sure to select 'Seafood Restaurant' as your category.
  • Add Your Location: Provide your business address so that customers can easily find you. If you're near well-known landmarks like the New Bedford Whaling Museum or the Zeiterion Theatre, mention these in your profile to help customers relate to your location.
  • Include Contact Information: List your phone number and website link clearly. This makes it easy for customers to reach out to you or learn more about your offerings.
  • Craft Your Business Description: Write a compelling business description that highlights what sets you apart. Include any local involvement, such as sponsoring community events or sourcing ingredients from local farms, to resonate with New Bedford's community-focused culture.
  • Upload High-Quality Images: Showcase your business with high-resolution images of your products, services, or storefront. Pictures of mouth-watering dishes or well-curated merchandise can capture attention and draw customers in.
  • Verify Your Business: Complete the verification process to confirm your business's legitimacy. Typically, Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your address. This step is crucial as it enhances your profile's credibility.

How Locals Search for Businesses in New Bedford

To effectively connect with your community, it’s vital to understand how locals search for businesses in New Bedford. Here are some key insights:

  • Specific Services: Residents often search for particular services, such as local seafood restaurants or artisan shops that celebrate the region's rich maritime history. Tailor your profile to include keywords related to your offerings.
  • Trust in Reviews: New Bedford locals frequently rely on reviews and ratings, valuing word-of-mouth recommendations from friends or online feedback. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on your profile to boost your credibility.
  • Neighborhood Proximity: Many searches are geographically motivated. Highlight your location by mentioning nearby landmarks or neighborhoods, like your closeness to the bustling Acushnet Avenue or the scenic waterfront. This can help locals find you more easily.
  • Special Offers and Events: Local residents are often drawn to special promotions or community events. If you’re hosting a local art show or offering a seasonal menu, make sure to feature this on your profile to attract more customers.

By optimizing your Google Business Profile with these insights, you can create a stronger connection with your community and increase foot traffic to your business.
