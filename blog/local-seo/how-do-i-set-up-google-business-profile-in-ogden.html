Why You Need a Google Business Profile

For any local business in Ogden, having a Google Business Profile is not just beneficial; it's essential. This profile allows you to appear in local searches and on Google Maps, making it easier for potential customers to find you. Here are some compelling reasons to set up your profile:

  • Increases your visibility in local searches: When someone searches for businesses like yours in Ogden, a well-optimized profile can put you at the top of the list, right where you want to be.
  • Provides key information: Your profile can display your hours, location, services, and even a brief description of your business, giving potential customers all the information they need to choose you.
  • Engages with customers: Through reviews and the Q&A section, you can directly interact with customers, answer their questions, and respond to their feedback, showing you care.
  • Offers insights: Google provides valuable analytics that show how customers find you and what actions they take, helping you tailor your marketing strategies.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is a straightforward process that can be completed in just a few minutes. Here’s how to do it:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by visiting the Google Business Profile homepage to begin the setup process.
  • Click on 'Manage now' and sign in: Use your existing Google account or create a new one. Make sure you have access to the email associated with your business.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key. Enter your business name as it appears in Ogden and ensure your address is precise, as this helps locals find you easily.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best represents your business. Think like a customer: what would they search for? For example, if you're a bakery, you might select 'Bakery' or 'Dessert Shop.'
  • Add your contact information and business hours: Provide a phone number and your operating hours, ensuring potential customers can reach you without hassle.
  • Upload high-quality photos: Pictures speak volumes. Showcase your products, storefront, or services with clear, bright images that capture attention and entice customers.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business, often by sending a postcard to your address. Follow the instructions provided to complete this step and activate your profile.

How Locals in Ogden Search for Businesses

Understanding the search habits of Ogden residents can significantly enhance your profile’s effectiveness. Here are some insights to consider:

  • Keyword usage: Many locals search using specific phrases like 'best coffee in Ogden' or 'Ogden plumbing services.' Incorporating these keywords into your business description can improve your search ranking.
  • Local landmarks and neighborhoods: Mentioning nearby landmarks, such as the historic Ogden Union Station or the beautiful Ogden River Parkway, in your descriptions can help increase relevance and make your business more relatable to locals.
  • Proximity matters: Residents often look for businesses 'near me.' Make sure your address is accurate and that you are listed as open during your stated business hours.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Positive reviews can enhance your credibility and improve your visibility in local search results. Consider asking satisfied customers to leave a review and make it easy for them by providing links.
