How Do I Get More Customers in Akron?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you're a small business owner in Akron, Ohio, you're in a vibrant market with plenty of opportunities to attract new customers. Akron is known for its rich history in the rubber industry, a strong healthcare sector, and a burgeoning arts scene, creating a diverse economic landscape. The key is knowing where to focus your efforts, both online and offline, to stand out from the competition.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a user-friendly website that showcases your services clearly, with high-quality images of your products or services tailored to the Akron community.
  • Optimize your site for local SEO so you show up when potential customers search for services in Akron, incorporating keywords like 'Akron small business' or 'services in Akron.'
  • Set up a Google My Business profile – it’s crucial for local visibility and helps you appear in local searches. Ensure your profile is complete with your hours, contact information, and engaging images of your business.
  • Regularly update your site with fresh content, like blog posts or service updates, to keep customers engaged; consider topics that resonate with Akron residents, such as local events or community initiatives.
  • Utilize social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter to share your story, connect with your audience, and promote special offers, highlighting local collaborations or community involvement.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Customer reviews can make or break your business. Here’s how to leverage them:

  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook, focusing on the unique aspects of your services that cater to the Akron market.
  • Respond to all reviews, both positive and negative, to show you care about customer feedback and are committed to improving your services.
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new business – word-of-mouth is powerful in a close-knit community like Akron, where relationships matter.

Networking and Community Involvement

Connecting with the local community can significantly boost your customer base:

  • Attend local networking events or join the Akron Chamber of Commerce to meet other business owners and participate in community discussions.
  • Participate in local fairs, markets, or trade shows, such as the Akron RubberDucks games or the Akron Art Walk, to showcase your services and meet potential customers face-to-face.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for cross-promotion – this can expand your reach without a huge marketing budget, especially if you align with businesses that share a similar customer base.

Remember, growing your customer base takes time and effort, but by implementing these strategies, you can build a loyal clientele in Akron. Stay persistent, keep learning, and you'll see the fruits of your labor pay off.
