Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile acts as your digital storefront, often serving as the first impression potential customers have of your business. It provides essential information that helps them make informed decisions. Here are some compelling reasons to set it up:

  • Boosts your visibility on Google Search and Maps: When locals search for services or products, your business can appear at the top of search results, especially if you optimize it correctly.
  • Allows customers to find your hours, location, and contact information easily: This is particularly important in Spokane Valley, where residents tend to support local shops. Accurate information helps customers choose your business over others.
  • Enables you to respond to customer reviews and engage with your community: Interacting with your customers through reviews shows that you value their opinions and fosters a loyal customer base.
  • Enhances your credibility and trustworthiness: A well-maintained profile with positive reviews can build trust among locals, making them more likely to choose your business over competitors.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward, and here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Click on 'Manage now' to begin the setup process.
  • Enter your business name: Be sure to use the exact name your customers recognize. This ensures consistency across platforms.
  • Select the correct category: Choose a category that accurately reflects your services. For example, if you run a coffee shop in Spokane Valley, select 'Coffee Shop' to attract the right audience.
  • Input your location: If you have a physical storefront, accurately enter your address. Spokane Valley residents often prefer local businesses, so this is a critical step.
  • Add your phone number and website: Providing contact details allows for direct connections. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and efficiently.
  • Verify your business: Google will provide several methods for verification, such as sending a postcard to your business address or a verification call. Choose the method that works best for you.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos of your business, your operating hours, and a compelling description of your services. This is your chance to showcase what makes your business unique!

How Locals in Spokane Valley Search for Businesses

Understanding how your neighbors search for businesses can give you an edge in reaching them effectively. Spokane Valley locals often:

  • Use phrases like 'near me' or 'in Spokane Valley': When searching for services, whether it's a restaurant, a handyman, or a local shop, these phrases help them find the best options in their vicinity.
  • Prioritize reviews and ratings: Locals look for feedback from fellow residents, so encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews. This can significantly influence future customers' decisions.
  • Look for businesses that are active on social media: An engaging social media presence showcases your community involvement and makes your business more relatable.
  • Appreciate clear, updated information: Ensure your profile reflects accurate operating hours, especially during holidays or seasonal changes when local residents are looking for services.

Tips for Maintaining Your Google Business Profile

Maintaining your Google Business Profile is just as crucial as setting it up. Here are some actionable tips:

  • Regularly update your information: Keep your business hours, contact information, and location current. In Spokane Valley, where events and seasons change, updating details can help you attract more customers.
  • Respond to reviews: Whether they are positive or negative, responding to customer reviews shows you care. Thank customers for positive feedback and address any concerns raised in negative reviews professionally.
  • Post updates and offers: Use the post feature to share special promotions, new products, or upcoming events. This keeps your profile dynamic and engaging.
  • Include high-quality images: Regularly add new photos to showcase your products, services, and even behind-the-scenes looks at your business. This personal touch resonates well with the local community.
