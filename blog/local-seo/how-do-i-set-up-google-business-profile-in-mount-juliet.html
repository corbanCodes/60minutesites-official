Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for local visibility. Here's why it matters:

  • Increased Discoverability: It helps potential customers find your business when they search online. For example, if someone is looking for 'best pizza near me' while they are at the Mount Juliet Plaza, your listing can pop up, bringing in foot traffic.
  • Maps Integration: Your profile appears in Google Maps, making it easier to locate your business. Imagine a family looking to enjoy a weekend outing at the nearby Mount Juliet Parks; your business could be their next stop!
  • Showcase Your Offerings: It allows you to showcase your hours, services, and customer reviews. This includes adding enticing pictures of your products or services which can draw in customers curious about what you offer.
  • Enhanced Customer Engagement: Updating your profile can increase engagement with your customers. Consider posting updates about special events or promotions, which can keep your audience informed and engaged.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile site: Go to the Google Business Profile site and click 'Manage now.' This will be your gateway to creating a powerful online presence.
  • Enter Your Business Details: Enter your business name and address. Make sure to select the correct location, especially in neighborhoods like Providence or Lakewood, which are bustling with potential customers.
  • Select Your Category: Choose the appropriate category for your business to help Google understand what you offer. For instance, if you run a coffee shop, select 'Café' to attract coffee lovers in the area.
  • Add Contact Information: Add your phone number and website. If you don’t have a website, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and effectively, ensuring your business has a professional online face.
  • Verify Your Business: Verify your business via the postcard that Google will send to your address. This step is crucial as it confirms your legitimacy and ensures that you can manage your profile.
  • Enhance Your Profile: Once verified, fill in your profile with photos, business hours, and a description that tells your story. A compelling description can include your mission, what sets you apart, or a personal anecdote that resonates with the Mount Juliet community.

How Locals in Mount Juliet Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents search for businesses in Mount Juliet can help you tailor your profile. Here’s what you should know:

  • Using Google Maps: Many locals use Google Maps to find nearby services, especially when looking for places to eat or shop. Ensure your profile is optimized to show up in these local searches.
  • Specific Search Terms: People often include specific terms like 'best coffee in Mount Juliet' or 'family-friendly restaurants near me' in their searches. Incorporate these keywords into your business description and posts to improve search visibility.
  • Importance of Reviews: Reviews play a significant role; locals tend to trust businesses with higher ratings and positive feedback. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on your profile, as word-of-mouth is powerful in a close-knit community like Mount Juliet.
  • Community Influence: Community events and social media recommendations often influence search behavior, so stay active on local platforms. Engage with local events or sponsor community initiatives, which can enhance your visibility and reputation.
