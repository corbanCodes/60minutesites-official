Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Having a Google Business Profile does more than just help your business appear in local search results and on Google Maps; it’s your digital storefront that can attract customers right to your door. This visibility can significantly boost your customer foot traffic, especially in a vibrant community like Matthews where people love supporting local businesses. Here are some key benefits:

  • Increased online visibility: Your business can appear in local search results, Google Maps, and even in Google’s local pack, making it easier for potential customers to find you.
  • Direct engagement with customers: Interact with your audience through reviews and a dedicated Q&A section, allowing you to address concerns and showcase your commitment to customer service.
  • Easy access to essential information: Customers can quickly find your business hours, location, and contact information, which is vital for driving foot traffic, especially during local events or festivals.
  • Promotion of updates and offers: Share news about special offers, seasonal promotions, or events, such as local farmers' markets or community gatherings at the Matthews Community Center.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Ready to dive in? Follow these simple yet effective steps to set up your profile and make your business pop:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Head over to the official website and click on 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be precise in entering your business name and select the correct Matthews neighborhood. Whether your business is nestled in downtown Matthews or near the Matthews Community Center, accuracy is key.
  • Select the right business category: Choose a category that best represents your business. This helps Google direct the right customers to you. For example, a coffee shop should choose 'Coffee Shop' or 'Café' to reach caffeine enthusiasts looking nearby.
  • Input your business phone number and website URL: Ensure that potential customers can easily reach you. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly, allowing you to establish an online presence that complements your Google Business Profile.
  • Verify your business: Upon completing the initial setup, you’ll receive a postcard from Google containing a verification code. This step is crucial as it confirms your business’s legitimacy. Once verified, your profile will be fully activated, enhancing your visibility.

How Locals in Matthews Search for Businesses

Understanding how Matthews residents search for services can significantly help you tailor your profile for optimal engagement. Here are some common ways locals find businesses:

  • Using specific keywords: Locals often enter phrases like 'best coffee near me' or 'plumbing services Matthews' into Google. By incorporating these keywords into your business description, you increase your chances of appearing in relevant searches.
  • Seeking recommendations: Many Matthews residents look for suggestions on local Facebook groups or community forums. Engaging in these platforms can foster trust and encourage word-of-mouth referrals.
  • Checking Google Maps: When residents are out and about, they often rely on Google Maps to discover nearby businesses. Ensuring your profile is complete with correct location details is vital.
  • Reading reviews: Customers frequently check reviews on Google and Yelp before making a decision. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, which can enhance your business's reputation and attract new clientele.
