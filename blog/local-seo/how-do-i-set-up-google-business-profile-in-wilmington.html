Setting up a Google Business Profile is essential for small businesses in Wilmington, North Carolina, as it enhances visibility and helps attract local customers. This guide will walk you through the steps to create an effective profile and explain how Wilmington locals typically search for businesses.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward and can significantly impact your business. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website and sign in with your Google account.
  • Click on 'Manage Now' to start the process.
  • Enter your business name and address; ensure the information is accurate.
  • Select the appropriate category for your business to help Google show your profile to relevant searches.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL.
  • Verify your business, usually via mail or phone, to confirm ownership.
  • Complete your profile by adding photos, business hours, and a description of your services.

Understanding How Locals in Wilmington Search for Businesses

Wilmington residents often rely on online searches to find local businesses. Here are some common methods:

  • Using Google Search: Many locals start with a simple Google search, typing in what they need followed by 'Wilmington' to find businesses nearby.
  • Google Maps: Locals frequently use Google Maps to discover businesses in their area, making it crucial to have an accurate location on your profile.
  • Reviews and Ratings: Wilmingtonians often check reviews and ratings to determine the reputation of a business before deciding to visit.
  • Local Directories: Some residents still refer to local directories, but the trend is moving towards online search engines.

Setting up a Google Business Profile can greatly improve your visibility in Wilmington's competitive landscape. Ensure your profile is complete and accurate to attract more local customers. Start today to optimize your online presence.
