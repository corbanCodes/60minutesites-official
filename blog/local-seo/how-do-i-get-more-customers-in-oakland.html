How Do I Get More Customers in Oakland?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Oakland can be a game-changer for your small business. With the right strategies, you can tap into the vibrant local market and build a loyal customer base. Oakland's rich cultural diversity and entrepreneurial spirit offer unique opportunities for businesses to thrive.

Build a Professional Website

Your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront and should reflect your brand effectively. Here's what actually works:

  • Ensure it's mobile-friendly; many people are browsing on their phones, especially in a tech-savvy city like Oakland.
  • Include clear calls to action (CTAs) to guide visitors on what to do next.
  • Optimize for local SEO with keywords relevant to Oakland, such as 'best coffee in Oakland' or 'Oakland hair salons', to show up in search results when potential customers look for services.
  • Keep your content fresh and engaging to encourage return visits, maybe by highlighting local events or community initiatives.

Leverage Google My Business

Claiming and optimizing your Google My Business listing can significantly increase your visibility. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Fill out all the information completely, including hours, address, and services offered. In a city with busy neighborhoods like Temescal and Rockridge, accurate details are crucial.
  • Add high-quality photos showcasing your business environment and products; this can attract foot traffic in popular areas like Jack London Square.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; positive reviews can boost your ranking and encourage new customers to visit your establishment.

Harness the Power of Reviews

Reviews can make or break a local service business. The truth is, people trust other customers' experiences:

  • Ask your happy clients to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook, especially since many Oakland residents rely on these platforms for recommendations.
  • Respond to all reviews, positive or negative, to show you care about customer feedback and are engaged with the community.
  • Use testimonials on your website to build trust and highlight local success stories.

Network and Build Referrals

Networking is essential in a community-focused place like Oakland:

  • Attend local events, trade shows, and meetups, such as those held at the Oakland Museum of California or during Art Murmur, to connect with other business owners and potential clients.
  • Build partnerships with complementary local businesses for cross-promotion, especially in vibrant districts like Uptown and Downtown.
  • Don’t underestimate the power of word-of-mouth; ask satisfied customers for referrals to tap into Oakland's close-knit community.

Engage on Social Media

Social media is a powerful tool for reaching new customers. Here’s how to make it work for you:

  • Choose platforms where your target audience hangs out; Facebook and Instagram are popular among Oaklanders, especially for events and food.
  • Share engaging content that showcases your expertise and connects with the community, perhaps by highlighting local events or collaborating with local influencers.
  • Run targeted ads to reach specific demographics in Oakland, focusing on the diverse neighborhoods that align with your business.

Remember, building a customer base takes time and dedication. Stay focused, keep hustling, and leverage these strategies to grow your business in Oakland. With its unique blend of culture, community, and commerce, you've got this!
