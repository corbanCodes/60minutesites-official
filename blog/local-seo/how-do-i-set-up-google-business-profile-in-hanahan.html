Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name: Input your business name accurately and choose the correct category that best describes your services. This ensures you appear in the right searches.
  • Add your business location: Include your physical address and make sure to highlight nearby landmarks, such as Hanahan City Hall or the local parks. This enhances your visibility in local searches.
  • Input your contact information: Provide your phone number and website URL to make it easy for customers to reach you. Double-check for accuracy to avoid customer frustration.
  • Verify your business: Google will typically send a verification postcard to your business address. Follow the instructions on the card to verify your listing. This step is crucial for building trust with potential customers.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, fill in additional details like business hours, services offered, and upload high-quality photos. Consider adding photos of your storefront, products, or happy customers engaging with your services. A vibrant profile can attract more visitors.
