How Do I Get More Customers in Thornton?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Thornton, Colorado can be a game-changer for your small business. With its growing community and vibrant local economy, the opportunities are ripe for the picking if you know where to look. Thornton is known for its family-friendly atmosphere, outdoor recreational activities, and a strong sense of community, making it an ideal location for small businesses to thrive.

Build a Professional Website

Your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s like your digital storefront, and you need to make it count. Here's what actually works:

  • Ensure it’s mobile-friendly; many customers will find you on their phones.
  • Use clear calls-to-action, like 'Book Now' or 'Get a Quote'.
  • Optimize for local SEO so you show up in search results when people are looking for services in Thornton. Incorporate keywords like 'Thornton restaurants' or 'business services in Thornton'.
  • Include customer testimonials and case studies to build trust.
  • Keep your content fresh and engaging with a blog or resources page, possibly focusing on local events or community highlights.

Leverage Google My Business

A well-optimized Google My Business listing is key. The truth is, most locals will find you via Google, so make sure you stand out. Here’s how:

  • Claim your listing and fill out every detail accurately, including your business hours and location in relation to notable Thornton landmarks like the Thornton Community Center.
  • Add high-quality photos of your business and services, such as images of your offerings or your team engaging with the community.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; they boost your visibility and credibility.
  • Post updates and offers to keep your audience engaged, especially during local events such as Thornton's Harvest Festival or the Fourth of July Celebration.

Encourage Reviews and Referrals

Word-of-mouth is powerful, especially in a close-knit community like Thornton. Here are some tips:

  • Ask happy customers directly for reviews; timing is everything right after a successful service.
  • Create a referral program to reward customers who bring in new business, perhaps by offering discounts or freebies.
  • Use social media to showcase customer stories and testimonials, highlighting how your business is contributing to the Thornton community.

Engage on Social Media

Social media is a great way to connect with the local community. Here’s what I’ve seen work time and time again:

  • Post regularly about your services, local events, and behind-the-scenes content, especially highlighting events happening in Thornton, such as the Thornton Arts and Culture Center exhibitions.
  • Use local hashtags to reach a wider audience in Thornton, like #ThorntonCO, #ThorntonBusiness, or #ThorntonCommunity.
  • Engage with followers by responding to comments and messages promptly.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for cross-promotion, such as teaming up with a local coffee shop for a special event.

Network and Attend Local Events

Networking is more than just exchanging business cards; it’s about building relationships. Here’s how:

  • Attend local business meetups, fairs, and Chamber of Commerce events to meet potential clients and partners. Participate in the annual Thornton Business Expo to showcase your services.
  • Sponsor or participate in community events to increase your visibility, like the Thornton Community Celebration or local sports events.
  • Create partnerships with other local businesses for joint promotions, fostering a spirit of collaboration in the Thornton area.
