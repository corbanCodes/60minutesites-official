Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for attracting local customers in Franklin. Here’s why having one is crucial for your business:

  • Boosts visibility in local searches: When potential customers search for services or products you offer, a well-optimized profile helps you rank higher in local search results. This means more eyes on your business. For example, if someone types 'best breakfast spots in Franklin', your cafe could show up at the top.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile includes vital details such as business hours, location, and contact information. This data is crucial for customers who are on the go and need quick access to your services. Make sure your hours are accurate, especially during holidays or special events.
  • Enables customer reviews: Positive reviews can significantly enhance your business's reputation. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on your profile. For instance, a glowing review about your customer service could sway a potential customer’s decision.
  • Engages the community: You can post updates, special offers, or upcoming events to keep locals informed and engaged. If you’re hosting a promotion for a local festival like the Franklin Pumpkinfest, share that on your profile and attract more visitors.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process. Follow these detailed steps to ensure your profile stands out:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile homepage and click on 'Manage now.' This will direct you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be sure to use the exact name and address you have consistently listed on your website and social media. This consistency helps with local SEO.
  • Select your business category: Google provides a category dropdown to help define what your business does. Choose the most accurate option, such as 'Coffee Shop' or 'Plumber', to ensure you appear in relevant searches.
  • Add your business phone number and website: If you have a website, include the URL. This allows potential customers to learn more about your services. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using a service like 60 Minute Sites to create one efficiently.
  • Verify your business: Google will typically send a postcard to your business address with a verification code. This step is crucial as it confirms that your business is legitimate and helps prevent fraudulent listings.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos of your products, your storefront, or your team. Also, fill in your business hours and write a compelling description that highlights what makes your offerings unique, such as locally sourced ingredients or exceptional customer service.

How Locals in Franklin Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Franklin typically search for businesses can help you tailor your profile and your offerings:

  • Use of local search terms: Locals often search for terms like 'best coffee near me' or 'plumbers in Franklin.' You can increase your chances of appearing in these searches by including relevant keywords in your profile description.
  • Importance of Google Maps: Many residents check Google Maps for nearby services. Ensure that your profile has accurate location information and consider adding landmark references, such as being near the Franklin Theatre, to make it easier for people to find you.
  • Impact of word-of-mouth referrals: Franklin has a close-knit community where word-of-mouth is powerful. Positive reviews can significantly influence new customers. Encourage satisfied customers to share their experiences online.
  • Seasonal influences: Local events and seasonal changes can impact how people search. If you have seasonal offerings, like pumpkin spice lattes in the fall, update your profile to reflect these promotions, especially during community events like the annual Main Street Festival.
