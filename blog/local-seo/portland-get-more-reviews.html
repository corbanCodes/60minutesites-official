How to Get More Google Reviews in Portland

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Have you ever wondered what makes a Portland café or boutique the talk of the town? Often, it’s the glowing Google reviews that entice locals and visitors alike. If you want your business to shine in the City of Roses, getting more Google reviews is essential. Think of reviews as modern-day word-of-mouth – they can help you stand out in the vibrant Portland marketplace, from the bustling Pearl District to the eclectic shops in Hawthorne. In this guide, we’ll explore actionable strategies tailored for Portland’s unique culture to help you gather those coveted reviews.

Encourage Happy Customers to Leave Reviews

One of the easiest ways to boost your Google reviews is simply to ask! After a positive interaction, whether it’s a delightful meal at a food cart in the Alberta Arts District or a successful consultation in Downtown Portland, encourage your customers to share their experiences online. Here are some strategies:

  • Have friendly conversations: During an enjoyable moment in your establishment, take the opportunity to mention how much reviews mean to your business. A personal touch can make customers feel appreciated and more inclined to share their thoughts.
  • Include a call-to-action: Utilize your receipts or business cards to add a short note encouraging customers to leave reviews. A simple phrase like "Loved your experience? Share it on Google!" can go a long way.
  • Send follow-up emails: After a customer's visit, send a thank-you email that includes a gentle reminder to leave a review. Personalize these messages by referencing their specific experience, which shows that you genuinely care about their feedback.

Make It Easy to Leave Reviews

If you want Portlanders to share their thoughts online, you have to make it simple for them. Complicated processes can deter even the most enthusiastic customers. Here are some ideas:

  • Provide a direct link: Ensure that your Google review page link is prominently featured on your website. This can be in the footer or as a pop-up after a purchase, making it easy for customers to access.
  • Use QR codes: Consider placing QR codes at your checkout area or on tables that lead directly to your Google review page. This tech-savvy approach resonates well with Portland’s younger, digitally engaged demographic.
  • Share clear instructions: Use your social media channels and email newsletters to provide straightforward instructions on how to leave a review. A quick tutorial or a step-by-step guide can eliminate any confusion.

Respond to Existing Reviews

Engaging with your current reviews shows prospective customers that you care about their feedback. Responding positively can also encourage more patrons to leave their own reviews. Here’s how to handle it:

  • Thank customers: Always express gratitude for positive reviews. A heartfelt response can build loyalty and encourage repeat visits.
  • Address negative feedback: When dealing with criticism, respond thoughtfully and constructively. Acknowledge the issue, apologize if necessary, and explain how you would improve the experience. This demonstrates that you value all opinions and are committed to excellence.
  • Share stories: In your responses, consider sharing a personal anecdote or insight related to the review. This not only engages the reviewer but also provides potential customers with a deeper understanding of your business's personality.

Leverage Your Social Media Presence

Your social media platforms are great channels to encourage reviews. You can reach a wider audience and remind your followers to share their experiences. Make the most of these platforms by:

  • Creating posts: Regularly highlight positive reviews on your social media pages. This not only shows appreciation but also acts as social proof, enticing new customers to check out your offerings.
  • Running contests: Consider hosting a contest where customers who leave a review can enter to win a gift card or a special experience. This can create excitement and incentivize participation.
  • Regular reminders: Use your social media stories and posts to share reminders and direct links to your Google review page. A consistent approach keeps the idea fresh in the minds of your audience.

Incentivize Reviews (Carefully)

Offering incentives can be a delicate balance, but with the right approach, it can work wonders. Here are some tips on how to do it responsibly:

  • Offer discounts or freebies: Consider giving a small discount or a complimentary item to customers who show proof of leaving a review. This can be especially effective after a memorable experience, such as a unique dish at a local food truck.
  • Clarify your stance on ratings: Make it clear that all reviews are welcome, regardless of star ratings. This transparency builds trust and encourages honesty in feedback.
  • Check Google’s guidelines: Familiarize yourself with Google’s policies regarding incentivized reviews to ensure compliance. This way, you can avoid any potential penalties while still encouraging participation.

Building a collection of Google reviews can create a powerful word-of-mouth marketing tool for your business in Portland. The city is known for its strong community ties, and positive reviews can enhance your reputation among both locals and tourists.
