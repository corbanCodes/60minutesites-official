Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your profile is straightforward. Here’s how to do it step by step:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile site and click on 'Manage Now.' This will guide you through the registration process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be sure to enter your official business name and location. If you operate from a storefront where customers can visit, select 'Yes' when asked if you want to add a location. This is crucial for local search visibility.
  • Choose the appropriate business category: This helps Google understand what you offer and ensures that your business appears in relevant searches. For example, if you own a bakery, select 'Bakery' to attract the right customers.
  • Provide your phone number and website: If you have a website, link it here. If you don’t, 60 Minute Sites can help you create a quick and professional site that enhances your online presence.
  • Verify your business: You will need to verify your business through a phone call or a postcard sent to your business address. This step is crucial as it confirms your legitimacy and enables your profile to be fully activated.

Once completed, take time to explore additional features within your profile that allow you to post updates, respond to reviews, and engage with your community.
