Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile allows you to connect with your customers directly in Hastings. It’s a free tool that helps you showcase your business to potential clients searching for services in the area. Here's why you should set it up:

  • Increase visibility in local searches: When locals search for services, your business will appear in the local pack, making it easier for them to find you. Think about how often you search for dining options or shops in Hastings – you want to be one of the first businesses they see.
  • Manage customer reviews and feedback: Engage with your customers by responding to reviews. In Hastings, word of mouth is powerful, and positive reviews can greatly influence potential customers’ decisions.
  • Provide essential information: Include your hours, location, and services to give customers all the details they need to visit you. This is especially useful for local residents looking to support small businesses in their community.
  • Share photos and updates: Upload vibrant images of your products, your storefront, or community events you’re involved in. Hastings is known for its artistic community—showcasing your business visually can attract more visitors.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Sign in to Google My Business: If you already have a Google account, this is easy. If not, creating one takes just a few minutes.
  • Enter your business name and address accurately: Make sure your address is precise so local customers can find you easily. For example, if you're located near the Hastings Riverwalk, mention that in your description.
  • Choose the right category for your business: Selecting the right category helps Google show your business to the right audience. If you own a café, for instance, you might choose categories like “Café” or “Coffee Shop.”
  • Add a phone number and website link: Ensure your contact information is up-to-date. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly.
  • Verify your business: You can verify your business through a postcard, phone, or email. This step is crucial as it confirms that your business is legitimate.
  • Complete your profile: Fill in your hours, services, and upload high-quality images. Consider taking photos during peak hours to showcase your atmosphere or products in action.

How Locals in Hastings Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search for businesses can help you tailor your online presence. In Hastings, many residents rely on Google to find everything from coffee shops along the Mississippi River to home repair services in the neighborhoods. Key search habits include:

  • Using specific keywords: Phrases like "organic grocery Hastings" or "best plumbers near me" are common. Think about what terms your customers would use and incorporate them into your profile.
  • Looking for businesses with good reviews: Hastings locals trust their neighbors’ recommendations, so strive for quality service that earns you those glowing reviews.
  • Checking for recent posts and updates: Regularly update your profile to reflect new products, specials, or community events. This shows that you’re active and engaged with your clientele.
  • Using Google Maps: Many residents use Google Maps to find locations and get directions. Ensure your location is accurately marked so customers can easily find you.
