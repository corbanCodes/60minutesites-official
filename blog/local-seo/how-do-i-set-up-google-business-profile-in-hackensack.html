Getting Started with Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can significantly boost your online presence. Here’s a more detailed breakdown of what you need to do:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the official Google Business Profile page. This is your first step toward establishing a strong online presence.
  • Click on 'Manage now': This initiates the setup process. Make sure you're logged into the Google account you want to associate with your business.
  • Enter your business name and address accurately: Ensure that the information you provide is correct, as this helps customers find you easily. Consider using a local landmark, like the Hackensack Performing Arts Center, as a reference point to enhance visibility.
  • Select the correct business category: Choosing the right category is critical for search visibility. For instance, if you run a bakery, select 'Bakery' instead of a more generic term like 'Food.' This specificity increases your chances of appearing in relevant searches.
  • Provide your phone number and website URL: Make it easy for potential customers to reach you. If you don't have a website yet, consider using services like 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and effectively.
  • Verify your business: Google offers various verification methods, such as phone or postcard verification. This step is essential, as it confirms your business's legitimacy and allows your profile to go live.
  • Complete your profile: Add high-quality photos of your products or services, your business hours, and a compelling description. For example, if you own a local coffee shop, describe your unique brews and cozy atmosphere. Engaging visuals and accurate information can attract local customers and set you apart from the competition.

Understanding Local Searches in Hackensack

Hackensack locals are savvy searchers, often looking for businesses that are nearby and have glowing reviews. Here’s how they typically search:

  • Using phrases like 'best restaurants near me' or 'top-rated salons in Hackensack': Tailor your profile to include keywords that reflect what locals might be searching for.
  • Checking Google Maps: Many residents use Google Maps to find nearby services. Ensure your location is marked accurately so that customers can find you without hassle.
  • Reading reviews and ratings: Online reviews play a significant role in decision-making. Encourage happy customers to leave favorable reviews and respond to them to show you're engaged and care about customer feedback.
  • Using specific landmarks: Locals often search using landmarks in their queries, such as 'coffee shops near Hackensack University Medical Center.' Mention local landmarks in your profile description to enhance search relevance.
  • Looking for engaging photos and up-to-date information: Invest time in curating a gallery that showcases your business. High-quality photos of your offerings and a regularly updated profile will help attract potential customers.
