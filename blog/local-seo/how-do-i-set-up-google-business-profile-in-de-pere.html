Here's something that might surprise you: many local businesses in De Pere overlook a powerful tool right at their fingertips—Google Business Profile. Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Having a Google Business Profile is like having a digital storefront. It allows you to provide essential information about your business directly in Google search results. Here’s why it matters:

  • Increased visibility when locals search for services: When someone in De Pere types in "coffee shop near me" or "best plumbing service," your business could be one of the first they see, thanks to your optimized profile.
  • Free promotional space for your business hours, location, and services: This is your chance to showcase exactly what you offer and when you're available. For instance, if you have special hours for holidays or unique services, this is the place to highlight them.
  • Ability to showcase customer reviews, which builds trust: Positive reviews are like gold in the local business world. They can significantly influence potential customers' decisions. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews to enhance your credibility.
  • Insight into customer interactions and how they find your business: Google Business Profile provides analytics that show how customers are searching for you, where they're coming from, and what actions they're taking, allowing you to fine-tune your marketing strategies.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to set yours up today:

  • Visit Google Business Profile and click on 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup page where all the magic begins.
  • Sign in with your Google account or create a new one. If you don’t already have a Google account, you’ll need to create one. This account will be used to manage your profile.
  • Enter your business name and address. Make sure it's accurate, as locals in De Pere look for specific locations. If you have a storefront, consider adding landmarks nearby, like the iconic De Pere Bridge, to help customers find you easily.
  • Select a category that fits your business. This helps Google show your profile to the right audience. Be specific; for instance, if you run a bakery, choose "Bakery" instead of simply "Food." This precision helps attract customers actively seeking your services.
  • Add your business phone number and website, if you have one. Ensure that the phone number is current and that your website offers a great user experience, as many customers may want to explore your offerings further.
  • Verify your business. Google often sends a postcard with a verification code to your business address. This step is crucial to ensure that your business is legitimate. Once you receive the postcard, enter the code to complete your verification.

What Locals in De Pere Are Searching For

Understanding how people in De Pere search for businesses can help you tailor your profile effectively. Here are some common trends:

  • Many residents search for local dining options: Especially near popular spots like the De Pere Riverwalk, where foot traffic is high. Consider highlighting any unique dishes or specials you offer to entice potential customers.
  • Service-related searches: Searches for services like plumbing or landscaping are frequent as homeowners seek nearby professionals. Make sure to list your specialties, such as emergency services or eco-friendly options.
  • Local events and activities drive searches: Businesses involved in community engagement, like shops near St. Norbert College, often thrive. If you sponsor local events or participate in farmers' markets, mention this in your profile.
  • Reviews play a crucial role: Locals often look for recommendations from others in the area. Encourage your satisfied customers to leave detailed reviews and respond to them personally to build a rapport with your audience.
