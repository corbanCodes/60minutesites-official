Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Having a Google Business Profile is akin to having a digital storefront right on Google’s search results. It not only enhances your business's visibility but also provides potential customers with essential information they are looking for, such as:

  • Your business hours: Clear operating times help customers plan their visits, especially if you're located near popular spots like the New Brighton Farmers Market.
  • Contact information: Ensure your phone number and email address are readily available, making it easy for customers to reach out with inquiries.
  • Your location on the map: Pinpointing your business on Google Maps is essential for locals navigating to your establishment. This is particularly important if you're in a busy area like downtown New Brighton.
  • Customer reviews: Positive reviews can significantly influence potential customers’ decisions, so encourage your satisfied clients to share their experiences.
  • Photos of your products or services: Visuals help customers get a sense of what you offer. Showcase high-quality images of your products, your storefront, or even behind-the-scenes shots of your team at work.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be done in just a few steps. Here’s how you can effectively do it:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile homepage. This is your launchpad for establishing a professional online presence.
  • Click on 'Manage now' and sign in: Use your existing Google account or create a new one. Signing in allows you to manage your business profile efficiently.
  • Enter your business name and choose the right category: Choose a category that accurately reflects what you do. For example, if you run a bakery, select 'Bakery' to help Google categorize your business correctly.
  • Provide your business address: Ensure your address is accurate and matches your physical location in New Brighton. Consider including a landmark nearby to help customers find you more easily.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL: These details are crucial for communication and will help customers reach you effortlessly.
  • Verify your business: Google usually requires you to verify your business by sending a postcard with a unique code to your physical address. This step is essential for confirming your business’s legitimacy.
  • Once verified, optimize your profile: Take the time to fill out your profile completely. Add high-resolution photos, your business hours, and any special offers or promotions you might have. This is your chance to make a great first impression.

How Locals Search for Businesses in New Brighton

Understanding how residents in New Brighton search online can give your business a significant advantage. Here are some common search habits to keep in mind:

  • Search phrases: Many locals often use phrases like 'best coffee near me' or 'plumber in New Brighton' when searching for services. Make sure your profile includes relevant keywords that your potential customers might use.
  • Reliance on Google Maps: Many locals use Google Maps for directions and to check hours of operation. Ensure that your business is correctly pinned on the map, making it easier for customers to find you.
  • Importance of customer reviews: Online reviews can play a crucial role in decision-making for potential customers. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on your profile, and respond to feedback to show that you value your customers’ opinions.
  • Community engagement: New Brighton residents appreciate businesses that actively engage with their community. Consider showcasing local events, promotions, or partnerships on your profile to connect with the community.
