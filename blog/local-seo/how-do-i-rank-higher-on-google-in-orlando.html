How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Orlando?

9 min read

Hey there, Orlando small business owners! If you're looking to attract more locals and visitors alike, improving your local SEO is a game changer. Let's dive into some practical tips that'll help your business stand out in the sunny streets of Orlando.

Optimize Your Google My Business Listing

Your Google My Business (GMB) listing is crucial for local SEO. Here's how to make it shine:

  • Claim and verify your GMB listing.
  • Fill out all the information completely, including your address, phone number, and business hours.
  • Use high-quality images showcasing your products or services.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews and respond to them promptly.
  • Keep your business information updated, especially if you're located near popular spots like Lake Eola Park or Universal Studios.

Leverage Local Keywords

Using local keywords can help you rank better in Orlando searches. Consider these tips:

  • Focus on keywords that include 'Orlando' along with your services (e.g., 'best pizza in Orlando').
  • Use neighborhood names in your content, like Winter Park or Thornton Park.
  • Create blog posts or articles about local events or partnerships that include these keywords.

Build Local Backlinks

Backlinks from local websites help boost your credibility. Here's how to get started:

  • Partner with other local businesses for events or promotions and get links back to your site.
  • Get featured in local blogs or news sites by sharing your story.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business groups to increase your visibility.

Create Local Content

Content that resonates with the Orlando community can work wonders. Consider these strategies:

  • Write about local events, festivals, or attractions (like the Orlando Science Center).
  • Share customer stories or testimonials that highlight your connection to the community.
  • Use social media to promote your content and engage with locals.

Look, I get it—running a small business is no walk in the park. But by focusing on local SEO, you can make sure more people in Orlando find you. Start with these tips today, and watch your visibility grow. If you need more help, don’t hesitate to reach out or ask your fellow business owners for advice!
