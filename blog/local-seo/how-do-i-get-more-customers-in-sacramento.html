How Do I Get More Customers in Sacramento?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Sacramento is a vibrant city with a growing local economy, making it a fantastic place for small businesses to thrive. If you're looking to attract more customers, you've come to the right place. Let's dive into some effective strategies that will get your name out there.

Build a Professional Website

Your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront where potential clients can learn about your services. Here's what actually works:

  • Make sure your website is mobile-friendly; many people search on their phones.
  • Use clear calls to action (CTAs) so visitors know what to do next.
  • Include testimonials and case studies to build trust.
  • Optimize for SEO with local keywords like 'Sacramento plumber' or 'landscaping in Sacramento'.

Leverage Google My Business

This is crucial for local visibility. Claiming your Google My Business listing helps you appear in local searches. The truth is, many customers will find you through Google first:

  • Ensure your information is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Add high-quality photos of your work.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; they make a big difference in attracting new clients.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Word-of-mouth is powerful in Sacramento. People trust local recommendations:

  • Ask your happy customers for reviews on Google and Yelp.
  • Offer incentives for referrals—maybe a discount on future services.
  • Join local business groups where you can network and share referrals with other businesses.

Utilize Social Media Effectively

Social media is a great way to connect with your community. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Share behind-the-scenes content to give your business a personal touch.
  • Run contests or giveaways to increase engagement.
  • Use local hashtags to reach Sacramento residents specifically.

Network in the Local Community

Networking is essential in Sacramento's tight-knit community. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Attend local events and fairs to meet potential customers.
  • Join local business organizations like the Sacramento Chamber of Commerce.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses to expand your reach.
