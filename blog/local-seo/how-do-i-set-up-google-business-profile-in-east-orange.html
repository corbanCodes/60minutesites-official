Getting Started with Your Google Business Profile

Establishing a Google Business Profile is a straightforward process and can significantly elevate your local presence. Here’s a detailed step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Navigate to the Google Business Profile page and sign in using your Google account. If you don’t have an account yet, this is the perfect time to create one.
  • Manage your business: Click on the 'Manage now' button. You’ll be prompted to enter your business name. If your business is already listed, you may find it in the suggestions.
  • Select a business category: Choose the most relevant category for your business. This helps Google accurately classify your services, making it easier for locals to find you. For example, if you run a coffee shop, choose 'Coffee Shop' rather than just 'Cafe'.
  • Provide your location: If you serve customers face-to-face, provide your physical address. For businesses that operate in specific neighborhoods, such as East Orange’s Elmwood or East Ward, specify these areas in your service settings.
  • Add contact information: Include a phone number and a link to your website. This makes it easy for customers to reach out for inquiries or bookings. If you don’t have a website yet, consider creating a simple landing page to start.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business. This is often done by sending a postcard to your business address, which contains a verification code. Follow the instructions provided to complete this step.
  • Enhance your profile: After verification, boost your profile's appeal by uploading high-quality photos of your business, products, or services. Include your business hours and a comprehensive description highlighting what makes your business unique in East Orange.

How Locals in East Orange Search for Businesses

Understanding the search behavior of East Orange residents is key to optimizing your Google Business Profile. Here are common ways locals search for businesses:

  • Direct searches: Many locals search for specific businesses by name, particularly if they’ve received recommendations from friends or family. Ensure your business name is clear and recognizable.
  • Category searches: Searches like 'best pizza in East Orange' or 'plumbers near me' are prevalent. Make sure your primary category accurately reflects your business to appear in relevant searches.
  • Map searches: With a significant number of users utilizing Google Maps, your business must appear on the map. Ensure your location is correctly marked and that your address is accurate to attract foot traffic.
  • Reviews matter: East Orange locals heavily consider online reviews. Encourage happy customers to leave positive feedback on your profile. Responding to reviews, whether positive or negative, also shows you value customer input and enhances trust.
  • Community engagement: Being an active participant in East Orange’s local events or initiatives, such as the East Orange Farmers Market or community clean-ups, can significantly boost your visibility. Residents tend to support businesses that give back to their community, so consider partnerships or sponsorships.
