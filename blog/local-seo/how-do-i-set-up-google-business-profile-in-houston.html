What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating an effective Google Business Profile is straightforward, and here's the secret to doing it right:

  • Claim and verify your profile to establish ownership. This step is crucial for businesses in Houston, where competition is fierce.
  • Fill out every section completely—especially business name, address, and phone number. Ensure your address is accurate, as Houston's vastness can lead to confusion.
  • Choose the right categories to help Google understand what your business does. Houston has diverse industries, so specify your niche clearly.
  • Add high-quality photos that showcase your products or services. Given the city's vibrant art scene, visually appealing images can make a significant impact.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews and respond to them to build trust. In a city that values community feedback, positive reviews can significantly influence potential customers.
  • Keep your information updated, especially hours and special events. In Houston, where festivals and events happen year-round, showcasing your participation can attract more visitors.

The best part is that once your profile is set up, it works for you 24/7, helping you attract local customers without you having to lift a finger.

Trust me on this one—if you're not on Google, you're missing out on a huge chunk of local traffic. In a city where food trucks and local eateries are part of the fabric of daily life, being discoverable online is essential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take for my profile to appear on Google?

Once you verify your profile, it usually takes a few days to start showing up in local search results. Many Houston businesses report visibility within 48 hours post-verification.

Q: Can I manage my profile from my phone?

Yes! Google has a mobile app that allows you to manage your profile on the go, making it easy for Houston business owners who are always on the move.

Q: What type of photos should I upload?

Focus on high-quality images of your products, your storefront, and any events you host. Given Houston's diverse and artistic culture, consider showcasing local art or unique aspects of your business.

Q: How can I get more reviews?

Ask happy customers to leave a review after their purchase. You can also send follow-up emails with a direct link to your profile. In Houston, consider offering a small incentive, like a discount on a future purchase, to encourage reviews.

Q: Is there a cost to use Google Business Profile?

No, setting up your Google Business Profile is completely free! This is an excellent opportunity for Houston entrepreneurs looking to maximize their marketing budget.
