Here's something that might surprise you: many local customers in New Brunswick, New Jersey, rely heavily on Google to discover their favorite shops, restaurants, and services. With the right setup, you can enhance your visibility, attract more foot traffic, and build lasting relationships with your community.

Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Having a Google Business Profile is essential for visibility in local searches, especially in a bustling area like New Brunswick. This free tool allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps, which is where many customers begin their journey. Here are some compelling benefits:

  • Increased Visibility in Local Searches: Your business can show up in local search results and Google Maps, making it easier for potential customers to find you when they search for related services.
  • Essential Information at Your Fingertips: You can provide crucial details like hours of operation, address, and contact information, ensuring that customers have all they need to visit your business.
  • Showcase Customer Reviews and Photos: Positive reviews can significantly enhance your credibility. Displaying high-quality photos of your products or services can attract attention and entice more customers.
  • Engagement with Customers: You can create posts, respond to customer inquiries, and manage reviews directly through your profile, fostering a community around your brand.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is straightforward. By following these steps, you can make your business more visible and accessible to local customers:

  • Create or Claim Your Profile: Visit Google My Business and either claim your existing business listing or create a new one. If you’re claiming an existing listing, ensure that the information is accurate and up to date.
  • Enter Your Business Information: Fill out your business name, address, and phone number accurately. It’s crucial that this information matches what’s displayed on your website and other online platforms to avoid confusion.
  • Select Your Business Category: Choose the category that best describes your business. For instance, if you own a coffee shop, select “Coffee Shop” as your primary category. This helps Google show your listing to the right audience searching for specific services.
  • Verify Your Business: Follow the verification process, which often involves receiving a postcard from Google at your business address. This step is vital as it confirms your legitimacy and allows you to manage your listing effectively.
  • Add Photos: Upload high-quality images that showcase your products, services, and the overall ambiance of your business. Consider including pictures of your team, your storefront, and even happy customers enjoying your offerings. Visuals can make a significant impact on attracting new customers.

How Locals Search for Businesses in New Brunswick

Understanding how residents of New Brunswick search for businesses can help you tailor your profile and increase its effectiveness. Many locals look for:

  • Specific Services: Searches often include specific terms, such as "best pizza in New Brunswick" or "affordable plumbing services." By using keywords relevant to your business in your profile description, you can improve your chances of appearing in these searches.
  • Location-Based Queries: Many searches include neighborhood names, such as "restaurants near the Rutgers campus" or "shops in downtown New Brunswick." Make sure your listing reflects your exact location to capture these localized searches effectively.
  • Reviews and Recommendations: Locals frequently check reviews to gauge the quality of a business. Encouraging satisfied customers to leave positive feedback can make a significant difference in how your business is perceived. Consider implementing follow-up emails after a purchase or visit to kindly ask for a review.
