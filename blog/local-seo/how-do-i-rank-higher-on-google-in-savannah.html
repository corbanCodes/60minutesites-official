How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Savannah?

9 min read

Hey there, fellow business owner in Savannah! If you're looking to boost your local SEO and get more customers through your doors, you're in the right place. Let's dive into some practical strategies that can help you stand out in our beautiful city, known for its historic charm and vibrant culture.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Here's the thing: your Google Business Profile is often the first impression potential customers get of your business. To make it count, you should:

  • Claim and verify your profile.
  • Fill out all the information completely, including address, phone number, and hours of operation. Make sure your address is listed correctly in the Historic District if that's where you are!
  • Add high-quality images that showcase your products or services, especially those that capture the essence of Savannah, such as the moss-draped oaks or the River Street waterfront.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, as they can really help your ranking in Savannah searches.

Use Local Keywords Strategically

Look, I get it. Knowing what keywords to use can feel overwhelming. But focusing on local keywords is key. Here’s how:

  • Think about what your customers are searching for; include terms like "Savannah bakery" or "best coffee in Forsyth Park." Use popular local attractions and neighborhoods in your keywords, like "Savannah Riverfront dining" or "historic Savannah tours."
  • Use these keywords naturally in your website content, blog posts, and meta descriptions.
  • Don’t forget to use local landmarks or events as context—mentioning the Savannah Music Festival, the annual St. Patrick's Day Parade, or the unique charm of the Historic District can help connect your business to what locals love.

Create High-Quality Local Content

The good news is that creating content can be a fun way to engage with your community! Consider:

  • Writing blog posts about local events, history, or tips for newcomers to Savannah, including guides to local favorites like the Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil tour.
  • Creating videos that showcase your products in local spots—like a sunset at River Street or the bustling Saturday market at Forsyth Park.
  • Sharing customer testimonials that highlight their experiences with your business and the local vibe, perhaps featuring stories from visitors who came to Savannah for the historic architecture or the arts scene.

Build Local Citations and Backlinks

Getting your business listed in local directories is crucial for credibility. Here’s what you can do:

  • List your business on local sites like Savannah.com, Yelp, and TripAdvisor, especially since many tourists rely on these platforms for recommendations.
  • Partner with other local businesses or organizations for events, and ask them to link back to your site. Collaborating with the Savannah Chamber of Commerce can also enhance your visibility.
  • Attend local networking events to build relationships and spread the word about your business—face-to-face connections matter, especially in a close-knit community like Savannah!

So there you have it! By optimizing your online presence and connecting with the Savannah community, you can improve your local SEO and attract more customers.
