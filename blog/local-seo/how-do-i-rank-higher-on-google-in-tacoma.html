Local SEO Basics for Tacoma Businesses

To get started with local SEO, here are some essential steps you should take:

  • Optimize your Google My Business profile: Make sure your listing is fully filled out with your business name, address, phone number, and hours. Include high-quality photos of your Tacoma storefront or products, and consider adding images of iconic Tacoma landmarks, such as the Chihuly Bridge of Glass or Point Defiance Park.
  • Use local keywords: Incorporate keywords that are specific to Tacoma in your website content. Think about phrases like "best coffee in Hilltop" or "Tacoma landscaping services" to target potential customers searching for services in their area.
  • Get reviews: Encourage your satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp. Respond to them to show you care about your Tacoma community, and consider highlighting local influencers in your industry who may help promote your business.
  • Create local content: Write blog posts or articles about Tacoma-specific events, attractions, or issues that relate to your business. This could be anything from local festivals like the Tacoma Jazz & Blues Festival to seasonal activities at the Point Defiance Zoo & Aquarium, which can help draw in local traffic.
  • Build local backlinks: Partner with other Tacoma businesses or get listed in local directories, such as the Tacoma-Pierce County Chamber of Commerce, to improve your site's authority.

Optimize for Local Search

Once you've got the basics down, it's time to focus on local search optimization:

  • Use location pages: If you serve multiple neighborhoods in Tacoma, consider creating separate pages for each area, like North End or South Tacoma, highlighting your services tailored to those locations. You might also want to include neighborhood-specific events or attractions.
  • Leverage social media: Share local events, promotions, and community involvement on your social platforms. Tag your posts with local hashtags, such as #TacomaWA or #TacomaFoodie, to increase visibility.
  • Ensure NAP consistency: Make sure your Name, Address, and Phone number are consistent across all platforms, from your website to social media and directories. This consistency is key to gaining trust with both customers and search engines.
  • Utilize schema markup: Implement local business schema on your website to help search engines understand your business better. This can improve your chances of appearing in local searches, particularly when users are looking for services in Tacoma.

What Makes Tacoma Special

Doing business here means being part of a tight-knit community that values connection, creativity, and a sense of pride in local offerings, making it a great place for entrepreneurs.

It usually takes a few months to start seeing significant results, but consistent efforts can pay off over time. Local SEO is an ongoing process that requires patience.

Q: Do I need a website to improve my local SEO?

Q: What are local citations?

Local citations are online mentions of your business, including your name, address, and phone number, which help improve your local SEO by validating your business's presence.
