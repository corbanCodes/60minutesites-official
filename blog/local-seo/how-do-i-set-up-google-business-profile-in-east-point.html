Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile often serves as the digital storefront for your business, and it's usually the first interaction potential customers have with your brand. Here’s why it’s vital:

  • Boosts your visibility on Google Maps and Search: When locals search for services or products, a well-optimized profile ensures that your business appears prominently, especially in a city with bustling neighborhoods like East Point, known for its rich history and vibrant community.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile displays vital details such as hours of operation, location, and contact information. For example, if you're a local café near East Point's popular Greenbriar Mall, ensuring your profile is accurate can direct hungry customers right to your door.
  • Showcases your offerings: Highlight your products and services, and encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews. A solid collection of positive feedback can significantly influence new customers. Think about how a well-reviewed local mechanic can attract new clients who seek trust and reliability when searching for auto services.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can be completed in just a few steps. Here’s a detailed guide to get you started:

  • Create a Google account: If you don’t have one already, visit google.com and sign up for a free Google account. This account will be your gateway to managing your business profile.
  • Visit Google Business Profile: Navigate to google.com/business and click on ‘Manage now.’ This will take you to the setup page where you can get started.
  • Enter your business name: Type in your business name. If it appears in the search results, select it. If not, click on ‘Add your business’ to create a new listing.
  • Choose the right category: Selecting a relevant category is crucial. This categorization helps Google understand your business and match it with user searches. For example, if you run a family-owned bakery, choose categories like ‘Bakery’ or ‘Dessert Shop’ to attract the right customers.
  • Provide your location: Ensure you enter your complete business address correctly. This will help customers locate you easily, especially if you are situated near local landmarks like the East Point City Hall or close to the East Point MARTA station.
  • Add contact information: Include a phone number and website link if available. This makes it easy for customers to reach out to you directly. For instance, if you have an online ordering system, link it in your profile to streamline the process.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your physical address. Once you receive it, follow the instructions to verify your business. This step is critical for ensuring your business is legitimate and trusted.
  • Optimize your profile: After verification, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos of your business, products, and team. Regularly update your hours of operation and write a compelling business description that reflects your unique brand voice.

  • Mobile searches are prevalent: East Point residents often use their mobile devices to find nearby services. Ensure your Google Business Profile is mobile-friendly by using clear images and concise information that is easy to access on smaller screens.
