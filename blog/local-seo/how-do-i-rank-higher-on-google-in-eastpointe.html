Tips for Improving Local SEO

To enhance your local SEO efforts, consider implementing the following strategies:

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing to provide accurate information about your business.
  • Encourage customer reviews on Google and Yelp, as positive feedback boosts your local ranking.
  • Utilize local keywords in your website content, focusing on terms relevant to Eastpointe.
  • Ensure your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) are consistent across all online platforms.
  • Engage with the local community through social media and local events to increase your online presence.
