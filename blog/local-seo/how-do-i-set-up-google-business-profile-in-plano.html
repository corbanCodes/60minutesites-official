What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to standing out on Google: a complete and optimized profile. Trust me on this one, the more information you provide, the better your chances of being discovered. Here’s what you need to focus on:

  • Accurate business name: Make sure it matches your storefront.
  • Address and phone number: Keep it consistent across platforms. In Plano, including landmarks like the Shops at Legacy or the Legacy West can help.
  • Business hours: Update them regularly, especially during holidays, as Plano hosts various community events.
  • Categories: Choose relevant categories that describe your services. Consider adding specific tags related to Plano's local culture, such as 'artisanal coffee' or 'family-friendly dining.'
  • Photos: Add high-quality images of your business, products, and team. Highlighting your involvement in local events can enhance your appeal.
  • Reviews: Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews; they really do impact your ranking. In a community like Plano, testimonials from local patrons can build trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

You can claim your profile by searching for your business on Google, clicking on it, and following the prompts to verify ownership. Ensure your business is listed in Plano’s local directories for easier visibility.

Q: How often should I update my profile?

Update your profile whenever there are changes to your business hours, services, or if you have new photos or promotions to share. Keeping your profile current is essential, especially during Plano’s local festivals and events.

Q: Can I manage multiple locations?

Yes! You can manage multiple locations through one Google Business account by creating separate profiles for each location. This is particularly useful if you have branches in different Plano neighborhoods, like West Plano or East Plano.

Q: What should I do if I receive a negative review?

Respond professionally and politely. Address their concerns and offer a solution. This shows prospective customers that you care about feedback. Engaging positively with your customers can enhance your reputation in this close-knit community.
