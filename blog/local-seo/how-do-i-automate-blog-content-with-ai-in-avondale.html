  • Analytics: AI can analyze which topics are trending or resonating with your audience, allowing you to adjust your strategy in real time. Tools like BuzzSumo can help you discover what local topics are hot, enabling you to create content that reflects the interests of Avondale residents.
  • Customization: AI can create personalized content that speaks directly to the demographics of Avondale. For example, if you notice that families are a significant part of your customer base, you can use AI to generate articles focusing on family-friendly events and activities happening in the area.
