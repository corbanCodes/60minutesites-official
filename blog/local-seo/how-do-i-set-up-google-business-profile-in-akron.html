What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating a standout Google Business Profile is all about providing the right information and engaging with your audience. Here’s the secret to getting it right:

  • Complete your profile with accurate business information (name, address, phone number) that reflects your Akron location.
  • Use a consistent business name across all platforms to build brand recognition in the local market.
  • Add high-quality photos that showcase your products or services, especially featuring local landmarks or Akron-specific themes.
  • Write a compelling business description highlighting what makes you unique, such as your commitment to sustainable practices or support for local charities.
  • Encourage reviews from happy customers and respond to them, as Akron locals often rely on community feedback when making decisions.
  • Keep your business hours updated, especially during Akron's festivals like the National Hamburger Festival or the Akron Art Prize, which can attract more visitors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to set up a Google Business Profile?

It only takes about 15-30 minutes to set up your profile, but be sure to keep it updated regularly to reflect any changes in your business.

Q: Can I manage my profile from my phone?

Absolutely! You can download the Google My Business app to manage your profile on the go, making it easy to update information while attending local events or meetings.

Q: What if my business moves locations?

You can easily update your address in your Google Business Profile to keep everything accurate for your customers, ensuring they can find you in Akron’s bustling neighborhoods.

Q: How can I get more reviews?

Encourage your satisfied customers to leave reviews by asking them directly or sending a follow-up email after their visit. Consider offering incentives or highlighting reviews during local events to promote engagement.
