Understanding Local SEO in Akron

Local SEO is all about optimizing your online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches. Here’s how you can make it work for you:

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing - This is crucial for local searches, especially since Akron's population relies heavily on online reviews to make purchasing decisions.
  • Use local keywords - Include 'Akron' and nearby neighborhoods like Highland Square, known for its vibrant arts scene, or Wallhaven, with its beautiful parks, in your content.
  • Get local backlinks - Partner with other Akron businesses or local bloggers to build links. Consider reaching out to the Akron Beacon Journal for local features that can link back to your site.
  • Encourage customer reviews - Ask happy customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp, particularly after local events like the Akron Art Museum exhibitions or the Akron Zoo visits.
  • Keep your NAP consistent - That’s Name, Address, Phone number across all platforms. This is essential for maintaining credibility and improving local search rankings.

Tips for Ranking Higher in Akron Searches

Here’s the thing: ranking higher in local searches takes some effort, but it’s totally doable. Here are some specific tips for Akron:

  • Create local content - Write blog posts about local events such as the Akron Marathon or partnerships with local schools and nonprofits.
  • Optimize your website for mobile - Many customers will search for businesses on their phones, especially with events like the Akron RubberDucks games attracting a younger crowd.
  • Use local schema markup - This helps search engines understand your business better and enhances your visibility. Include details like your business type and location.
  • Engage on social media - Use platforms like Facebook and Instagram to connect with your community, sharing updates about local festivals like the Lock 3 Summer Concert Series.
  • Participate in local events - Sponsor or take part in Akron events like the annual Akron Art Walk to increase your visibility and make valuable connections.
