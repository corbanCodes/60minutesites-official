How Do I Get More Customers in Buffalo?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Buffalo, New York can be a challenge, but with the right strategies, you can stand out from the competition. The local market is ripe with opportunity, especially for small businesses that know how to leverage both online and offline tactics.

Build a Professional Website

Your website is the digital storefront of your business, so it needs to be solid. Here’s what actually works:

  • Ensure it’s mobile-friendly; many people browse on their phones.
  • Make sure it loads quickly to avoid losing impatient visitors.
  • Include clear calls to action, like ‘Contact Us’ or ‘Get a Quote’.
  • Optimize for local SEO with Buffalo-focused keywords to attract nearby customers.
  • Use high-quality images and testimonials to build trust.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Having a well-optimized Google Business Profile can drive local traffic. The truth is:

  • Claim and verify your listing; this is crucial.
  • Fill out all the details: business hours, services, and add great photos.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews.
  • Post updates and promotions regularly to engage potential customers.

Leverage Social Media Effectively

Social media is not just for big brands. Here’s how to do it right:

  • Choose platforms where your audience hangs out (Facebook, Instagram, etc.).
  • Share engaging content that reflects your brand personality.
  • Use local hashtags to increase visibility within Buffalo.
  • Run targeted ads to reach specific demographics in the area.
  • Connect with local influencers or businesses for collaborations.

Maximize Referrals and Networking

Word of mouth is powerful in Buffalo. Consider these strategies:

  • Encourage happy customers to refer friends and family.
  • Create a referral program with incentives.
  • Attend local networking events to meet other business owners.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business organizations.
  • Be active in community events; it builds visibility and relationships.

Collect and Showcase Customer Reviews

Reviews can make or break your business. Here’s how to handle them:

  • Ask for reviews after a purchase or service; timing is everything.
  • Respond to all reviews, positive or negative, to show you care.
  • Display your best reviews prominently on your website and social media.
  • Use testimonials in your marketing materials to build credibility.
