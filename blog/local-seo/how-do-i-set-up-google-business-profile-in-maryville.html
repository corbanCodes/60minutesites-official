Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Creating a Google Business Profile can put your business on the map—literally. This free tool helps you manage how your business appears in Google search results and on Google Maps. Here’s why you should care:

  • Increased visibility for locals searching for your services: When someone types in queries like 'best coffee shop in Maryville' or 'plumber near me,' having a Google Business Profile can ensure your business is among the top results.
  • Easy access to essential information: Your profile can include vital details such as your hours of operation, location, contact number, and website link. This convenience encourages potential customers to engage with your business directly.
  • Customer reviews and ratings: Reviews can significantly influence purchasing decisions. A profile with positive reviews fosters trust and encourages new customers to choose your services over competitors.
  • Insights on customer engagement: Google provides analytics that show how people find and interact with your profile, helping you understand what works and what doesn’t.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile site and click on 'Manage now.' This is your starting point for creating or claiming your business.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure to input your correct business name and physical address. If you operate from home or don’t serve customers at a physical location, you can choose to hide your address.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best fits your business. This is critical as it helps Google show your profile to relevant searches. For example, if you run a bakery, select 'Bakery' or 'Pastry Shop' for optimal visibility.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL: Providing direct contact information allows potential customers to reach you easily. If you don’t have a website yet, 60 Minute Sites can help you create a professional one quickly.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business through a method they provide, typically via mail (a postcard) or phone. This step is essential to confirm that you are the rightful owner of the business.
  • Complete your profile: Add appealing photos of your products or services, your business hours, and a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. For example, if you own a local bakery, mention your signature pastries or any special recipes that set you apart.

Understanding how locals search can help you tailor your profile for better results. Here’s what you should know:

  • Search phrases: Maryville residents often look for services using phrases like 'best pizza near me' or 'auto repair in Maryville.' Be sure to include these keywords in your business description to improve your chances of being found.
  • Local landmarks: Mentioning nearby landmarks, like Maryville College or the historic downtown area, can help catch the attention of locals. For example, saying you’re located just a block away from the college can attract students and faculty looking for convenience.

  • Encouraging reviews: Positive reviews play a significant role in decision-making. Actively encourage satisfied customers to leave feedback after their purchase. Consider implementing a follow-up email or a thank-you note that invites them to share their experience online.
