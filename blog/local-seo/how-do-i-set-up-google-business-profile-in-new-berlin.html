Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward, and it's essential for growing your visibility. Follow these detailed steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Head to the Google Business Profile site and click on 'Start Now.' This will guide you through the initial setup.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure to choose the correct address for New Berlin. This is crucial as it determines how and where your business will appear in local searches.
  • Select your business category: This helps Google understand what you offer, so choose the most relevant category. For example, if you own a bakery, select 'Bakery' over a more general category like 'Restaurant.'
  • Add your business phone number and website: Ensure your website is mobile-friendly, as many local customers will be searching on their phones. If you don't have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one that captures your brand essence.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your address, which can take a few days. Alternatively, you may have options for instant verification if you already have a Google Search Console account.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, fill out your profile completely. Add high-quality photos of your products or services, your business hours, and a compelling description that reflects your unique offerings. Don't forget to include keywords that might resonate with local customers, such as 'freshly baked goods' or 'eco-friendly cleaning services.'

How Locals in New Berlin Search for Businesses

Understanding how your neighbors search for businesses can help you tailor your Google Business Profile. Here are some insights to keep in mind:

  • Local search phrases: Residents often search for services using phrases like 'best coffee shop near me' or 'plumbers in New Berlin.' Think about the specific terms your potential customers might use and incorporate those into your profile.
  • Importance of reviews: Many locals rely on reviews and ratings to guide their choices, so encourage satisfied customers to leave feedback. You could even offer a small discount on their next purchase as a thank-you for taking the time to review your business.
  • Seasonal updates: Seasonal events and community gatherings can influence searches, so keep your profile updated with promotions or events throughout the year. For example, if you're participating in the New Berlin Farmers Market, highlight that event on your profile.
  • Utilize local landmarks: Mentioning proximity to local landmarks or popular spots, such as the New Berlin Hills Golf Course or the New Berlin Recreation Department, can attract customers visiting that area. This not only enhances your visibility but also creates a connection with the community.
  • Engage with the community: Regularly posting updates, offers, or events through Google Posts can significantly boost your visibility. Share stories about your business's involvement in local events, whether it's sponsoring a youth sports team or contributing to local charities.
