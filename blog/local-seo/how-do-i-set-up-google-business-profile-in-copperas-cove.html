Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can reap significant benefits for your business. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by heading to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now'. This will take you to the setup page where you can begin creating your profile.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure to use your exact business name as it appears in real life. This consistency helps with brand recognition. For example, if your business is called “Copperas Cove Coffee Co.,” don’t abbreviate it.
  • Choose the right business category: Selecting the most accurate category is crucial as it helps Google understand what you offer. For instance, if you run a bakery, choose 'Bakery' instead of 'Restaurant' to attract the right audience.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: Providing these details makes it easy for potential customers to contact you. If you don’t have a website yet, consider creating a simple one with 60 Minute Sites to enhance your online presence.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business through a method they provide—this can be by mail, phone, or email. Verification ensures that you are the legitimate owner of the business, which is essential for gaining customer trust.

Once your profile is live, keep it updated with your business hours, special offers, and high-quality photos. For example, if you have a seasonal menu or a weekend special, sharing that information can entice more customers to visit.
