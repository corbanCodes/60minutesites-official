Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Setting up a Google Business Profile (GBP) is essential for anyone looking to attract more customers. Here’s why:

  • Boosts your visibility on Google Search and Maps: When potential customers search for services or products in Jonesboro, they often turn to Google. A well-optimized GBP ensures your business shows up in relevant search results and on Google Maps, drawing in more foot traffic.
  • Helps potential customers find your hours, location, and contact information quickly: Customers appreciate convenience. With your GBP, they can easily find essential details like your operating hours, exact location, and contact information, making it easier for them to choose your business over competitors.
  • Allows you to showcase photos, respond to reviews, and post updates about your business: Visuals matter. By adding enticing images of your products or services, you can attract customers’ attention. Plus, engaging with reviews shows you care about customer feedback and enhances your reputation.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your GBP is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage Now': Start your journey by heading to the official website where you’ll manage your profile.
  • Enter your business name; if it doesn’t appear, select 'Add your business': Ensure your business name is accurate. If you’re a new business in Jonesboro, don’t worry; you can easily add it here.
  • Fill in your information, including address, phone number, and categories: Be thorough and precise. Choose categories that best describe your business, as this helps Google match you with relevant searches.
  • Verify your business: This can usually be done via a postcard sent to your business address. Verification is crucial as it establishes your credibility with Google.
  • Add photos and complete your profile with a detailed description: Use high-quality images that showcase your business's atmosphere and offerings. Your description should highlight what makes your business unique to Jonesboro – perhaps your family recipes or locally-sourced ingredients.
  • Keep your information updated: Especially your hours during holidays or special events. If you have a special event at a local landmark like the Jonesboro Art Museum, update your hours accordingly to accommodate customers.

How Locals Search for Businesses in Jonesboro

Understanding how people in Jonesboro look for businesses can give you an edge. Many locals rely on Google to find nearby services. Here are some common search behaviors:

  • Searching for specific business types: For instance, someone might search for 'best barbecue in Jonesboro'. Make sure your GBP reflects what you offer to capture these searches.
  • Looking for services with modifiers: Queries like 'open now' or 'near me' are common. Having accurate hours listed can help you attract customers actively seeking immediate service.
  • Checking reviews and ratings: Many locals turn to reviews to find trusted options. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback, as this can significantly impact your reputation and attract new clientele.
  • Utilizing local landmarks as reference points: People often search for restaurants or services near familiar locations, such as 'restaurants near Jonesboro Mall'. Highlight your proximity to such landmarks in your profile to increase your visibility.
