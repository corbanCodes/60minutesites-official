How Do I Get More Customers in Shoreview?

5 min read

If you’re trying to make your mark in Shoreview, Minnesota, you might be feeling the challenge of attracting more customers. The good news is that there are numerous practical strategies you can implement, both online and offline, to grow your business and connect with the community. Shoreview, known for its beautiful parks and recreational areas such as the Shoreview Community Center and the scenic Snail Lake, provides a unique backdrop for your business endeavors, making it essential to leverage these local attributes in your marketing efforts.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Building a strong online presence is essential for small businesses in Shoreview. Here are some effective tactics to consider:

  • Optimize your website for local search: Incorporate keywords like 'Shoreview', 'Minnesota', and specific services you offer in your website content. Use tools like Google Keyword Planner to identify popular search terms in your area. Also, ensure that your website is mobile-friendly, as many users will be searching on their phones.
  • Create and maintain active social media profiles: Focus on platforms like Facebook and Instagram, which are popular among local residents. Share behind-the-scenes content, customer testimonials, and engage in conversations to foster a sense of community. Consider using local hashtags to increase your visibility.
  • Utilize Google My Business: This free tool allows you to manage how your business appears in Google Search and Maps. Make sure to fill out all relevant information, including hours of operation, address, phone number, and photos of your business. A well-maintained listing can significantly enhance your visibility.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews online: Positive feedback can significantly influence potential customers. Create a simple follow-up email template that thanks customers for their business and kindly requests a review on platforms like Google or Yelp. Consider offering a small incentive for leaving a review, like a discount on their next purchase.
  • Run targeted ads on social media: Utilize Facebook Ads to reach specific demographics in the Shoreview area. You can target users by location, age, interests, and more. This can be particularly effective for promoting special events or sales you may be hosting.

Offline Strategies to Connect with the Community

Don't underestimate the power of face-to-face interactions. Here are some offline strategies to help you connect with customers:

  • Participate in local events: Engage with the community by showcasing your products at events like the Shoreview Farmers Market or the Shoreview Community Festival. These venues not only allow you to display your offerings but also help you make personal connections with potential customers.
  • Develop partnerships with other local businesses: Cross-promotion can be a powerful tool. For instance, collaborate with a nearby café to offer special discounts for customers who show a receipt from your business, and vice versa. This strategy not only drives customers to both establishments but also fosters community spirit.
  • Host workshops or classes: Use your expertise to attract new customers by hosting workshops or classes at your business location. For example, if you own a craft store, consider offering DIY workshops. This not only adds value but also creates a space for community engagement.
  • Utilize flyers and local bulletin boards: Create eye-catching flyers to advertise special offerings or events and post them in high-traffic areas such as the Shoreview Community Center or local coffee shops. Make sure to include QR codes that link directly to your website or social media pages for easy access.
  • Offer referral incentives: Encourage your current customers to bring in their friends and family by offering referral bonuses. For example, for every new customer they refer, both the referrer and the new customer could receive a discount or a free item. This fosters loyalty and encourages word-of-mouth marketing.

The Shoreview Business Community

The business community in Shoreview is vibrant and supportive, consisting of various local shops, restaurants, and services. Engaging with this community can lead to valuable partnerships and shared marketing opportunities. Consider joining local organizations, such as the Shoreview Chamber of Commerce, to network with other business owners and stay informed about community events. Networking events often provide opportunities to participate in local trade shows or community celebrations, allowing you to showcase your business to a broader audience. By actively participating in this network, you can not only promote your business but also contribute to the overall growth and prosperity of Shoreview. Building relationships within the community can lead to collaborative marketing efforts, which can be particularly effective in attracting local customers.
