How Do I Get More Customers in West Valley City?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you're running a small business in West Valley City, Utah, you know how crucial it is to attract more customers. With a vibrant community and a growing economy, there are plenty of opportunities to get noticed and stand out from the competition. West Valley City is not only the second-largest city in Utah but also offers a diverse demographic and a supportive atmosphere for local entrepreneurs.

Master Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. This is your digital storefront, and it needs to shine. Here's what actually works:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly and loads quickly, as many local consumers search on their phones.
  • Optimize for local SEO so you show up in search results when potential customers look for services in West Valley City. Utilize local keywords that reflect the area's unique offerings.
  • Use clear calls-to-action (CTAs) to encourage visitors to contact you or make a purchase. Phrases like 'Visit Us Today!' or 'Call Now for Special Offers!' can be effective.
  • Include high-quality images and testimonials to build trust. Consider showcasing local landmarks or community events in your visuals.
  • Don't forget to keep your content fresh and relevant! Blog about local events or topics that resonate with your audience.

Leverage Google My Business

Setting up a Google My Business profile is non-negotiable. It helps you appear in local searches and on Google Maps, which is essential in a city with a population of over 135,000. Here’s how to make the most of it:

  • Fill out every section completely, including hours, services, and a detailed description that incorporates local flavor, mentioning landmarks or community ties.
  • Add photos that showcase your work or products, ideally including images of your business with recognizable local backdrops.
  • Regularly update your profile with posts about promotions or events, especially those related to local festivals like the West Valley City Arts Festival.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, as these can significantly influence new customers. Highlighting your good standing in the community can be a major draw.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Word-of-mouth is still one of the strongest tools for small businesses. Here’s how to boost it:

  • Ask happy customers for reviews on Google or Yelp, focusing on how your services or products have positively impacted them.
  • Offer incentives for referrals, like discounts or freebies for customers who bring in new business. Consider partnering with other local businesses to offer joint referral incentives.
  • Create a referral program that rewards both the referrer and the new customer to encourage sharing. Make sure to promote this program through your website and social media.

Engage on Social Media

Social media can be a game-changer for connecting with your local audience. Consider these tips:

  • Choose platforms where your audience hangs out; for many local businesses in West Valley City, Facebook and Instagram are key due to their popularity among residents.
  • Share engaging content like behind-the-scenes footage, promotions, or customer stories. Highlight local events or initiatives you’re involved in to strengthen community ties.
  • Run targeted ads that reach people in West Valley City to increase visibility, especially during high-traffic shopping seasons or local events.
  • Interact with your followers by responding to comments and messages promptly, fostering a community feel.

Network with the Community

Building relationships in the local community can lead to more customers. Here’s how:

  • Attend local business networking events to meet other entrepreneurs, such as those hosted by the West Valley City Chamber of Commerce.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business groups, which often provide resources and networking opportunities for small businesses.
  • Participate in community events or sponsor local sports teams to increase your visibility. Events like the West Valley City International Days can be excellent opportunities to engage with potential customers.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for cross-promotions, as this can widen your reach and introduce you to new customer bases.
