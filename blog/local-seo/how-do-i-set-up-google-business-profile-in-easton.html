Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Having a Google Business Profile is essential for your Easton business. It helps you appear in local search results, making it easier for customers to find you. Here are some key benefits:

  • Increases visibility in local searches: When someone searches for a service like 'plumbers near me' or 'best pizza in Easton,' a well-optimized Google Business Profile can ensure your business appears at the top of the results.
  • Allows you to manage your business information: You can update your address, hours of operation, and even special holiday hours to keep your customers informed, which is crucial for local businesses that rely on foot traffic.
  • Enables customer reviews: Positive reviews build trust and credibility. When potential customers see that others have had great experiences, they are more likely to choose your business over competitors.
  • Showcases your business photos: Visuals are powerful. By adding high-quality photos of your products, services, or even your storefront on Northampton Street, you can make your business more appealing to potential customers.
  • Provides insights: Google offers analytics that show how customers find your business, whether through searches, Google Maps, or direct links. This data can help you tailor your marketing strategies effectively.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by accessing the Google Business Profile homepage to initiate the setup process.
  • Click 'Manage now': Sign in with your existing Google account. If you don’t have one, you’ll need to create a Google account.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be precise when entering your business name and address. This information should match what you use in other online listings to avoid confusion.
  • Choose the correct category: Selecting the right category is crucial as it tells Google what your business is about. For instance, if you own a bakery, choose 'Bakery' to ensure you appear in relevant searches.
  • Add your phone number and website link: This makes it easy for potential customers to reach you. If you have a website, include the URL to direct traffic there.
  • Verify your business: Google usually verifies businesses via mail or phone. This step is essential to confirm that you are the owner and helps prevent fraud.
  • Complete your profile: Enhance your profile by adding appealing photos and specifying your business hours. Consider including images of your team, your workspace, or your products, especially if you’re located near popular spots like the Easton Farmers' Market.

  • Reviews and ratings matter: Customers tend to read reviews before making decisions. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback, as this can significantly impact your business's reputation.
  • Local events and landmarks influence searches: Easton is known for its vibrant festivals and cultural events. If your business is involved in local happenings or is located near landmarks like the Crayola Experience, mention these connections in your profile.
