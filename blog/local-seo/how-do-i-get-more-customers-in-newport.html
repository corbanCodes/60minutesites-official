How Do I Get More Customers in Newport?

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Here's the thing: running a small business in Newport, Rhode Island, with its stunning coastline and historic charm, means you're not just competing with other businesses but also the allure of the scenery. To attract more customers, you need a blend of online and offline strategies that connect with the local community and visitors alike. Newport is not just a destination; it's an experience filled with rich history and vibrant culture. By leveraging this unique character, you can create meaningful connections with potential customers.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Having a strong online presence is crucial for small businesses. Here are some actionable strategies:

  • Optimize your website for local searches: Ensure your website is search engine friendly by incorporating keywords that reflect Newport attractions and your specific services. For instance, if you own a coffee shop, use terms like "Newport coffee near the Cliff Walk" or "best pastries in Newport" to attract local search traffic. Consider adding a blog that features local events or highlights nearby attractions to boost your SEO further.
  • Engage on social media platforms: Create beautiful, engaging content on Instagram and Facebook. Share stunning photos of your products, events, and the breathtaking Newport scenery. Use local hashtags, like #NewportRI or #DiscoverNewport, to broaden your reach. Additionally, consider running targeted ads during peak tourist seasons to attract visitors.
  • Leverage Google My Business: Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing. Keep your information updated—include your hours, location, and special offerings. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, as this can significantly improve your visibility on local searches and can even drive foot traffic.
  • Start an email newsletter: Create a newsletter that keeps customers informed about promotions, new offerings, and local events. Offer a sign-up incentive, like a discount on their next purchase, to grow your subscriber list. Share stories from your business journey or highlight local events that may interest your audience.
  • Encourage reviews on popular platforms: Make it easy for your customers to leave reviews on platforms like Yelp and TripAdvisor. Positive reviews from tourists can enhance your reputation and attract new customers. Consider adding a direct link to your review page in follow-up emails or on your receipts.

Offline Strategies to Draw in Locals and Visitors

While online tactics are essential, don't underestimate the power of face-to-face engagement in Newport. Here are some effective offline strategies:

  • Participate in local events and festivals: Events like the Newport Folk Festival and the Newport Jazz Festival draw thousands of visitors. Set up a booth or sponsor a local event to showcase your business. Offer samples or exclusive festival deals to attract attention and engage with attendees.
  • Network with other local businesses: Form partnerships with other local enterprises to create joint promotions or events. If you run a boutique, collaborate with a nearby cafe to offer a discount to customers who shop at both locations. This creates synergy within the community and helps draw in diverse customer bases.
  • Offer loyalty cards or incentives: Implement a loyalty program where customers earn points for repeat purchases. This not only encourages return visits but also fosters a sense of community. For example, a local bookstore could offer a free book after a certain number of purchases.
  • Host workshops or classes: Consider hosting workshops that align with your business, inviting locals to participate and learn. If you own a bakery, you could offer baking classes. This not only boosts your visibility but also positions your business as an integral part of the community.
  • Create eye-catching signage: Invest in vibrant, creative signage that reflects the Newport vibe. Use nautical themes or historical references to attract passersby. A well-placed sign can catch the eyes of both locals and tourists, leading to increased foot traffic.

Embracing the Newport Business Community

The Newport business community is vibrant and supportive, offering numerous opportunities for collaboration. Here’s how to engage:

  • Join the Newport Chamber of Commerce: By becoming a member, you'll connect with other local entrepreneurs and gain access to valuable resources, including workshops and networking events.
  • Participate in networking events: Attend local business networking events to build relationships with other business owners. Share ideas and collaborate on projects to enhance your visibility and community presence.
  • Engage with local community organizations: Understanding the needs and interests of Newport residents is crucial. Partner with local organizations to support community initiatives, which can also enhance your brand’s image.
  • Support local charities and initiatives: Consider sponsoring local events or donating a portion of your sales to local charities. This not only builds goodwill but also increases your visibility among community members.
  • Consider partnerships with local artisans: Collaborate with local artisans or creators to showcase unique products in your store. This not only attracts a diverse customer base but also strengthens community bonds.

By blending online and offline strategies while actively engaging with the Newport business community, you can effectively draw in more customers.
