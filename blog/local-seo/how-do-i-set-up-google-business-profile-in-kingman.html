Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is easier than you might think. By following these detailed steps, you can create a profile that attracts customers and makes your business shine:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by going to the Google Business Profile website and signing in with your Google account. If you don’t have an account yet, it’s a quick process to create one.
  • Manage Your Listing: Click on 'Manage Now' and enter your business name. This step allows you to check if your business is already listed. If it is, you can claim it and manage the information. If not, select 'Add your business to Google' and fill out the necessary details, including your business name, address, and contact number.
  • Choose Your Categories: Selecting the right categories is essential. Categories such as 'Restaurant,' 'Retail,' or 'Service Provider' help Google understand what your business does, making it easier for potential customers to find you. Be specific! If you're a pizza restaurant, select 'Pizza' in addition to 'Restaurant.'
  • Add Business Hours: Business hours are crucial for transparency. Clearly state your operating hours so that customers know when they can visit or call. If you have special hours for holidays or events, be sure to include those as well.
  • Include a Website Link: A link to your website is necessary for customers looking for more information. If you need assistance creating a website, consider using 60 Minute Sites, which can help you establish a professional online presence quickly.
  • Write a Compelling Description: Your business description should be concise yet informative. Highlight what makes your business unique, such as special offers or signature dishes. Make sure to include keywords that locals might use to search for your services.
  • Upload Photos: High-quality images are vital for attracting attention. Include your logo, images of your products, your storefront, and any other visuals that represent your business. Photos can help convey the atmosphere and quality of your offerings.
  • Verify Your Business: Verification ensures that your business information is accurate and secure. Google usually sends a verification postcard to the business address you provided, but you may also have the option to verify via phone or email. Follow the instructions provided to complete this step.

How Locals in Kingman Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Kingman search for businesses can significantly enhance your Google Business Profile's effectiveness. Here’s what you should keep in mind:

Consider adding landmarks nearby, such as the Route 66 Museum or the Mohave County Fairgrounds, as reference points.
  • Incorporate Keywords: Think about the terms locals might use when searching for businesses. Phrases like 'best burger in Kingman' or 'affordable auto repair near me' are commonly searched. Use these keywords in your profile description and in any posts you make to increase visibility.
  • Encourage Local Reviews: Positive reviews have a significant influence on potential customers. Encourage satisfied clients to leave reviews on your Google Business Profile. Consider offering incentives, such as discounts on future purchases, to motivate customers to share their experiences.
  • Leverage Social Media: Many Kingman residents share recommendations and experiences on social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram. Integrating your Google Business Profile with social media can help amplify your reach. Post engaging content, share customer testimonials, or highlight community involvement to foster connections with locals.
  • Engage with the Community: Participate in local events or sponsor community activities to increase your visibility. Building relationships with other local businesses can also be beneficial. Consider collaborating with nearby shops or restaurants for cross-promotions.
