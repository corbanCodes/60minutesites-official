Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be done in just a few simple steps. Here’s how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the official site to begin the setup process.
  • Sign in with your Google account: If you don’t have one, you’ll need to create a new account. This account will manage your business listing.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure you enter the exact name and address to help customers find you easily. For example, if you own a bakery on 30th Street, be specific.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best represents your business. If you run a coffee shop, select ‘Coffee Shop’ to target the right audience.
  • Add your phone number and website: This allows customers to reach you directly. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using a service like 60 Minute Sites to create a quick, professional one.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a verification code to your business address. Follow the instructions to complete the verification process.

After verification, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos, your business hours, and a captivating description that showcases what makes your business special. This is your chance to tell your unique story, whether you’re serving artisanal coffee or homemade pastries.
