How Do I Get More Customers in Houston?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Houston is a bustling hub for small businesses, known for its diversity and entrepreneurial spirit. With a population exceeding 2.3 million, it is the largest city in Texas and the fourth largest in the United States. The city's economy is robust, anchored by industries such as energy, healthcare, aerospace, and manufacturing. If you're looking to attract more customers, you're in the right place. There are countless opportunities to grow your customer base here, thanks to Houston’s dynamic market and supportive community.

Building Your Online Presence

A professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It's where potential clients will go to learn more about you. Here's what actually works:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly; many Houstonians are searching on the go, especially in a city known for its traffic.
  • Optimize your site for local SEO to rank higher in searches relevant to your services in Houston, utilizing keywords like 'Houston plumber' or 'Houston catering services.'
  • Use clear calls-to-action (CTAs) to encourage visitors to contact you or book a service, integrating local language that resonates with the Houston audience.
  • Regularly update your content with blogs or news about the Houston market to keep it fresh and engaging for both users and search engines.

Getting Found on Google

Having a solid Google Business Profile is non-negotiable in Houston's competitive landscape. Here's how to make it shine:

  • Fill out every section completely, including hours, services, and contact info, ensuring you specify if you're located in areas like The Heights, Montrose, or downtown.
  • Use high-quality images showcasing your work or products, highlighting local landmarks or the vibrant Houston skyline where relevant.
  • Regularly post updates or special offers to keep your profile active and engage with local events or holidays.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; they can greatly influence potential clients, especially in a city where word-of-mouth is vital.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Positive reviews can make or break your business in Houston. Here's how to leverage them:

  • Ask happy customers for reviews on Google and Yelp after a service, especially targeting Houston-centric keywords.
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new clients, such as discounts or special deals that resonate with the local community.
  • Share testimonials on your website and social media to build trust, and consider featuring local businesses or clients to strengthen community ties.

Engaging on Social Media

Social media is a powerful tool for connecting with the Houston community. Here's what I've seen work:

  • Choose platforms that suit your target audience; Instagram and Facebook are often effective for local service businesses.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content to humanize your brand and build a connection, perhaps highlighting local events or community stories.
  • Run targeted ads to reach specific demographics in Houston, including neighborhoods or interest groups that align with your business.
  • Join local groups or forums, such as Houston-focused Facebook groups, to engage with potential customers and offer value.

Networking and Community Engagement

Never underestimate the power of face-to-face connections in Houston. To build your local customer base:

  • Attend local business events and trade shows, such as the Houston Small Business Expo, to network with other business owners.
  • Join organizations like the Houston Chamber of Commerce to connect and collaborate with other local entrepreneurs.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions and community events, such as food festivals or charity runs that spotlight Houston’s diverse culture.

Building your customer base in Houston takes effort, but by implementing these strategies and leveraging the unique aspects of this vibrant city, you can set the stage for growth. Stay focused, engage with your community, and watch your business thrive in this exciting market!
