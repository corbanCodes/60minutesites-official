Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward and can significantly boost your visibility in Mint Hill. Here’s how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now'. This is your first step toward enhancing your online presence.
  • Enter your business name: Make sure to choose a name that matches your actual business name to avoid confusion. Google will prompt you to select the correct category that fits your services. Choose wisely, as this helps Google understand what you offer.
  • Add your location details: Provide accurate address information, and don't forget to include your service areas. Mint Hill has a friendly small-town feel, so make sure to specify nearby neighborhoods like Idlewild or Mint Hill Village. This can help you attract local customers who are searching for services in their immediate area.
  • Provide contact information: Include a phone number where potential customers can reach you easily, and add a website link. If you don't have a website yet, consider creating one with 60 Minute Sites, which specializes in helping local businesses establish a professional online presence.
  • Verify your business: Follow the verification steps provided by Google. This may involve receiving a postcard at your business address. Verification is crucial as it lends credibility to your listing.
  • Add business hours and appealing visuals: Once verified, take the time to add your business hours, ensuring they reflect your actual operating times. Include high-quality photos of your products, services, and the interior of your business to attract more customers. A welcoming image of your storefront can go a long way in making a good first impression.
  • Highlight special offers: If you have any promotions or unique offerings, make sure to feature them on your profile. This can entice local customers to choose your business over others. For example, if you’re running a seasonal discount or a community event, showcasing this information can draw more attention.
