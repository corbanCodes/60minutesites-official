How Do I Get More Customers in Salt Lake City?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Salt Lake City can be a rewarding challenge. With its vibrant community, breathtaking mountain scenery, and a growing economy bolstered by industries like technology, healthcare, and tourism, there are plenty of opportunities for small businesses to thrive if you know where to focus your efforts.

Building Your Online Presence

Your website is the foundation of getting customers online, and it needs to look professional. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a user-friendly, mobile-responsive website that highlights local attractions and culture.
  • Optimize your site for local SEO with keywords relevant to Salt Lake City, such as 'Salt Lake City services' or 'local Salt Lake City shops.'
  • Create valuable content that addresses your customers' needs, such as local guides or community news.
  • Include strong calls to action that guide visitors to contact you, making it easy for them to reach out for inquiries.
  • Don’t forget to track your analytics to understand visitor behavior and improve your strategies.

Harnessing the Power of Google

Google is often the first place potential customers look for services. To make sure they find you:

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing, ensuring it features your location near landmarks like Temple Square or Liberty Park.
  • Use high-quality images of your business and the surrounding Salt Lake City environment to attract local customers.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, which can significantly impact your visibility.
  • Regularly update your listing with posts about promotions, community events, or partnerships with local organizations.
  • Utilize Google Ads to reach a larger audience in your area, targeting specific neighborhoods or demographics in Salt Lake City.

The Importance of Reviews and Referrals

Word-of-mouth is still one of the most powerful marketing tools, especially in a tight-knit community like Salt Lake City:

  • Ask happy customers to leave reviews on multiple platforms such as Google, Yelp, and Facebook, emphasizing your local service.
  • Offer a referral program that incentivizes your current customers to bring in new ones, perhaps with discounts for local community members.
  • Share testimonials on your website and social media to build trust within the local community.
  • Join local business groups, such as the Salt Lake Chamber of Commerce, to strengthen your network and gain referrals.

Leveraging Social Media

Social media can be a game-changer for local businesses:

  • Choose the platforms where your target audience hangs out (Instagram, Facebook, etc.), focusing on local events and happenings.
  • Post engaging content regularly, including behind-the-scenes looks at your business and highlights of local culture or seasonal activities.
  • Use local hashtags to reach a community audience, such as #SaltLakeCity or #SLCbusiness.
  • Run targeted ads to promote your services to Salt Lake City residents, especially during events like the Sundance Film Festival or local farmers' markets.
  • Engage with followers by responding to comments and messages quickly, creating a local community around your business.

Networking and Community Engagement

The hustle of running a local business can be intense, but getting out there can pay off:

  • Attend local networking events, such as those hosted by the Salt Lake City Business Alliance, to connect with other business owners.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business associations, which often host events in the heart of downtown Salt Lake City.
  • Participate in community events or sponsor local activities, such as Art Strolls or the Salt Lake City Marathon, to increase visibility.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for joint promotions, especially during holiday seasons or citywide events.
  • Always be ready with your business cards and an elevator pitch to make the most of spontaneous networking opportunities.

Remember, building your customer base takes time and effort, but with the right strategies, you can stand out in Salt Lake City. Stay consistent, stay engaged, and watch your business grow amidst the stunning backdrop of the Wasatch Mountains and the supportive local community.
