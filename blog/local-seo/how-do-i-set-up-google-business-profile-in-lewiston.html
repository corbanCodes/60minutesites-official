Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is essential for attracting customers in Lewiston. This free tool allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps. Here's why it's crucial:

  • Improves visibility when locals search for services: When someone types in 'best pizza in Lewiston' or 'flower shop near me,' you want to be among the top results that pop up on their screens. A well-optimized profile increases your chances of being discovered.
  • Enables customer reviews, enhancing your reputation: Positive reviews from satisfied customers act like word-of-mouth recommendations. Encourage your patrons to leave reviews, and respond to them to show you value their feedback.
  • Provides essential information like hours of operation and contact details: Ensure your hours are up-to-date, particularly during holidays or special events. This way, customers can plan their visits without any hassle.
  • Helps potential customers discover your location easily: With Google Maps integration, customers can easily find their way to your business. This is particularly important for those unfamiliar with the area, such as tourists visiting the beautiful Bates College campus or exploring the scenic Riverwalk.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your profile set up is straightforward. Follow these steps to make sure you’re visible to potential customers:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile page: Begin by signing in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, creating an account is quick and easy.
  • Enter your business name and address: Ensure this information is accurate since locals rely on this information. If you have multiple locations, make sure to create a profile for each one.
  • Choose the right category for your business: This is crucial for aligning your business with the right audience. For example, if you own a bakery, select 'Bakery' rather than just 'Food.' This specificity helps Google match your business with relevant search queries.
  • Add your business phone number and website link: Include a direct link to your website to drive traffic. If you don't have a website yet, consider using services like 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and easily.
  • Upload high-quality photos of your business and products: Visuals matter! Showcase your space, offerings, and even your staff. Bright, engaging images can attract more clicks and visits.
  • Verify your business: Google will ask you to verify your business via mail, phone, or email, allowing them to confirm that you are the owner. This step is essential for your profile to go live and be fully accessible to users.

How Locals in Lewiston Search for Businesses

Understanding how Lewiston residents search for businesses can help you tailor your profile effectively. Local searches often include:

  • Specific terms: People often search for specific services or products like 'best coffee shop in Lewiston' or 'plumbers near me.' Use these phrases in your profile description and posts.
  • Neighborhood mentions: Searches often include local neighborhoods, such as 'businesses in the Tree Streets area.' Highlighting your location can help your business appear in these localized searches.
  • Keywords related to local events or landmarks: For instance, 'restaurants near Bates College' or 'stores near the Lewiston/Auburn Airport' can be beneficial. If your business is close to popular spots, mention them in your profile.
  • Utilizing Google Maps: Many locals use mobile devices for directions, especially when exploring downtown or looking for last-minute dining options. Make sure your location is accurately pinned on the map.

By focusing on these local search habits, you can enhance your profile with relevant keywords and attract more customers. Engaging with the community through local events or collaborations can also be a great way to boost your visibility.
