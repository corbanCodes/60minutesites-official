Why Google Business Profile Matters in Biddeford

Creating a Google Business Profile is vital for local visibility, especially in a vibrant city like Biddeford, where community and local commerce thrive. Here's why it matters:

  • Boosts online visibility: When potential customers search for local services, a well-maintained Google Business Profile can push your business to the top of search results. For instance, if someone in Biddeford types 'best breakfast near me,' your café could be just a click away.
  • Provides essential information: Customers often look for crucial details such as hours of operation, exact location, and contact information. Having this data readily available can lead to increased foot traffic as people can easily plan their visits around your hours.
  • Showcases your offerings: By uploading high-quality photos and encouraging customer reviews, you can create a compelling representation of your business. For example, a local bakery can display mouth-watering images of their pastries, enticing potential customers and making their decision easier.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be accomplished in just a few steps. Follow these detailed instructions to ensure you’re making the most of this valuable resource:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Sign in using your Google account. If you don’t have one, it’s easy to create a new account.
  • Claim or create your business listing: Enter your business name, address, and phone number accurately. This is crucial since incorrect information can lead to lost customers.
  • Select relevant categories: Choose categories that best represent your business. For example, if you own a bakery in Biddeford, you should select both 'Bakery' and 'Cafe' to capture a wider audience. You might also consider more specific categories like 'Artisan Bakery' or 'Dessert Shop' to attract niche customers.
  • Update your business hours: Regularly update your business hours, including special holiday hours, to prevent any potential confusion. For instance, if you’re closed on holidays, make sure to clearly state this on your profile.
  • Upload high-quality visuals: Pictures speak volumes. Invest time in capturing appealing images of your storefront, products, and even staff. This not only enhances your profile but also builds a welcoming image of your business.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Ask satisfied customers to leave positive reviews. Responding to reviews—whether they’re positive or negative—can build trust and demonstrate that you value customer feedback.

  • Search terms: Local search terms often include ‘near me’ or specific neighborhood names like ‘Saco Falls’ or ‘Biddeford Pool.’ Tailoring your content to include these terms can help your business appear in more relevant searches.
  • Community engagement: Residents appreciate businesses that actively engage with their community. Regularly responding to reviews and posting updates about community involvement can set your profile apart. For instance, if your business participates in the Biddeford Art Walk, make sure to highlight that on your profile.
