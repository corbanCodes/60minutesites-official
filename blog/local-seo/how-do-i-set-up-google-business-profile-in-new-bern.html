Why Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile acts as a digital storefront, helping locals and visitors alike find you when they search online. Here’s why establishing this profile is essential:

  • Increases Visibility in Local Search Results: With a well-optimized profile, your business can appear in the coveted local pack on Google, making you more visible to those searching for services in New Bern.
  • Provides Essential Information: Customers can easily find your hours of operation, location, and contact details, making it convenient for them to reach out or visit your establishment.
  • Showcases Customer Reviews and Photos: Positive reviews can build trust and credibility. Encourage your satisfied customers to leave reviews, and consider posting high-quality photos of your products or services to attract more interest.
  • Enables You to Engage with Customers: Use your profile to post updates, special promotions, or events happening in your business. This not only keeps your audience informed but also helps you build a loyal customer base.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be done in just a few minutes. Follow these actionable steps to ensure you’re maximizing your business’s online presence in New Bern:

  • Visit the Google My Business Website: Go to the Google My Business website and click on the “Manage now” button. This will take you to the setup page.
  • Enter Your Business Name and Address: If your business doesn’t appear in the suggestions, select “Add your business.” Be sure to enter the correct address to help customers find you easily.
  • Select the Right Category: Choose a category that best fits your business. This helps Google display your profile to the right audience. For example, if you own a café, select categories like “Coffee Shop” or “Cafe.”
  • Add Your Phone Number and Website URL: If you have a business website, include it. This provides customers with additional information and encourages them to explore more.
  • Verify Your Business: Verification can be done via mail, phone, or email. Google will send you a verification code that you’ll need to enter to confirm your business location.
  • Complete Your Profile: Once verified, take the time to fill out your profile with engaging photos, accurate business hours, and a captivating description of your services. A well-rounded profile attracts more customers.

How New Bern Locals Search for Businesses

Understanding the search habits of locals in New Bern can help you tailor your profile for maximum impact. Here are some common behaviors to keep in mind:

  • Google Maps Usage: Many locals use Google Maps to find nearby restaurants, shops, and services. Ensure your location is accurately marked to help them find you easily.
  • Neighborhood-Centric Searches: Residents often search for businesses by specific neighborhoods, such as Downtown New Bern, Trent Woods, or the historic district. Incorporating these terms into your profile can enhance your visibility.
  • Recommendations Matter: Locals frequently seek recommendations from friends and family, so having positive reviews on your profile can significantly impact your business. Consider creating a strategy to encourage satisfied customers to leave glowing reviews.
  • Seasonal Searches: Keep an eye on local events and holidays, as searches may spike during these times. Update your profile with event-specific promotions or details to attract more customers during peak times.
