How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Tempe?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Hey there, Tempe business owner! If you're looking to boost your local SEO game and get more foot traffic through your doors, you’re in the right place. Tempe is a vibrant city known for its dynamic blend of college life, outdoor activities, and cultural events. With Arizona State University (ASU) influencing the local economy and lifestyle, and the picturesque Tempe Town Lake serving as a hub for recreation, it’s essential to make your business shine in this lively environment.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Here's the thing: your Google Business Profile (GBP) is your best friend when it comes to local SEO. Make sure you:

  • Claim and verify your GBP listing.
  • Fill out every section completely, including business hours, phone number, and website link.
  • Use high-quality photos that showcase your business and products, especially in vibrant locations like Mill Avenue or near the ASU campus.
  • Regularly post updates, offers, or events to keep your profile fresh.

Utilize Local Keywords

Look, I get it, figuring out the right keywords can feel overwhelming. But focusing on local keywords is key. Consider:

  • Using phrases like “best coffee in Tempe” or “Tempe family dentist” in your website content.
  • Including neighborhood names, like “downtown Tempe,” “near ASU,” or even specific landmarks such as “Tempe Marketplace” or “Tempe Diablo Stadium,” to attract local searches.
  • Implementing these keywords naturally in your blog posts, service pages, and meta descriptions.

Encourage Customer Reviews

The good news is that customer reviews can significantly boost your local SEO. To get more reviews:

  • Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp.
  • Respond to reviews, both positive and negative, to show that you value customer feedback and are engaged with the community.
  • Consider offering incentives for reviews, like discounts on their next purchase or entry into a local giveaway.

Create Local Content

Creating content that resonates with the Tempe community can set you apart. Try:

  • Writing blog posts about local events, like the Tempe Festival of the Arts, the Ironman Arizona race, or the annual Oktoberfest celebration.
  • Featuring local partnerships or collaborations with ASU student organizations or local artists on your website.
  • Sharing stories or testimonials from local customers to build a sense of community and connection.

So there you have it! By optimizing your Google Business Profile, using local keywords, gathering customer reviews, and creating relevant content, you can improve your local SEO and attract more customers in Tempe. Take these tips to heart and watch your business flourish in our amazing community filled with opportunities and vibrant culture!
