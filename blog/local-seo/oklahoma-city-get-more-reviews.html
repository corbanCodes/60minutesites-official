How to Get More Google Reviews in Oklahoma City

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

I understand the struggle – encouraging customers to leave Google reviews can feel like an uphill battle. However, Oklahoma City, with its eclectic neighborhoods like Plaza District and historic landmarks such as the Myriad Botanical Gardens, offers a supportive community eager to share their experiences. With the right strategies, you can transform your approach to gathering reviews and, in turn, draw more local customers to your business. Let’s explore practical steps to enhance your Google reviews!

Create a Google My Business Profile

The first step in your review-gathering journey is to create or claim your Google My Business (GMB) profile. This is not just a listing; it's your digital storefront on Google, and it plays a crucial role in collecting reviews. To maximize its potential:

  • Complete All Sections: Ensure you fill out every section, including your business hours, location, services, and even your contact information. The more complete your profile, the better your visibility.
  • Add High-Quality Images: Visuals matter! Upload high-resolution images of your storefront, team members, and products. For instance, if you're a restaurant in the Plaza District, showcase your signature dishes to entice potential diners.
  • Regular Updates: Keep your profile fresh by updating it with any changes such as seasonal hours, special promotions, or new menu items. This dynamic approach keeps customers informed and engaged.

Ask for Reviews at the Right Time

When you ask for reviews can make all the difference. Timing your requests appropriately will help capture that moment of satisfaction. Here are some strategies to consider:

  • Right After Service: Request reviews immediately after a successful service or purchase. For example, if you're a local hair salon, ask your clients for feedback right after they see their new haircut in the mirror.
  • Follow-Up Emails: Send a personalized follow-up email thanking them for their business. Include a direct link to your review page and a gentle nudge to share their experience. A simple message can go a long way!
  • Receipts and Checkout Reminders: Include a reminder on your receipts or during the checkout process. A friendly note that says, 'We’d love your feedback! Please leave us a review,' can encourage customers to take action.

Make It Easy for Customers to Leave Reviews

Simplicity is key when it comes to gathering Google reviews. The easier you make it for customers to leave feedback, the more likely they are to do it. Here’s how to simplify the process:

  • Direct Links: Provide direct links to your Google review page in your emails, text messages, or on your website. Make sure these links are easily accessible and visible.
  • QR Codes: Consider creating a QR code that links directly to your review page. Place this code in your physical location, such as on the checkout counter or table tents, making it easy for customers to scan and leave a review.
  • Guide the Process: If you notice customers hesitating, offer to guide them through the review process. A quick walkthrough can make a big difference, especially for those who may not be tech-savvy.

Engage with Existing Reviews

Your existing reviews are a goldmine for building trust and credibility. Engaging with these reviews shows that you value customer feedback. Here’s how to effectively engage:

  • Respond Promptly: Make it a habit to respond to every review, whether positive or negative. Thank customers for their kind words and address any concerns thoughtfully. For instance, if a customer mentions a delay in service, acknowledge it and explain how you're improving.
  • Highlight Positive Feedback: Share glowing reviews on your social media platforms. This not only showcases your satisfied customers but also encourages others to leave their own reviews.
  • Use Reviews as Learning Tools: Take constructive criticism seriously. If multiple customers point out the same issue, consider it an opportunity to improve your services.

Host Events or Promotions

Creating buzz around your business can encourage more customers to leave reviews. Hosting events or special promotions is a fantastic way to do this. Here are some ideas:

  • Incentives for Reviews: Offer a discount or small gift for customers who take the time to leave a review. Just be sure to follow Google's guidelines and encourage honest feedback.
  • Community Events: Organize a community event, such as a local art showcase in the Plaza District or a charity fundraiser. Encourage attendees to share their experiences online, creating a positive ripple effect.
  • Cross-Promotional Partnerships: Team up with other local businesses in popular areas like Bricktown or Midtown for cross-promotional events. This not only increases your visibility but also fosters a sense of community among local businesses.

Implementing these strategies can significantly elevate your Google reviews in Oklahoma City. Remember, each review is an opportunity to connect with your customers and showcase your business's strengths.
