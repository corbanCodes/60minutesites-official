How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Spokane?

9 min read

If you're a small business owner in Spokane, Washington, you know how important it is to stand out in our vibrant community. With great local spots like Riverfront Park and the bustling Spokane Valley, competition can be tough, but I’m here to help you sharpen your local SEO game. Spokane is not only the largest city in Eastern Washington, but it also boasts a rich history, diverse culture, and an active arts scene that makes it a unique place for business.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

One of the first steps for local SEO is claiming and optimizing your Google Business Profile. This is crucial for Spokane businesses looking to show up in local searches. Here are some practical tips:

  • Make sure your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) are consistent across all platforms. Spokane addresses can sometimes be tricky due to shared street names, so be precise.
  • Use relevant keywords in your business description and choose the right categories; for instance, if you run a restaurant, include terms like 'local cuisine' or 'farm-to-table.'
  • Add high-quality photos of your business, products, and services. Showcase the unique architecture of your Spokane location, such as historic buildings downtown.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews and respond to them promptly, especially on platforms popular in Spokane, like Yelp and TripAdvisor.

Leverage Local Keywords

When it comes to local SEO, using the right keywords is key. Here’s how you can do it:

  • Include terms like 'best coffee shop in Spokane' or 'Spokane family dentist' in your website content to target local searches effectively.
  • Use neighborhood names, such as Kendall Yards, South Hill, or the University District, to resonate with specific local audiences.
  • Create blog content that discusses local events, like the Spokane International Film Festival or the Spokane County Interstate Fair, to attract more visitors and engage your community.

Build Local Links

Link-building can be a game changer for your local SEO. Here’s how to earn those valuable links:

  • Partner with other local businesses for joint promotions or events, such as the Spokane Arts Fund or local food festivals, and link to each other’s sites.
  • Get involved with local charities and organizations, like the Spokane Humane Society, as they often link back to sponsors.
  • Write guest posts for local blogs or news sites, such as The Spokesman-Review, to showcase your expertise and gain backlinks. This connects your business with the community and builds credibility.

Utilize Local Directories

Don’t underestimate the power of local directories. Here’s how to make the most of them:

  • List your business in Spokane-specific directories, like Spokane's local Chamber of Commerce or Visit Spokane, which is focused on tourism and local attractions.
  • Ensure your NAP is consistent across all directory listings, especially since Spokane has a variety of local guides and tourism sites.
  • Consider niche directories that relate to your industry, such as those focusing on health and wellness or arts and entertainment, as they can help you reach your target audience more effectively.

The bottom line is, if you want to thrive in Spokane’s local market, investing time and effort into your local SEO is a must. Start implementing these tips today, and watch your business grow in our beautiful city. With Spokane's dynamic community and supportive atmosphere, the opportunities for local businesses are endless. Let's get to work!
