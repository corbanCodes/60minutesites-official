Offline Strategies to Draw More Customers

While online presence is vital, offline strategies still play a crucial role in attracting foot traffic to your business:

  • Distribute flyers or brochures: Place your promotional materials in high-traffic areas, such as grocery stores, community centers, and libraries. Ensure your design is eye-catching and includes a special offer to entice readers to visit.
