Offline Strategies to Build Your Customer Base

For example, collaborate on promotions or events, cross-promote each other's services, or even create bundled offerings that encourage customers to explore multiple local businesses.

Additionally, a loyalty program can encourage repeat visits. For example, reward customers with a free item after a certain number of purchases to foster long-term relationships.
  • Utilize local signage and flyers: Make sure your business stands out in North Platte’s neighborhoods by utilizing eye-catching signage and distributing flyers. Consider placing flyers in popular local spots, like coffee shops or community centers, where potential customers are likely to frequent.
