What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to standing out on Google: a complete and optimized profile. Focus on these key elements:

  • Business name: Use your official business name for consistency. This helps in building brand recognition in Fresno's competitive market.
  • Address: Make sure your physical location is accurate. This helps locals find you easily, especially in neighborhoods like Tower District or Woodward Park.
  • Phone number: List a number where customers can reach you directly, as many Fresno residents prefer calling local businesses for inquiries.
  • Business hours: Keep them updated so customers know when to visit or call. Consider local events and festivals that might affect your hours.
  • Photos: Upload high-quality images of your products, team, and store to make your profile inviting. Highlight any unique aspects of your business, like outdoor seating or local art.
  • Description: Write a clear and engaging description about what you offer and why you're unique, especially emphasizing local ingredients or community involvement.
  • Categories: Choose relevant categories to help Google understand what your business does, like 'Italian Restaurant' or 'Local Coffee Shop'.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set up my Google Business Profile?

Simply go to Google Business Profile, click on 'Start Now', and follow the prompts to enter your business details. Make sure to verify your location, especially if you're in a popular area like Fig Garden.

Q: Can I update my profile later?

Absolutely! You can update your information anytime, so keep it current to reflect any changes, such as new menu items or holiday hours.

Q: Is it free to use Google Business Profile?

Yes! It's completely free, so take advantage of this powerful tool for your business. Many Fresno businesses have successfully increased foot traffic this way.

Q: How long does it take for my profile to show up on Google?

It usually takes a few days for your profile to be verified and appear in search results, so be patient! Check back regularly to ensure everything is set up correctly.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical location?

You can still create a profile by selecting the service area option, so people can find you based on the services you offer. This is especially useful for delivery services or mobile businesses.
