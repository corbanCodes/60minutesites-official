Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Just follow these steps to ensure your business is easily discoverable:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by going to the Google Business Profile page and sign in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, creating an account is simple and free.
  • Manage Your Business: Click on 'Manage now' and enter your business name to check if it’s already listed. If it is, you can claim it. If not, you’ll create a new profile.
  • Enter Your Business Details: Fill in your business name, address, and service area. Be specific; for example, if you run a bakery on Kemp Boulevard, make sure to mention this to attract local customers.
  • Choose Your Business Category: Selecting the right category is crucial as it helps Google understand what you offer. If you’re a coffee shop, for instance, choose 'Coffee Shop' or 'Café' to appear in relevant searches.
  • Add Your Business Hours: Clearly state your hours of operation. If you’re open late for the nightlife crowd in Wichita Falls, make sure to highlight those hours.
  • Provide Contact Information: Include your phone number and a link to your website. This makes it easy for potential customers to reach out or learn more about your offerings.
  • Verify Your Business: Google will require you to verify your business via postcard, phone, or email—choose the method that’s easiest and most convenient for you.
  • Optimize Your Profile: Once verified, take the time to enhance your profile. Add high-quality photos of your products or services, respond to reviews promptly, and post updates regularly to keep your customers informed and engaged.
