Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile acts like a welcoming front door to your business, offering a glimpse into what you provide. It’s completely free, yet it unlocks a treasure trove of opportunities. Here’s why you should prioritize creating one:

  • Visibility on Google Search and Maps: When potential customers search for services in Scranton, your business appears right at the top of search results and on Google Maps, making it easy for them to find you.
  • Control Over Your Business Information: You have the power to manage your business details, ensuring that your address, hours, and contact information are always current and accurate, which is crucial for building trust.
  • Insights into Customer Interactions: Gain valuable insights about how customers find your listing, the terms they use, and how they interact with your profile, helping you refine your marketing strategies.
  • Engagement Through Posts and Offers: You can keep your audience informed by posting updates about promotions, events, or new products, and you can directly respond to customer reviews, which fosters community engagement and loyalty.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Starting your Google Business Profile is a simple process that can be completed in just a few steps. Here’s how you can get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile Website: Head over to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Start Now' to begin the setup process.
  • Sign In or Create an Account: Use your existing Google account to sign in or create a new one if you don’t already have one.
  • Enter Your Business Name and Address: Be meticulous when entering your business name and address. Ensure it matches your physical location in Scranton, such as a well-known area like Peckville or the vibrant downtown.
  • Choose the Right Business Category: Selecting the appropriate category is crucial for appearing in relevant searches. For example, if you run a cozy café, choose categories like 'Café' or 'Coffee Shop' to attract local coffee lovers.
  • Add Your Phone Number and Website URL: Providing a contact number and website URL allows potential customers to reach you easily. If you don't have a website, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create a professional-looking site that complements your Google listing.
  • Verify Your Business: Verification is a vital step that ensures your business information is authentic. Google typically offers verification methods through mail or phone verification.
  • Enhance Your Profile: Once verified, take the time to enrich your profile with high-quality photos of your products or services, clearly stated business hours, and a captivating description that tells your brand story.

How Locals in Scranton Search for Businesses

Understanding the local search behavior can give your business a competitive edge. In Scranton, residents often utilize Google to find:

  • Restaurants with Local Flavor: Many people turn to online searches for dining options, especially those near landmarks like the Lackawanna County Courthouse, where they seek out popular eateries that reflect the local culture.
  • Service Providers with a Strong Reputation: Locals frequently search for trusted service providers, particularly in neighborhoods like Green Ridge, where community recommendations play a significant role in decision-making.
  • Unique Retail Shops: Shoppers look for retail stores that offer distinctive products. Word of mouth and customer reviews are powerful tools in guiding them to local favorites.
  • Local Events and Activities: Especially around popular spots like Steamtown National Historic Site, locals search for current events and activities to engage with their community.

Incorporating keywords related to these searches into your profile can significantly increase your chances of being discovered by potential customers, so think about what terms your ideal customers might use.
