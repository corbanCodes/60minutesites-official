How Do I Get More Customers in Joliet?

9 min read

Getting customers in Joliet, Illinois, is all about making genuine connections and leveraging both online and offline strategies. As a small business owner here, you have a fantastic opportunity to tap into a supportive community eager to support local services. Joliet, known for its rich history and vibrant culture, offers a unique backdrop for businesses to flourish, from its historic downtown area to the bustling commercial districts.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront and the first impression many potential customers will have of your business. Here's what actually works:

  • Invest in a clean, user-friendly design that reflects the charm of Joliet's historic sites like the Rialto Square Theatre.
  • Ensure it's mobile-responsive; many people search on their phones, especially during events like the Joliet Slammers games.
  • Include clear calls to action (CTAs) like 'Book Now' or 'Contact Us' that invite visitors to engage.
  • Optimize for local SEO with keywords like 'Joliet landscaping' or 'Joliet plumbing' to attract local searches.
  • Keep your content fresh and relevant, maybe even start a blog to engage visitors with local news and events.

Leverage Google My Business

I’ve seen this work time and time again. A well-optimized Google My Business listing can dramatically increase your visibility. Here’s how:

  • Claim your listing and fill in all the info – hours, services, photos of your business that showcase its connection to Joliet's community.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews, as they can boost your ranking and attract customers looking for services in Joliet.
  • Post updates or offers to keep your audience engaged and informed about local events or promotions.
  • Use Google Posts to share news, events, or promotions, especially during Joliet festivals or community gatherings that can make your business stand out.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

The hustle of running a local business is tough, but word-of-mouth can work wonders.

  • Ask satisfied customers for reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook; emphasize how their feedback supports local business growth in Joliet.
  • Offer incentives for referrals; for instance, a discount for both the referrer and the new customer, especially for services popular in the Joliet area.
  • Share testimonials on your website and social media to build trust with potential clients, highlighting your involvement in Joliet's community events.

Engage on Social Media

Social media is a powerful tool for connecting with your community. Here’s how to effectively use it:

  • Identify which platforms your target audience uses (Facebook, Instagram, etc.); for Joliet, Facebook groups focused on local events can be particularly effective.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content to humanize your business, such as preparing for local fairs or festivals.
  • Run local ads targeting Joliet residents to increase brand awareness, especially during events like the Joliet City Center Partnership meetings.
  • Collaborate with local influencers who can introduce your services to their followers, leveraging Joliet’s local pride.

Network Locally

Building relationships in Joliet can lead to business growth. Here’s what you can do:

  • Attend local business networking events and fairs, especially those organized by the Joliet Chamber of Commerce, to meet others in your industry.
  • Join the Joliet Chamber of Commerce for resources and connections, enhancing your visibility among local businesses.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions or community events, like the annual Joliet Block Party.
  • Don’t underestimate the power of face-to-face interaction. Build rapport with other local business owners and share leads during local meetups.

Getting more customers in Joliet is about building relationships, both online and offline. Embrace the hustle, stay consistent, and remember that your dedication to your community will pay off. Keep pushing forward and watch your business thrive!
