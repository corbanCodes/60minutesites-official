What is a Google Business Profile?

A Google Business Profile is a free listing that allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps. It acts like a digital storefront, helping potential customers find you and learn more about your offerings. Here's what you can do with it:

  • Showcase your business hours and location: Clearly list your operating hours and ensure your address is accurate so customers can easily find you.
  • Post updates about offers or events: Share limited-time promotions or upcoming events—perhaps a local music night at your bar or a seasonal sale at your retail outlet.
  • Engage with customers through reviews: Respond to customer feedback, both positive and negative, to show that you value their opinions and are committed to improvement.
  • Add photos to attract attention: Use high-resolution images that highlight your products, services, and the welcoming atmosphere of your establishment.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Go to the Google My Business website and click 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure it matches your physical location in Lufkin. Accuracy is key for local searches.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best describes what you offer. This helps Google serve your profile to potential customers looking for specific services, like "Italian Restaurant" or "Pet Grooming."
  • Add your contact information: Include your phone number and website URL so customers can easily reach you. If you have social media pages, consider linking those as well.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a verification code to your business address. This step is crucial as it confirms your legitimacy and helps build trust with customers.

Tips for Optimizing Your Google Business Profile

Once you've set up your profile, it’s time to make it shine. Here are some tips to attract more customers:

  • Keep your information up-to-date: Regularly check and update your business hours and services, especially during holidays or special events like the Lufkin Rodeo.
  • Use high-quality images: Invest in photography that captures your business’s unique ambiance. Show off your best dishes if you own a restaurant, or display your most popular products if you run a retail shop.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews: Word-of-mouth is powerful. Ask satisfied customers to share their experiences online and make sure to respond to reviews to foster a sense of community.
  • Utilize the Posts feature: Share news and special promotions regularly. For example, if you’re hosting a sale or participating in the Lufkin Christmas Parade, make sure everyone knows by posting about it.

  • Appreciate businesses that share local events: Many Lufkin residents feel a sense of pride in supporting local businesses that engage with the community, whether through sponsorships or participation in local fairs.
