How Do I Get More Customers in Philadelphia?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Philadelphia can be a rewarding journey if you know the right strategies. With a rich history, from the Liberty Bell to the vibrant arts scene, this city offers plenty of opportunities for small businesses to thrive. Philadelphia, known as the 'City of Brotherly Love,' boasts a diverse population and a robust economy, making it an ideal location for entrepreneurs.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, a professional website is your digital storefront. It’s the first impression potential customers get, and it needs to be good. Here’s what actually works:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly, as many people search on their phones, especially when looking for local services like restaurants in the bustling neighborhoods of Center City or South Philly.
  • Include clear calls-to-action, like 'Get a Quote' or 'Contact Us'.
  • Optimize for local SEO, using keywords like 'Philadelphia plumber' or 'best cheesesteak in Philly'.
  • Regularly update your content with blogs or tips, especially about local events or attractions, to keep visitors engaged and improve your ranking on Google.

Leverage Google My Business

Having a Google My Business profile is crucial for local visibility. I've seen this work time and time again for numerous businesses:

  • Claim and verify your business listing, ensuring it's updated with seasonal hours, especially during events like the Philadelphia Flower Show.
  • Include accurate information like hours, address, and services relevant to the local community.
  • Add high-quality images of your business and products, showcasing your unique offerings, from artisanal goods to local cuisine.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews, and respond to them promptly, particularly after large local events where you may have gathered many new customers.

Maximize Your Online Reviews

Online reviews can make or break your reputation. Here’s how to get more:

  • Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp, especially after they’ve enjoyed a meal at your restaurant in the vibrant food scene around Reading Terminal Market.
  • Display these reviews on your website to build trust, utilizing testimonials that reflect the rich history and culture of Philadelphia.
  • Respond to all reviews, positive or negative, to show you care about customer feedback and are engaged with the local community.

Utilize Social Media Effectively

Social media is a powerful tool for small businesses in Philly. Here’s how you can use it:

  • Choose platforms where your target audience hangs out—Instagram and Facebook are great for visuals, especially given Philadelphia's stunning murals and historical landmarks.
  • Share engaging content, like behind-the-scenes looks at your business or customer testimonials highlighting their experiences in local hotspots.
  • Run local ads targeting Philadelphia residents to boost visibility, focusing on neighborhoods like Fishtown and Manayunk, which are known for their local shops and vibrant communities.

Network and Build Local Relationships

Networking is essential in a city like Philadelphia. Here are some strategies:

  • Attend local business events, meetups, or trade shows, particularly those held at venues like the Pennsylvania Convention Center or the Philadelphia Convention and Visitors Bureau.
  • Join local business associations or chambers of commerce, such as the Greater Philadelphia Chamber of Commerce, to connect with other entrepreneurs.
  • Partner with other businesses for cross-promotions or referral programs, taking advantage of Philadelphia's strong sense of community and collaboration.
