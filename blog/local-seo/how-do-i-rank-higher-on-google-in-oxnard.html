Understanding Local SEO

Look, I get it, the online world can be overwhelming. But local SEO is all about making sure folks in Oxnard find you when they search for services you offer. Here’s how you can get started:

  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing. Make sure all your info is accurate, including your address, phone number, and hours.
  • Use local keywords in your website content. Think terms like 'Oxnard coffee shop' or 'best plumber in Oxnard'.
  • Get listed in local directories like Yelp, Yellow Pages, and local chamber of commerce websites. Consistency is key, so make sure your info matches everywhere.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp. The more positive reviews you have, the better your chances of showing up in searches.
  • Create local content. Blog about Oxnard events or news that relate to your business—this helps build community connections. For instance, discuss highlights from the annual Oxnard Strawberry Festival or share insights on local surfing conditions.
