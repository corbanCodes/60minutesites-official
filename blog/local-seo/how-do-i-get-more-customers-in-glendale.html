How Do I Get More Customers in Glendale?

9 min read

Glendale, California, is a vibrant city situated in Los Angeles County, known for its diverse community and robust economy. With a population of over 200,000 residents, Glendale offers a rich blend of cultural backgrounds, making it an ideal market for small businesses. The city's strategic location near major freeways and its proximity to downtown Los Angeles further enhance its appeal, providing ample opportunities for entrepreneurs to attract and engage customers effectively.

Building Your Online Presence

Having a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. Here's what actually works:

  • Make sure your website is mobile-friendly; many people browse on their phones.
  • Optimize for local SEO by including keywords like 'Glendale' and neighborhood-specific terms such as 'Montrose' or 'South Glendale' in your content.
  • Claim your Google My Business listing; it’s essential for local visibility, especially in a city with numerous small businesses.
  • Use high-quality images and clear descriptions of your services, showcasing the unique features of your Glendale location.
  • Include a blog to share valuable content related to Glendale's community events, trends, or business tips, improving your SEO over time.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews

Customer reviews can make or break your business. The truth is, people trust other customers more than they trust ads. Here’s how to leverage reviews:

  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp, particularly focusing on local keywords.
  • Respond to all reviews, both good and bad, to show you care; Glendale residents appreciate businesses that value their feedback.
  • Display testimonials on your website to build trust; consider highlighting reviews from local customers to create a sense of community.
  • Consider a follow-up email after a service to ask for feedback, helping you to maintain engagement with your Glendale clientele.

Referrals and Networking

Referrals are a powerful way to gain new customers. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new business, such as discounts or freebies that resonate with the Glendale community.
  • Attend local networking events, such as those hosted by the Glendale Chamber of Commerce or local business expos, to meet other business owners and potential clients.
  • Partner with complementary businesses in Glendale, such as cafes teaming up with local artists, for cross-promotions that create a buzz in the community.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business associations to stay connected and informed about opportunities in Glendale.

Social Media Engagement

Social media is a must for engaging with your community. Here’s how to do it effectively:

  • Choose platforms where your audience hangs out; Instagram and Facebook are particularly effective for visual businesses, especially those that showcase Glendale's scenic spots, like Brand Park or the Americana at Brand.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content and stories to humanize your brand, perhaps featuring local events or partnerships with other Glendale businesses.
  • Run local promotions or contests to encourage engagement, such as giveaways that involve popular Glendale landmarks.
  • Use targeted ads to reach specific demographics in Glendale, ensuring your promotions resonate with the local population.

Running a local business in Glendale can be a rewarding hustle. By focusing on your online presence, leveraging reviews, networking, and engaging on social media, you can attract more customers and thrive in this vibrant community. Keep pushing forward; your hard work will pay off!
