How Do I Get More Customers in Tulsa?

9 min read

If you're running a small business in Tulsa, Oklahoma, you're in a vibrant market with plenty of potential to attract more customers. With the right strategies, you can stand out from the competition and build a loyal customer base.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront and should showcase what you offer. Here’s what actually works:

  • Create a user-friendly website that loads fast.
  • Use local SEO strategies to rank in search results for Tulsa-specific queries.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly since many users search on their phones.
  • Integrate a blog with helpful content that addresses local needs or interests.
  • Set up Google My Business to improve visibility in local searches and show your location on the map.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews

Reviews can make or break your business. I've seen this work time and time again: happy customers spread the word. Here’s how to encourage reviews:

  • Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook.
  • Make it easy by providing links in follow-up emails.
  • Respond to all reviews—positive and negative—to show you care and are engaged.
  • Highlight glowing reviews on your website and social media.

Leveraging Referrals and Networking

Referrals are a powerful way to gain new customers without spending a fortune on ads. Here’s how to tap into this:

  • Start by asking your existing customers for referrals—don’t be shy!
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new business.
  • Network with other local businesses in Tulsa; consider cross-promotions or partnerships.
  • Attend local events or join organizations like the Tulsa Chamber of Commerce to meet potential clients and collaborators.

Engaging on Social Media

Social media is a must in today’s market. Here’s what you need to do:

  • Choose platforms where your audience hangs out—Facebook and Instagram are solid choices for Tulsa.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content to engage your community.
  • Use local hashtags to reach a broader audience in Tulsa.
  • Run local ads to target specific demographics and increase your visibility.

Running a local business in Tulsa is a hustle, but with these strategies, you're well on your way to attracting more customers and building a thriving enterprise. Keep pushing, keep connecting, and watch your business grow!
