Why You Need a Google Business Profile

A Google Business Profile is crucial for enhancing your visibility in the local market. With so much competition, being easily found online can make all the difference. Here’s how a well-optimized profile can elevate your business:

  • Improved visibility for local searches: When potential customers search for services or products in Williston, your business will appear in relevant search results, increasing your chances of attracting new clientele.
  • Direct connection to customers: Your profile allows customers to call or message you directly, making it easy for them to inquire about your offerings or place an order.
  • Showcase your products and services: You can upload images, menus, or lists of services that highlight what you do best, allowing customers to see what you have to offer at a glance.
  • Ability to collect and display customer reviews: Positive reviews build trust and credibility. They can significantly influence potential customers' decisions, so encouraging satisfied customers to leave feedback can enhance your reputation.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your profile is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to ensure your business shines online:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Sign in with your Google account or create one if you don’t have it yet.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key. Make sure your address is correct to help locals find you easily, especially if your business is located near popular spots like the Williston Basin International Airport or the local parks.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best describes your business. This helps Google match your listing with relevant searches, so think carefully about what category fits your offerings.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: If you have a website, include it in your profile. This not only provides customers with more information but also enhances your credibility.
  • Verify your business: Choose a verification method that suits you best, such as receiving a postcard with a verification code or a phone call. This step is essential for appearing in local searches.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos, accurate business hours, and a compelling description that showcases what makes your business special. Use local flavor in your description to resonate with the community.

How Locals in Williston Search for Businesses

Understanding the search habits of residents in Williston can give you a significant advantage in attracting customers. Here are some insights into how locals search for businesses:

  • Location-based searches: Many Williston residents use specific phrases like "coffee shops near me" or "best plumbing services in Williston." Make sure your business appears in these searches by optimizing your profile with local keywords.
  • Seasonal influences: Searches can fluctuate based on the season. For example, during the winter months, people might look for snow removal services or winter apparel. Stay ahead of the game by updating your offerings seasonally.
  • Community recommendations: Word-of-mouth is powerful. Encourage your satisfied customers to leave reviews and engage with your profile. Respond to reviews to show appreciation and foster community relationships.
  • Using local landmarks: Incorporate well-known local terms or landmarks in your description. Mentioning nearby attractions like the Missouri River or the Williston Area Recreation Center can help you connect with the community and improve your search visibility.
