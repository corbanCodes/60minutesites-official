Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is your ticket to being found online. It's more than just a listing; it's a way to share vital information about your business with potential customers. Here's what you can gain from it:

  • Improved visibility in local searches: When someone searches for services you offer in Goshen, like 'best coffee shop Goshen' or 'plumbing services near me,' your business can appear prominently in local search results.
  • Direct engagement with customers: Through reviews and Q&A, you can interact directly with customers. Responding to reviews shows that you value feedback, which can encourage more people to choose your business.
  • Accurate information about your location, hours, and services: Your profile ensures that customers have access to up-to-date information. If you're located near popular landmarks like the Goshen Theater or the historic downtown area, make sure to highlight that to attract foot traffic.
  • Insights into customer behavior: Google provides valuable insights into how customers find and interact with your business. You can see how many people called you directly from the search results, which can guide your marketing strategy.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward, and here’s how you can do it:

  • Start by going to the Google Business Profile page: Click on 'Manage now' to begin the process. You'll need a Google account, so have that handy.
  • Enter your business name: Choose the name your customers know you by. Select a category that best fits your services so Google can match you with relevant searches.
  • Add your location: If you're near popular spots like the Elkhart County 4-H Fairgrounds or the Goshen Farmers Market, mention that! Being geographically relevant can help you attract local clientele.
  • Provide your contact details: Include a phone number and link to your website. This makes it easy for customers to reach you directly or learn more about your offerings.
  • Upload photos: Pictures are worth a thousand words. Showcase your business through high-quality images of your storefront, interior, or products. If you're a café, include images of your most popular dishes or drinks.
  • Verify your business: Google will typically send a postcard to your address with a verification code. This step is crucial as it grants you full control over your listing, allowing you to make updates and respond to reviews.

How Locals in Goshen Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search can help you tailor your Google Business Profile effectively. Here’s what you should know:

  • Common search phrases: Residents often search for businesses using simple phrases like 'restaurants near me' or 'plumbing services Goshen.' This emphasizes the importance of including relevant keywords in your business description.
  • Reliance on Google Maps: Many locals use Google Maps to find directions to businesses, making accurate location information critical. Ensure your address is correct and consider adding a map link in your profile.
  • The impact of reviews: Customers frequently look for businesses with high ratings and recent feedback. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews to boost your credibility.
  • Connection to community events: Events like the Goshen Farmers Market draw in crowds and can influence searches. Link your profile to local happenings and consider participating to increase your visibility and engagement with the community.
