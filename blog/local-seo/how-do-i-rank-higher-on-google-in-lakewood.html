How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Lakewood?

9 min read

Lakewood, Colorado, is a vibrant community with a unique blend of urban convenience and outdoor beauty. Nestled just west of Denver, Lakewood boasts a rich history and a diverse population. As a small business owner here, you’ve got the potential to connect with a friendly, local crowd eager to support their neighbors. Let’s chat about how you can boost your local SEO and get noticed in this bustling city, known for its stunning parks and cultural events.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is a game changer for local visibility. Make sure to:

  • Claim and verify your listing to appear in local search results and Google Maps.
  • Use a local phone number and address so customers can easily reach you.
  • Fill out every section, including business hours, services, and photos that showcase your business environment.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews—this builds trust and helps with rankings.

Use Local Keywords

When crafting your website and content, sprinkle in local keywords. Think about:

  • Phrases like 'best coffee in Lakewood' or 'Lakewood plumbing services.'
  • The neighborhoods you serve, like Belmar, Green Mountain, and the historic downtown area.
  • Local landmarks or events that resonate with your audience, such as the Lakewood Cultural Center or the scenic Bear Creek Lake Park.

Create Local Content

Creating content that speaks to Lakewood residents can set you apart. Consider:

  • Blogging about local events, like the Lakewood Farmers Market held at the Belmar Shopping District.
  • Sharing stories about local partnerships or community involvement, such as collaborations with the Lakewood Arts Council.
  • Highlighting customer testimonials from fellow Lakewood residents to build community trust.

Build Local Backlinks

Getting backlinks from reputable local sites can improve your SEO. Try:

  • Partnering with local bloggers or news outlets like the Lakewood Sentinel for features about your business.
  • Sponsoring community events, such as the annual Lakewood Heritage Festival, and getting mentioned on their websites.
  • Joining local business organizations like the Lakewood Chamber of Commerce for networking and referral opportunities.

Mobile Optimization is Key

A significant amount of local searches come from mobile devices. Ensure your website:

  • Loads quickly and is mobile-friendly for users on the go.
  • Shows your location clearly, with easy navigation to find directions.
  • Has a simple contact form for inquiries, making it convenient for potential customers to reach you.

Look, I get it—navigating the world of local SEO can feel overwhelming, especially with everything else on your plate. But by implementing these strategies, you can enhance your visibility and attract more customers in Lakewood. So, roll up your sleeves and start optimizing! Your local community is waiting to discover what you have to offer.
