Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is not just a simple task; it's a powerful way to enhance your online visibility and engage with your local audience. Follow these detailed steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile website and clicking 'Manage now.' If you already have a Google account, log in; if not, you’ll need to create one.
  • Enter your business name and address: Provide your business name exactly as it appears in the real world and ensure that your address is precise. This accuracy is crucial for locals trying to find you. For example, if you own a café in the Oviedo Marketplace, state your business name and include specific landmarks nearby to help with searches.
  • Select your business category: Choosing the right category is essential. This helps Google match your profile with relevant searches. If you're a florist, for instance, select 'Florist' and consider adding a sub-category like 'Wedding Florist' if applicable, which can attract a more targeted audience.
  • Add your phone number and website: Make it easy for potential customers to reach you. Include a direct phone number and a link to your website. This not only encourages inquiries but also enhances your credibility. If you have a special offer or a seasonal menu, consider linking directly to that page.
  • Verify your business: Verification is a crucial step. Google typically sends a postcard with a verification code to your physical address. This process not only confirms your legitimacy but also helps you gain trust from customers who see your profile.
  • Optimize your profile: Once verified, it’s time to make your profile shine. Add your business hours, photos of your products or services, and a compelling description. Highlight what makes your business unique—do you use locally sourced ingredients? Are you known for exceptional customer service? Details like these can set you apart from competitors.

How Locals Search for Businesses in Oviedo

Grasping how Oviedo residents search for businesses can give you a significant advantage. Here’s a closer look at common search behaviors:

  • Using Google Search: Many locals will type phrases such as ‘best tacos near me’ or ‘plumbers in Oviedo’ when in need of services. Make sure your profile contains relevant keywords to appear in these searches.
  • Checking Google Maps: Residents often rely on Google Maps to find nearby establishments, especially for day-to-day needs like grocery shopping or finding a café. Ensure your location is pinpointed accurately to appear on the map.
  • Social Media Recommendations: Platforms like Facebook and Instagram are popular for gathering recommendations. Engage with these platforms by sharing your Google Business Profile link and encouraging satisfied customers to leave reviews.
  • Yelp and Local Listings: Websites like Yelp serve as a trusted source for authentic reviews. Encourage your happy customers to leave feedback on these platforms, as positive reviews can significantly influence potential clients.
