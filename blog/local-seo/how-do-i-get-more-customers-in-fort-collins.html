How Do I Get More Customers in Fort Collins?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Fort Collins can feel like a challenge, but with the right strategies, you can stand out and attract a loyal clientele. The local market is vibrant, featuring a blend of young professionals, families, and outdoor enthusiasts, creating plenty of opportunities to tap into.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront and needs to make a great first impression. Here’s what actually works:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly; many customers will be browsing on their phones, especially as Fort Collins has a significant number of millennial residents.
  • Optimize for local SEO; use keywords like 'Fort Collins' and 'your service' to rank better in search results. Consider targeting specific neighborhoods like Old Town or Jessup Farm.
  • Include clear calls-to-action; make it easy for potential customers to contact you or request a quote, and consider offering a first-time customer discount.

Leverage Google My Business

Claiming and optimizing your Google My Business listing can significantly boost your visibility. Here’s how:

  • Fill out all the information completely, including hours, services, and photos that showcase your offerings in the Fort Collins area.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; this builds trust and helps with local rankings. Highlighting your proximity to local landmarks like Horsetooth Reservoir can attract more attention.
  • Post updates and promotions regularly; it keeps your audience engaged and informed about events like the Fort Collins Music Festival.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Word of mouth is powerful, especially in a community-oriented place like Fort Collins. Here's how to maximize it:

  • Ask happy customers to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook; positive feedback can sway new customers. Given Fort Collins' tight-knit community, this can lead to a snowball effect.
  • Create a referral program; incentivize existing customers to refer friends and family, perhaps by offering discounts that can be used at local attractions like the Fort Collins Museum of Discovery.
  • Share customer testimonials on your website and social media; it builds credibility and taps into local pride.

Engage on Social Media

Social media is a fantastic way to connect with your community. Consider these tips:

  • Post regularly on platforms where your target audience hangs out; Facebook and Instagram are popular in Fort Collins, particularly among the younger demographic.
  • Use local hashtags to reach a broader audience; this can attract more local customers. Consider hashtags specific to Fort Collins, such as #FortCollinsEats or #FoCoEvents.
  • Run contests or giveaways; this can increase engagement and visibility, especially if you collaborate with local influencers or businesses.

Network and Participate in Local Events

Being active in the community can lead to new customers. Here's what works:

  • Attend local networking events and meetups, such as those organized by the Fort Collins Chamber of Commerce; building relationships can lead to referrals.
  • Sponsor or participate in local events like the Fort Collins Farmers Market or the Larimer County Fair; this increases your visibility and shows community support.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions; it’s a win-win for everyone involved, especially during community events like First Friday Art Walk.

Running a local business in Fort Collins is no small feat, but with dedication and the right strategies, you can attract more customers and thrive. Keep pushing forward, and don’t hesitate to leverage the strong community support around you.
