Understanding Local SEO

Local SEO is all about optimizing your online presence to attract more business from relevant local searches. Here are some actionable tips to help you rank higher in local searches:

  • Claim Your Google My Business Listing: This is vital. Ensure your business information is accurate, including your address, phone number, and operating hours. Don't forget to select the appropriate categories for your business. Also, add high-quality images that showcase your products or services, as businesses with photos receive 42% more requests for directions and 35% more click-throughs to their websites.
  • Optimize for Local Keywords: Use keywords that reflect your business and location, such as 'best coffee in Tigard' or 'Tigard plumbing services.' Research local search terms using tools like Google Keyword Planner or Ubersuggest. Consider incorporating long-tail keywords that are specific to your offerings and include local landmarks or neighborhoods, such as 'family-friendly restaurants near Tigard's Cook Park.'
  • Encourage Customer Reviews: Positive reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp can significantly improve your rankings. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews by making it easy—provide links in follow-up emails or on your website. Responding to reviews, both positive and negative, shows that you value customer feedback and helps build trust.
  • Utilize Local Directories: Ensure your business is listed in local directories, including Yelp, Yellow Pages, and local chambers of commerce. Being part of these directories not only improves your visibility but also builds credibility. Make sure all listings have consistent information to avoid confusion.
  • Create Local Content: Write blog posts or create pages that focus on local events, news, or topics that resonate with your Tigard audience. For example, you could cover the highlights of the annual Tigard Festival of Balloons or provide tips for enjoying the natural beauty of the Tualatin River. Such content can attract local visitors to your site and establish your business as a community authority.

What Makes Tigard Unique for Local Businesses

Tigard has a rich local culture and community spirit, making it an ideal place for small businesses to thrive. Here are a few unique aspects:

  • Community Events: Tigard hosts a variety of events, like the annual Tigard Festival of Balloons, which can offer great opportunities for local businesses to engage with the community. Participating in or sponsoring these events can boost your visibility and allow you to connect with potential customers face-to-face.
  • Proximity to Portland: Being close to Portland allows Tigard businesses to attract customers from the city while maintaining a hometown feel. Make sure to highlight this accessibility in your marketing efforts, showcasing how your business offers a unique experience just a short drive from the urban hustle.
  • Local Support: Many Tigard residents prefer to shop local, creating a supportive environment for small businesses. Consider collaborating with other local businesses for cross-promotions or community initiatives, as this can enhance your reputation and help you reach a broader audience.
  • Natural Beauty: With parks like Cook Park and the Tualatin River, businesses that align with outdoor activities can easily draw in customers looking to enjoy the scenery. If your business offers outdoor gear, local art, or even food that complements a day in nature, make sure to market these connections to attract nature-loving locals.
