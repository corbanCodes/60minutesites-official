Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Having a Google Business Profile is indispensable for any local business in Shawnee. It gives you a platform to showcase your offerings and makes it easier for potential customers to find the critical information they need about your business. Here’s why prioritizing this profile is essential:

  • Increases visibility in Google search results: When locals search for services in Shawnee, having a well-optimized profile makes sure your business appears at the top of the results. This is especially important in a community with vibrant local spots like the Shawnee Mall or the historic downtown area.
  • Displays essential information: Your profile allows you to list your hours of operation, address, and contact information, ensuring that potential customers have everything they need at their fingertips. For example, if you own a café near the Shawnee Twin Lakes, make sure to highlight your hours during peak visiting times.
  • Encourages customer reviews and photos: Positive reviews can build trust and encourage new customers to check you out. Encourage satisfied customers to leave glowing reviews and share photos of their experience; this social proof can be invaluable.
  • Provides actionable insights: Google provides analytics on how customers find your business. This data can help you refine your marketing strategy, allowing you to target specific demographics or adjust your services based on feedback.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can be completed in just a few steps. Follow this detailed guide to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Manage Now.' This will take you to the setup interface.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure you enter your business name exactly as it appears in the real world, along with your full address. For example, if your shop is located on Bell Street near the Shawnee Public Library, include the correct address to help locals find you.
  • Select the appropriate category: Choose a category that best fits your business. This is important because it helps Google show your business to the right audience. For instance, if you own a bakery, select 'Bakery' to attract those searching for fresh pastries.
  • Add your phone number and website: If you have a business website, include it here. It’s a great way for potential customers to learn more about your offerings. Additionally, make sure your phone number is correct; you want to be reachable!
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business. This is usually done through a postcard sent to your business address. Follow the instructions provided to complete this step.
  • Enhance your profile: Once verified, you’ll want to complete your profile by adding high-quality photos of your business, your products, or even your team. A detailed description that highlights what makes your business unique will also attract more interest.
