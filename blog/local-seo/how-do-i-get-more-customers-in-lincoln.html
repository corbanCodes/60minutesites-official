How Do I Get More Customers in Lincoln?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Lincoln, Nebraska is all about standing out in a friendly, competitive market. With a mix of local charm and a growing economy, there are countless opportunities for small businesses to thrive.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront, and it needs to make a great first impression. Here's what actually works:

  • Make sure it’s mobile-friendly; many folks are browsing on their phones.
  • Include clear calls to action, like ‘Call Now’ or ‘Get a Quote’.
  • Optimize for search engines (SEO) to help local customers find you easily.
  • Showcase customer testimonials to build trust and credibility.

Leverage Google My Business

Google My Business is a game changer for local visibility. Here’s how to maximize it:

  • Claim your listing and fill it out completely with accurate info.
  • Post regular updates to keep your audience engaged.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; positive feedback boosts your profile.
  • Use photos to showcase your products or services and make your listing pop.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Word of mouth is still one of the strongest marketing tools. Here are some tips to get more referrals:

  • Ask happy customers directly for referrals and reviews.
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new business.
  • Highlight reviews on your social media and website to attract new customers.

Get Active on Social Media

Social media is an excellent way to connect with your local community. Here’s what works:

  • Find out which platform your target audience uses most, whether it’s Facebook, Instagram, or TikTok.
  • Share engaging content that showcases your personality and what you offer.
  • Run local promotions or contests to boost engagement and reach.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses to expand your audience.

Network Locally

Building relationships in Lincoln is crucial. Here’s how to network effectively:

  • Attend local events or business expos to meet potential customers and fellow business owners.
  • Join local business organizations like the Lincoln Chamber of Commerce for support and resources.
  • Offer to help local charities or community events; it’s great exposure and builds goodwill.

Remember, building a customer base takes time and effort, but with the right strategies in place, you can see real growth in your Lincoln business. Stay persistent, engage with your community, and keep refining your approach!
