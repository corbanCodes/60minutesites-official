Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started is easier than you think! Here's the secret to setting up a great profile:

  • Claim your business on Google Business Profile by visiting the official website and following the prompts to verify your business.
  • Fill out all your business information completely: include your business name, address in Santa Ana (for example, near landmarks like the Santa Ana Zoo or the Bowers Museum), phone number, and hours of operation.
  • Add high-quality photos of your products or services—consider showcasing local favorites like the vibrant murals in downtown Santa Ana or unique items from the local farmer's market.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews, as they build trust and attract more clients—especially important in a community that values personal recommendations.
  • Keep your information up-to-date, especially if your hours change or you have new offerings, such as seasonal items or special events.

Optimizing Your Profile

Once you've set up your profile, it’s time to optimize it for even better visibility. Trust me on this one, it makes a difference:

  • Use relevant keywords in your business description that reflect local interests, such as "authentic Mexican cuisine in Santa Ana" or "family-friendly activities near the Santa Ana River." This helps Google understand what you do and connect you with local searches.
  • Update your posts regularly with news, promotions, or events—consider highlighting local festivals, art walks, or community gatherings that attract residents.
  • Use the Q&A feature to answer common questions customers have about your business, such as parking availability or menu items, particularly for popular local events.
  • Monitor and respond to reviews to engage with your customers and show you value their feedback, which is crucial in a close-knit community like Santa Ana.
