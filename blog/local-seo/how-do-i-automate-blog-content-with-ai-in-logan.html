This ensures that your blog remains active, with fresh content for your audience to engage with regularly. Imagine setting a schedule for weekly posts on local events or seasonal activities and letting the AI handle the rest.

If your analytics show a spike in interest in local history, you can pivot your content strategy to include more posts about Logan's rich heritage, such as the historical sites in the downtown area or stories about the pioneers who settled in Cache Valley.
