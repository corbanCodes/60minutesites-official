Getting Started with Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is an uncomplicated process that can yield significant rewards. Here are the steps, detailed for your convenience:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile page. Log in using your Google account. If you don’t have one, creating an account takes just a few minutes.
  • Click on 'Manage now': This will take you to a prompt where you can enter your business name. Ensure you use your official business name for consistency and recognition.
  • Select the correct category: Picking the right category is crucial as it helps Google understand what your business does. For instance, if you run a bakery, choose 'Bakery' to appear in relevant searches.
  • Enter your business address: If you have a physical storefront in Auburn, provide the address. If you deliver services, such as landscaping in neighborhoods like Owasco or Skaneateles, opt for the option that allows you to specify service areas.
  • Add your contact details: Include your business phone number and website. Ensure your phone number is local, as this builds trust and makes it easier for locals to reach you.
  • Verify your business: Google requires verification to confirm that you are the rightful owner of the business. This can be done via a postcard mailed to your business address, a phone call, or email, depending on what Google offers you.
  • Enhance your profile: After verification, enrich your profile with high-quality photos of your products, your storefront, or your team. Don’t forget to include your business hours and any special offers or events to attract more customers.

How Locals in Auburn Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents in Auburn typically search for businesses can significantly enhance your online strategy. Here are some insights to consider:

  • Utilizing Google Search and Maps: Many locals turn to Google Search or Google Maps to find nearby services. For example, a family might search for 'best ice cream shop near me' while strolling around the city center. Ensure your profile is optimized with relevant keywords to appear in these searches.
  • Incorporating local keywords: Use keywords that residents are likely to search for, such as 'best pizza in Auburn' or 'affordable electricians near me.' These phrases should be naturally integrated into your business description and posts.
  • Encouraging customer reviews: Online reviews play a pivotal role in local business visibility. Encourage your satisfied customers to leave glowing reviews on your profile, as community trust is paramount. Consider offering a small incentive, like a discount on their next purchase, for leaving a review.
  • Staying updated on local events: Auburn has a rich community life, with events like the Auburn Public Theater performances or the local farmers’ market. Share timely updates on your Google Business Profile, showcasing your participation in these events or any special promotions you are running in conjunction with them.
