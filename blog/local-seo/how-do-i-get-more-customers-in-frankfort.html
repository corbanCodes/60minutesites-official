How Do I Get More Customers in Frankfort?

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

The question isn't if your business can thrive in Frankfort, but how you can make it happen. With a charming downtown, a tight-knit community, and a growing population, there are countless opportunities for small businesses to attract more customers. From the picturesque Main Street lined with local shops to the scenic beauty of the Kentucky State Capitol, Frankfort's vibrant atmosphere is ripe for entrepreneurship. Let’s dive into both online and offline strategies that can help your business stand out and flourish in this unique location.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Establishing a strong online presence is vital for small businesses. Here are some actionable strategies tailored specifically for Frankfort:

  • Optimize your website for local SEO: Use keywords related to Frankfort, such as 'Frankfort dining', 'shopping in Frankfort', or 'Frankfort services', to ensure your site ranks higher in search results. Incorporate neighborhood names like 'East Frankfort' or 'South Frankfort' to target specific demographics.
  • Engage with potential customers on social media: Share content that resonates with the local community, such as photos from the Frankfort Fall Festival or behind-the-scenes looks at your business. Use relevant hashtags like #FrankfortKY and tag local landmarks to increase visibility.
  • Create an email newsletter: Regularly update your customers with a well-designed newsletter that features local events, your latest products, and exclusive deals. Consider adding a section highlighting other local businesses to foster community spirit.
  • Utilize Google My Business: Claim and optimize your profile to enhance your visibility in local searches. Make sure to include your business hours, location, and engaging images of your products or storefront. Encourage customers to ask questions directly through this platform.
  • Encourage reviews: Actively ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp. You can make this easier by providing links in follow-up emails or offering small incentives, such as a discount on their next purchase, for leaving a review.

Offline Strategies to Boost Foot Traffic

While online strategies are essential, don't forget the power of personal connections. Consider these offline tactics that can make a significant impact in Frankfort:

  • Participate in local events: The Frankfort Fall Festival and the Frankfort Farmers Market are fantastic opportunities to showcase your products and services. Set up a booth or sponsor a local activity to gain exposure and connect with potential customers.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses: Partner with nearby shops for cross-promotions, such as a 'Shop Local' day where customers receive discounts when they visit multiple businesses. This can help draw traffic from one store to another, benefiting everyone involved.
  • Offer special discounts: Create enticing offers for first-time customers or referral programs that reward existing customers for bringing in new business. For example, a 'Bring a Friend' discount can encourage people to visit your store together.
  • Utilize direct mail campaigns: Target nearby neighborhoods with well-designed flyers or postcards that highlight your unique offerings. Include a special discount code to encourage recipients to visit your business. Consider highlighting local landmarks or events in your messaging to establish a sense of community.
  • Host workshops or community gatherings: Organize events at your location that allow customers to interact with your products or services. This could be a cooking class, a craft night, or a discussion on a topic relevant to your business. Building personal relationships can convert casual visitors into loyal customers.

Embracing the Frankfort Business Community

Frankfort boasts a vibrant business community that prides itself on supporting local ventures. Joining local chambers of commerce or business groups can open doors to valuable networking opportunities. Here are some ways to get involved:

  • Attend monthly meetings or mixers: These gatherings are a great way to meet fellow business owners, exchange ideas, and discuss challenges. You can find local groups via the Frankfort Area Chamber of Commerce, which also hosts numerous events throughout the year.
  • Collaborate on community projects: Working together with other businesses on initiatives like park clean-ups or local charity fundraisers can significantly boost your visibility and reputation within the community.
  • Participate in community service events: Show your commitment to Frankfort by getting involved in local service projects. This could be donating your time or resources to local schools or shelters, which will resonate well with community members.
  • Stay informed about local legislative changes: Understanding how local laws affect small businesses can help you adapt and thrive. Participate in local government meetings or subscribe to newsletters from the chamber of commerce to keep up-to-date.
  • Engage with local media: Reach out to local newspapers, radio stations, and blogs to share your story or offer expert opinions on subjects related to your business. Features in community publications can enhance your visibility and establish your business as a trusted local entity.

By combining both online and offline strategies, you can effectively draw more customers to your business in Frankfort. This approach not only helps you reach a broader audience but also fosters a sense of community that is essential for long-term success.
