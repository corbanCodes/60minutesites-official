What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

To make your Google Business Profile stand out in Fargo, here’s what you need to focus on:

  • Complete all sections of your profile, including your business name, address (consider including landmarks like the Fargo Theatre or West Acres Mall), phone number, and website.
  • Use high-quality images that showcase your products or services, especially those that resonate with the local culture, such as images featuring local ingredients or designs that reflect Fargo's artistic community.
  • Write a compelling business description that tells your story and highlights what makes you unique. Mention your connection to Fargo and any community involvement, such as participation in the Fargo Street Fair or local charity events.
  • Keep your hours up-to-date, especially during Fargo's busy seasons, including the Fargo Film Festival in March or the Red River Valley Fair in July.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews; in Fargo, where community support is strong, glowing testimonials can really boost your credibility!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set up my Google Business Profile?

Simply go to the Google Business Profile website, sign in with your Google account, and follow the prompts to create your profile. Make sure to verify your business through the mail or phone option provided to enhance your credibility.

Q: What if my business is not showing up in searches?

Make sure all your information is complete and accurate. Also, try to get more reviews and post updates regularly to boost your visibility. Engaging with local events or collaborating with other Fargo businesses can also help improve your profile's prominence.

Q: Can I manage multiple locations on one account?

Yes, you can manage multiple locations from one account, making it easy to handle all your businesses in one place. This is particularly useful for businesses that operate in different parts of Fargo, such as those near the NDSU campus or the Sanford Medical Center.
