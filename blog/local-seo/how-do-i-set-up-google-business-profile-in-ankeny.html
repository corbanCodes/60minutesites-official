Why Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is your business's first impression online. It's where customers can find essential information about you, such as location, hours, and services. Here are some compelling reasons why you should take the time to set it up:

  • Increased visibility on Google Maps and Search: With Ankeny's growing population and vibrant community, having your business visible on Google Maps can lead to foot traffic from locals and visitors alike. When someone searches for 'best pizza in Ankeny,' your profile can be the one they see.
  • Ability to respond to customer reviews: Engaging with customers by responding to reviews—both positive and negative—builds trust and shows that you value their feedback. This interaction can sway potential customers who are considering your services.
  • Insights into how customers find and interact with your business: Google provides data on how many people viewed your profile, clicked on your website, or called your business. This information can help you tailor your marketing strategies and improve customer engagement.
  • Free advertising opportunity to showcase your services: Your profile allows you to highlight promotions, events, or seasonal offerings. If you're hosting a special event at your local Ankeny Market & Pavilion or running a sale, this is the perfect platform to share that information.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile in Ankeny is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Navigate to the Google Business Profile page and click 'Start Now.' This will guide you through the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure your business name is exactly how it appears in the real world, and ensure your address matches your physical location in Ankeny. This detail is crucial for local search accuracy.
  • Choose your business category: Selecting the correct category helps Google understand what you do. For example, if you own a bakery, you would select 'Bakery' as your primary category. This classification plays a significant role in search results.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: If you have a website, include it here. A direct link makes it easier for potential customers to learn more about your offerings.
  • Verify your business: Google may send a postcard to your address with a verification code, which you will need to enter to confirm your business. Keep an eye on your mailbox for this important step!
  • Fill out your profile: Complete your profile with hours of operation, high-quality photos, and a description that highlights what makes your business unique. For instance, if you offer artisan bread or specialty coffee, share that story to attract food enthusiasts in Ankeny.

How Locals in Ankeny Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search for businesses can help you optimize your profile. Here’s what you should know:

  • Google Maps is a go-to tool: Many locals use Google Maps to find nearby services, especially with well-known landmarks like Prairie Ridge Middle School or the Ankeny Market & Pavilion serving as reference points. Make sure your business is easily locatable on the map.
  • Common search phrases: Locals often use specific phrases like 'best coffee shop in Ankeny' or 'plumbers near me.' Incorporate these keywords into your business description and posts to improve your chances of appearing in relevant searches.
  • Local events influence searches: Events such as the Ankeny SummerFest create a spike in searches for businesses related to food, entertainment, and goods. Consider running promotions or events during these times to maximize your visibility.
  • Customer reviews can sway decisions: Positive reviews play a significant role in influencing new shoppers. Encourage satisfied customers to leave feedback on your profile. You might even consider incentivizing reviews through discounts or special offers for repeat customers.
