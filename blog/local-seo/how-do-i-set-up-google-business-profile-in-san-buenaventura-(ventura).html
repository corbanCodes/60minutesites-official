How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is a simple process, and following these steps will have you online in no time:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile page.
  • Sign in or Create an Account: Use your existing Google account or create a new one tailored for your business.
  • Add Your Business Name and Address: Make sure to enter your business name accurately. If you have multiple locations, double-check that you’re entering the details for your San Buenaventura site. This is where locals will come to find you.
  • Select Your Business Category: Choose the category that best represents your business. Whether you’re a quaint café in Midtown or a surf shop in Pierpont, make sure the category accurately reflects what you offer to attract the right audience.
  • Provide Contact Information: Add your phone number and website URL. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and effortlessly. They specialize in helping local businesses establish an online presence without the hassle.
  • Verify Your Business: Google will typically send a postcard to your business address to verify your listing. Follow the instructions on the postcard to complete this essential step.
  • Complete Your Profile: Once verified, fill out your profile with additional details such as business hours, high-quality photos, and a compelling description of your services. For instance, include photos of your products, your storefront, or even happy customers enjoying your services to create a welcoming impression.
