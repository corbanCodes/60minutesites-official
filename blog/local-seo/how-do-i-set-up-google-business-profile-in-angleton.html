Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating your Google Business Profile is a simple yet impactful process. Follow these detailed steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website. This is your starting point, where you’ll begin to create your profile.
  • Click on ‘Manage now’ and sign in using your Google account. If you don’t have one, it’s quick to set up a free account.
  • Enter your business name and address. Ensure the information is accurate and specific, especially if your business is near well-known Angleton landmarks like the Angleton DQ, where families gather, or the historic Brazoria County Courthouse, a central meeting point in town.
  • Select your business category. Choosing the right category is essential as it helps Google match your listing with the right customers. For instance, if you run a coffee shop, you might select “Coffee Shop” or “Café.”
  • Provide your phone number and website. Having a contact number is crucial for potential customers to reach you, and a website adds credibility. If you don’t have a website yet, consider utilizing 60 Minute Sites for an efficient and professional setup.
  • Verify your business. Google will typically send a postcard to your business address containing a verification code. This step is vital, as it confirms that your business is legitimate and helps prevent fraud.
  • Enhance your profile by adding photos, business hours, and a compelling description that captures the unique essence of your Angleton business. High-quality images of your products or services, and even local events you participate in, can significantly attract customers.

How Locals in Angleton Search for Businesses

Understanding the search habits of local residents can give your business a competitive advantage. Here are some insights into how people in Angleton typically seek out businesses:

  • Many locals use specific search terms, such as ‘best coffee in Angleton’ or ‘plumbers near me,’ which highlights the importance of including relevant keywords in your profile description.
  • Residents frequently turn to Google Maps to find nearby services, making it essential for your profile to be fully optimized with accurate information.
  • Social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram play a significant role in how locals discover new dining options, shops, or services. Engaging content on these platforms can drive traffic to your Google Business Profile.
  • In a close-knit community like Angleton, word of mouth remains a powerful tool. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews and share their experiences online, as this can enhance your visibility and attract new clients.
