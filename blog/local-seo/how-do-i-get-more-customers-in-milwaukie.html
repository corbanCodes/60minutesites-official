How Do I Get More Customers in Milwaukie?

5 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Here's the thing: attracting customers to your small business in Milwaukie isn’t just about having a great product or service; it's about getting the word out effectively. Whether you're nestled near the serene banks of the Willamette River or in the bustling heart of downtown, there are plenty of strategies that can help you draw in more customers and grow your local presence. With the charm of the Milwaukie Farmers Market and the vibrant community events, there’s no shortage of opportunities to connect with your audience. Let’s dive deeper into the strategies that can help you thrive in this delightful Oregon town.

Online Strategies to Boost Customer Engagement

Leveraging the internet can open up a world of opportunities for your business. Here are some actionable online strategies that can make a difference:

  • Optimize your website with local SEO: Focus on local keywords like 'Milwaukie coffee shop' or 'best Milwaukie bakery.' Ensure your website is mobile-friendly and includes your location, hours of operation, and contact information. Use Google My Business to manage your online presence and encourage local searches.
  • Create engaging social media posts: Showcase your products with high-quality images and videos. Consider sharing behind-the-scenes looks at your business or customer testimonials to create a personal connection. Utilize platforms like Instagram and Facebook, where you can engage with the community through polls, stories, and live events.
  • Start a blog: Share valuable insights related to your industry, whether it's tips on home improvement if you run a hardware store or recipes if you own a café. This not only positions you as an expert but also helps attract traffic through organic search results. Use local topics or highlight community events to make your posts relatable.
  • Utilize email marketing: Create a monthly newsletter that shares updates, exclusive deals, or upcoming events. Use eye-catching subject lines and ensure your content is valuable to your readers, encouraging them to engage and visit your business.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews: After a positive experience, ask your customers to share their thoughts on platforms like Google and Yelp. Positive reviews can significantly influence new customers’ decisions and enhance your credibility in the community.

Offline Strategies to Attract Foot Traffic

While online strategies are crucial, don't overlook the power of connecting with the community in real life. Here are some effective offline methods that can help bring customers through your door:

  • Participate in local events or farmers' markets: Set up a booth at the Milwaukie Farmers Market or local fairs to showcase your products. This face-to-face interaction can build relationships and create lasting impressions on potential customers.
  • Partner with other local businesses: Collaborate with nearby businesses for cross-promotions. For example, if you own a bakery, team up with a local coffee shop to offer discounts when customers buy from both places, encouraging foot traffic to both establishments.
  • Host workshops or classes: If you have expertise in a specific area, such as cooking or crafting, host workshops in your store. This not only attracts new customers but also fosters a sense of community and learning.
  • Utilize eye-catching signage: Invest in attractive signage and window displays that reflect your brand and entice passersby. Highlight any current promotions or new arrivals to grab their attention as they walk by.
  • Join or support local charities: Get involved with local charities or community events. This not only builds goodwill but also increases your visibility within the community. Consider hosting a charity event or donating a portion of your sales to a local cause.

Embracing the Milwaukie Business Community

The strength of the Milwaukie business community is something special. By connecting with other local business owners, you can share ideas, resources, and even customers. Here are some ways to get involved and strengthen those connections:

  • Join the Milwaukie Chamber of Commerce: This is a fantastic way to network with other businesses and participate in community initiatives. The Chamber often hosts events and provides resources that can help you grow your business.
  • Attend local business meet-ups: Look for networking events or meet-ups where you can build relationships with fellow entrepreneurs. These gatherings can lead to collaborations and support systems that benefit your business.
  • Collaborate on local promotions: Participate in or initiate campaigns like a 'Milwaukie Shop Local' initiative. Such efforts encourage residents to support small businesses and can lead to a boost in customer traffic.
  • Engage with community boards or online groups: Stay active on local social media groups or forums to keep up with community events and opportunities. This not only helps you stay informed but also allows you to contribute and showcase your business.
  • Support local art and culture: Consider showcasing local artists’ work in your store or collaborating with them for events. This creates a sense of community and can attract customers who appreciate supporting local talent.

Growing your customer base in Milwaukie can be a fulfilling journey with the right strategies in place.
