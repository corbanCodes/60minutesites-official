How Do I Get More Customers in Beaverton?

5 min read

Beaverton is a gem nestled in the heart of Oregon, known for its lush parks, family-friendly neighborhoods, and a thriving local economy. The community spirit here is palpable, making it an inviting place for customers to explore. However, with so much competition among the unique cafes, shops, and service providers, it’s essential to employ a variety of effective strategies to draw in more customers and make your business stand out.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Your online presence is more than just a necessity; it’s a powerful tool that can significantly enhance your visibility and customer engagement. Here are some effective strategies to consider:

  • Optimize Your Website: Start by ensuring your website is user-friendly and mobile-responsive, as many customers browse on their smartphones. Incorporate relevant local keywords such as 'Beaverton coffee shop' or 'Beaverton home services' to enhance your search engine visibility. Consider adding a blog section to share local stories or tips that reflect your expertise and interest in the community.
  • Leverage Social Media: Use platforms like Instagram and Facebook to create a vibrant online presence. Share high-quality images of your products, behind-the-scenes content, and engaging stories about your business journey. Don’t forget to interact with followers—respond to comments, ask for feedback, and share user-generated content to foster a sense of community.
  • Create a Google My Business Listing: This free listing is your ticket to appearing in local searches. Make sure to provide accurate information about your business hours, location, and services. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, as positive feedback can significantly influence potential customers looking for reliable options in Beaverton.
  • Run Targeted Online Ads: Platforms like Facebook and Google Ads allow you to reach specific demographics based on location, interests, and behaviors. For instance, if you run a family-oriented business, target ads to parents in the Beaverton area, promoting special family deals or events.
  • Build an Email List: Offer a newsletter signup on your website to keep customers informed about promotions, new products, or upcoming events. Consider creating a lead magnet, such as a free guide or discount, to encourage sign-ups. Regularly send out engaging content to maintain contact and remind them of your offerings.

Offline Strategies to Gain Local Customers

While online tactics are essential, the personal touch is irreplaceable. Here are some offline strategies that can help you foster relationships and draw in local customers:

  • Host Local Events: Organize workshops, meet-ups, or product launches at your location. For example, if you own a bookstore, consider hosting a local author reading or a book club meeting. This not only attracts foot traffic but also creates a buzz around your business.
  • Collaborate with Other Local Businesses: Form partnerships with neighboring businesses for cross-promotions. For instance, if you run a bakery, team up with a nearby coffee shop to offer a special discount for customers who purchase a pastry and coffee together.
  • Participate in Community Fairs: Beaverton hosts various events, such as the Beaverton Farmers Market and the Beaverton Night Market. Setting up a booth at these events allows you to showcase your offerings, engage with the community, and build brand awareness.
  • Utilize Print Marketing: Design eye-catching flyers or postcards that highlight your business and special offers. Distribute them in local neighborhoods, community centers, or during events to keep your brand top-of-mind for residents.
  • Engage with Local Media: Reach out to local blogs, newspapers, or radio stations like Beaverton Valley Times to share your business story. A feature story or interview can give you tremendous exposure and credibility in the community.

The Beaverton Business Community

The Beaverton business community is characterized by a strong sense of collaboration and support. By getting involved, you can build valuable connections and enhance your business visibility. Here’s how you can immerse yourself in this thriving community:

  • Attend Networking Events: Regularly participate in events organized by the Beaverton Chamber of Commerce or local business associations. These gatherings are an excellent opportunity to meet other entrepreneurs, share insights, and discover potential partnerships.
  • Join Local Business Groups: Find a group that aligns with your industry. For instance, if you're in the retail sector, joining a local retail association can provide valuable resources and strategies tailored to your niche.
  • Participate in Community Service: Volunteer for local initiatives or sponsor community events. This not only enhances your reputation but also shows that your business cares about the community, attracting customers who value social responsibility.
  • Engage on Local Online Forums: Platforms like Nextdoor allow you to connect with residents and other businesses. Participate in discussions, offer advice, and share your business information to build relationships and trust within the community.
  • Support Local Causes: Aligning your business with local charities or community events can greatly enhance your reputation. Consider sponsoring a local sports team or donating to a community fundraiser, which can also attract customers who appreciate businesses that give back.

Building your customer base in Beaverton may take time and effort, but with the right strategies—both online and offline—your business can thrive.
