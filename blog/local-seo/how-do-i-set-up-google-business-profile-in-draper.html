Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is simpler than you might think. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile website and click 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup page where you can begin the process.
  • Enter your business name: Choose a name that accurately represents your business. If your business has a common name, consider adding your location or a unique identifier to help it stand out.
  • Select the right category: Picking the correct category is crucial as it helps Google match your business with relevant searches. Take some time to explore the available categories to find the one that best fits your offerings.
  • Add your location: If you have a physical store in Draper, make sure to add your address. This is essential for locals who are searching for nearby services. If you operate online or don’t have a physical location, you can specify the areas you serve instead.
  • Provide your contact information: Make it easy for potential customers to reach you by including your phone number and website. Ensure that your website is functional and up to date, as this will be the first impression many customers have of your business.
  • Verify your business: Google usually requires you to verify your business, which often involves receiving a postcard with a verification code sent to your listed address. Follow the instructions on this postcard to complete the verification process.
  • Optimize your profile: Once verified, enrich your profile by adding high-quality photos of your business, products, or services. Include your business hours and a compelling description that highlights your unique offerings. Use this space to share your story, what sets you apart, and why locals should choose you.

Locals often rely on feedback from their neighbors, so actively encourage satisfied customers to leave positive ratings. You could even consider creating a small incentive program for customers who take the time to write a review.

Consider linking your services to these happenings by offering promotions or participating in community events to boost your visibility and attract more foot traffic to your business.
