How to Find a Salon in Memphis

5 min read

Finding the right salon can feel as tricky as navigating the winding streets of Midtown Memphis. With so many options blending into one another, it's essential to know how to sift through them and find a place that meets your hair needs and makes you feel fabulous. Whether you're looking for a trendy cut in Cooper-Young, a classic style in Germantown, or a vibrant color in Downtown, there’s a perfect salon waiting for you. Let’s dive into how to make this search easier and more enjoyable.

Know Your Hair Needs

Before you start your search, it's important to identify what you're looking for. This foundational step will help you narrow down your options effectively. Consider the following:

  • Your hair type and texture: Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or coily? Different hair types require different techniques, so knowing yours will guide your choice.
  • The specific services you need: Are you in need of a fresh cut, color, treatments, or styling for a special occasion? If you’re seeking specialized services like balayage or keratin treatments, you’ll want to find a salon that offers these.
  • Your budget: Set a realistic budget for your salon visit. Memphis has a range of salons from budget-friendly options to high-end boutiques, so knowing your financial limits will help you find a suitable match.
  • Any stylist specialties you prefer: If you have specific requirements, such as expertise in curly hair or a knack for edgy hairstyles, note these down. It can be beneficial to look for salons that promote these specialties on their websites or social media.

Ask for Recommendations

Word of mouth is powerful. Talk to friends, family, and coworkers about their favorite salons. Local insights can be incredibly helpful. Here are some tips to make the most of this approach:

  • Look for specific stylist recommendations: Instead of just asking for a salon name, inquire about individual stylists. A personal recommendation can lead you to the perfect match.
  • Ask about their overall experience: What did they like or dislike about their visits? Personal experiences can give you a clearer picture of what to expect.
  • Check local social media groups: Platforms like Nextdoor or Facebook community groups are great places to ask for recommendations from locals who have tried various salons.
  • Consider visiting salons that have a strong community presence: Salons that engage with local events or charities often have a loyal customer base, which speaks to their quality and service.

Research Online Reviews

Online reviews can provide valuable insights into a salon's reputation and service quality. Websites like Yelp, Google Reviews, and even Instagram can be great places to start. When researching, pay attention to:

  • The quantity and quality of reviews: A salon with numerous positive reviews is likely to be a safe bet. Look for consistency in the feedback.
  • Comments on customer service and atmosphere: Do clients mention the friendliness of the staff? Is the environment inviting and comfortable? This information can greatly influence your salon experience.
  • Recent reviews: Ensure that the salon maintains high standards over time by checking the latest reviews. A few bad reviews can happen, but consistent patterns of dissatisfaction are a red flag.
  • Social media presence: Check platforms like Instagram for before-and-after photos, which can give you a visual sense of a stylist's work and creativity.

Visit the Salon

Once you’ve narrowed down your choices, consider visiting the salons in person. This allows you to gather firsthand impressions. During your visit, make sure to:

  • Gauge the atmosphere and cleanliness: A clean and well-organized salon speaks volumes about the professionalism of the staff and their attention to detail.
  • Meet the staff: Interact with the stylists and support staff. Friendly and attentive staff will make your experience much more enjoyable.
  • Ask questions: Don’t hesitate to inquire about the services offered, the products used, and the stylist's experience. A good stylist will be happy to share their knowledge and expertise.
  • Observe client interactions: Pay attention to how stylists interact with their clients. Happy, relaxed clients are often a sign of a great salon atmosphere.

Take Advantage of Promotions

Many salons in Memphis offer enticing first-time customer discounts or special packages. This can be a great way to try out a new place without committing to a full-price service. Here are some tips on how to find these deals:

  • Check the salon's website: Many salons advertise their promotions directly on their websites. Look for a 'Specials' or 'Offers' section.
  • Follow them on social media: Salons often post flash sales or exclusive discounts on their Instagram or Facebook pages. Engaging with their social media can also give you a feel for their style and culture.
  • Sign up for newsletters: Many salons offer discounts to subscribers of their email newsletters, so be sure to sign up for updates.
  • Inquire directly: When you call or visit a salon, don’t hesitate to ask if they have any ongoing promotions. You might be pleasantly surprised by what’s available.

Finding the perfect salon in Memphis doesn’t have to be overwhelming. With a little research and effort, you can discover a place that suits your style and needs. Explore different neighborhoods like the artsy vibe of South Main or the upscale shops in East Memphis to see what each salon has to offer.
