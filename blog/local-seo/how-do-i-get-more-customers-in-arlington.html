How Do I Get More Customers in Arlington?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you're a small business owner in Arlington, Texas, you're in a prime spot to grow your customer base. With a vibrant community and a rich blend of local culture, there are plenty of opportunities to stand out and attract more clients. Arlington is home to the Dallas Cowboys and Texas Rangers, making it a hub of sports and entertainment, which can be leveraged for local marketing efforts.

Build a Strong Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront, so make it count. Here's what actually works:

  • Invest in a user-friendly, mobile-responsive website, particularly important as many locals search on their phones while out and about.
  • Optimize for local SEO by including 'Arlington' in your keywords and mentioning local landmarks like the AT&T Stadium or Globe Life Field.
  • Create content that speaks to your community's needs and interests, such as local events, news, or sponsorships.
  • Ensure your contact information is easy to find and includes a call to action, encouraging visits to your shop or inquiries about your services.

Leverage Google My Business

Getting your business listed on Google My Business is a game-changer. It’s an easy way to improve your local search visibility. Here’s how to maximize it:

  • Fill out every section completely with accurate information including your operating hours, address, and business description highlighting your connection to Arlington.
  • Add high-quality photos of your business and services, showcasing the local atmosphere or unique features.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews, especially mentioning specific experiences they had at your location.
  • Regularly update your profile with posts and offers to engage potential clients, highlighting local events or promotions.

Harness the Power of Reviews

Word of mouth is still one of the most powerful marketing tools, and online reviews are the modern version. Here’s how to get more:

  • Request reviews from satisfied customers through follow-up emails or text messages, especially after local events or promotions.
  • Make it easy for them by providing direct links to your review profiles on platforms like Google, Yelp, and Facebook.
  • Respond to all reviews, both positive and negative, to show you value feedback and are engaged with your customers.
  • Display your best reviews on your website and social media to build trust, perhaps even featuring local patrons to strengthen community ties.

Networking and Local Partnerships

In Arlington, the local business community thrives on connections. Here’s how to tap into that:

  • Attend local Chamber of Commerce events and other networking opportunities, such as Arlington's Business Expo, to meet fellow business owners.
  • Partner with complementary local businesses for cross-promotions, for instance, a restaurant teaming up with a local brewery for special events.
  • Join networking groups that focus on business growth and community support, such as the Arlington Young Professionals.
  • Participate in local fairs and festivals, like the Arlington Fourth of July Parade or the Texas State Fair, to increase your visibility and connect with potential customers.

Utilize Social Media Effectively

Social media can be a powerful tool to engage with your customers. Here’s what I’ve seen work:

  • Focus on platforms that resonate with your target audience, like Facebook or Instagram, especially to highlight events in Arlington like sports games or community festivals.
  • Share local content or stories that highlight your connection to Arlington, showcasing your involvement in community initiatives or events.
  • Use targeted ads to reach potential customers in your area, particularly during local events or peak shopping times.
  • Engage with your followers regularly to build a loyal community, sharing user-generated content or spotlighting local customers.

In the hustle of running a local business in Arlington, remember that every effort counts. By leveraging these strategies, you're not just getting customers; you're building a community around your brand. Stay persistent, and you'll see the fruits of your labor!
