What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating a standout Google Business Profile is easier than you think. Here’s the secret to making yours shine:

  • Claim and verify your business to start building trust within the Mobile community.
  • Fill in all the details like business name, address, and phone number (NAP).
  • Add high-quality photos of your products, storefront, or services to attract attention; consider showcasing images that highlight the unique aspects of Mobile, such as the beautiful Mobile Bay or the historic architecture.
  • Write a clear and engaging business description that tells your story, including your connection to Mobile’s diverse culture and community.
  • Update your hours of operation, especially during local events like Mardi Gras or the Mobile Film Festival, when foot traffic increases.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews; they really help! Local reviews can build your reputation in the area.
  • Regularly post updates, offers, or events, especially those that coincide with Mobile’s local festivities or seasonal activities, to keep your profile fresh and engaging.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Just search for your business on Google, and if it appears, click 'Claim this business' and follow the prompts.

Q: Can I change my business information later?

Absolutely! You can update your profile anytime to reflect changes in hours, services, or anything else, especially during the busy tourist season in Mobile.

Q: How do I get more reviews?

Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews after their experience or send a follow-up email with a link to your profile. Highlighting local landmarks in your communications can enhance the appeal.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical location?

You can still create a Google Business Profile as a service area business by listing your service areas instead of a physical address, which is useful for those providing services across the beautiful Mobile region.

Q: Is it important to post regularly?

Yes! Regular updates keep your profile active and can improve visibility in search results, especially if you align your posts with local events and happenings.
