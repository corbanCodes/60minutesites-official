Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for your business to be discovered online. Here’s why:

  • Boosts visibility in local search results: When potential customers search for services or products in Shakopee, your profile can appear prominently in search results, making it easier for them to find you.
  • Helps customers find your location, hours, and contact information: Your profile acts as a digital storefront, allowing customers to quickly access your address, operating hours, and contact details, thus enhancing convenience.
  • Engage with customers through reviews and Q&A: Responding to reviews and answering questions shows that you value customer feedback and are engaged with the community, which can foster trust and loyalty.
  • Provides insights on customer interactions: With Google Business Profile's analytics, you can understand how customers connect with your listing, such as how they found you or what actions they took, allowing you to refine your marketing strategies.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the official site and click on 'Start Now' to begin the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Ensure that this information is accurate and matches what you have on your physical storefront and other online platforms, as this consistency helps with local SEO.
  • Select your business category: Choose the category that best represents your business. This is crucial as it helps Google list your business in relevant searches, so think carefully about how customers might describe what you offer.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create a professional-looking website quickly. Having a website linked to your profile can significantly enhance your credibility.
  • Verify your business: Google will provide a verification method, often sending a postcard with a code to the address you provided. This step is vital to confirm your business's legitimacy.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enrich your profile with detailed information, including your services, business hours, and high-quality photos that showcase your offerings. Regularly update your profile with posts about promotions, events, or new products to keep your customers engaged and informed.

How Locals in Shakopee Search for Businesses

Understanding how your future customers search can give you a competitive edge. Here’s what the data suggests:

  • Google Maps is a go-to resource: Many locals rely on Google Maps to find businesses nearby, especially when searching for restaurants, shops, and services. Ensuring your location is accurately marked can help drive foot traffic.
  • Mobile searches are prevalent: With a significant number of searches conducted on mobile devices, it’s imperative to optimize your profile for mobile viewing. This includes having clear images and easy navigation.
  • Utilize specific keywords: Keywords like “best pizza in Shakopee” or “local plumbing services” are frequently used by locals. Incorporate these phrases into your business description and posts to improve discoverability.
  • Stay relevant with community events: Shakopee is home to various events and seasonal activities that can influence searches. For example, if you're participating in the annual Shakopee River City Days, update your profile with relevant information and promotions to attract event-goers.
