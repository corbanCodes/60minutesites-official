Why Google Business Profile Matters

A Google Business Profile is essential for local visibility, helping you to stand out in searches that matter most to your community. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to set it up effectively:

  • Sign in to Google My Business: Head over to the Google My Business website and click 'Start Now.' If you already have a Google account, you can use that to log in, but if not, creating one is straightforward.
  • Enter Your Business Name and Address: Ensure that the name you provide matches your business signage. Enter your address accurately to help customers find you easily. If you're a service-area business, you can specify the areas you serve.
  • Select the Appropriate Business Category: Choose a primary category that best represents your business. For instance, if you run a coffee shop, selecting 'Cafe' will help Google understand your offerings and show your business to relevant searches.
  • Add Your Phone Number and Website Link: Make it easy for potential customers to reach you. Include a phone number that you answer regularly and link to your business website or a dedicated landing page, if available.
  • Verify Your Business: Verification is crucial for credibility. You may receive a postcard from Google with a verification code, or you can verify via phone or email, depending on your business type.
  • Complete Your Profile: Enhance your profile with high-quality photos that showcase your products or services, and don't forget to include your business hours and a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. Consider mentioning local landmarks or events in your description to resonate with the Kearns community.

Completing these steps ensures that your business not only appears in local search results but also provides potential customers with the information they need to choose you over competitors.

How Locals in Kearns Metroship Search for Businesses

Understanding how people in Kearns search for businesses can give you a significant edge. Locals often rely on Google searches while looking for:

  • Restaurants and Cafes: Many residents frequent spots near the Kennecott Nature Center, especially during weekends. If you’re in the food industry, consider optimizing your profile with menu items or special promotions to attract these customers.
  • Retail Shops: The Kearns Shopping Center is a popular destination for locals. Ensure your profile is updated with your latest offerings, seasonal sales, or community events that might draw in foot traffic.
  • Home Services: Residents often search for reliable plumbing and electrical services. Providing detailed descriptions of your services, along with positive customer reviews, can set you apart from the competition.
  • Health and Wellness Options: As Kearns values a healthy lifestyle, businesses like gyms, yoga studios, or health clinics should highlight any unique classes or services available, as well as any community involvement.

By optimizing your Google Business Profile with relevant keywords and accurate information, you can ensure that your business pops up when locals are searching. Think about the specific terms they might use and incorporate those into your business description, posts, and even responses to reviews.
