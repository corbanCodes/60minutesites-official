How Do I Get More Customers in Cleveland?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Cleveland is a vibrant city located on the southern shore of Lake Erie, known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse economy. With a population of over 380,000, Cleveland offers a wealth of opportunities for small businesses to thrive. If you're looking to attract more customers, you need to leverage both online and offline strategies that resonate with the local community, which is characterized by its strong support for local brands and businesses.

Building a Strong Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront, and it needs to shine. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a user-friendly website that reflects your brand and includes elements that resonate with local culture, such as references to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame or the Cleveland Museum of Art.
  • Optimize your site for local SEO to appear in search results when people look for services in Cleveland, considering keywords related to local landmarks or events.
  • Include clear calls to action, making it easy for visitors to contact you or book your services.
  • Regularly update your content and blog about local events or tips related to your business, such as the annual Cleveland International Film Festival or local sports events involving the Browns, Cavaliers, or Guardians.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly since many customers will be searching on their phones, especially during events like the Cleveland Air Show or local festivals.

Harnessing the Power of Google and Reviews

Google is where most customers start their search. You want to be found. Here’s how:

  • Claim your Google My Business listing and keep it updated with your hours, services, and photos, particularly showcasing your location in relation to well-known Cleveland sites like Playhouse Square or the West Side Market.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp. Positive reviews can significantly influence potential customers, particularly in a community that values local opinion.
  • Respond to all reviews, both good and bad, to show that you value customer feedback and care about service, emphasizing your commitment to the Cleveland community.

Maximizing Referrals and Networking

Referrals are one of the most powerful ways to gain customers. Here’s how to tap into that:

  • Build relationships with other local businesses that share your target market; think about cross-promotions with businesses in neighborhoods like Tremont or Ohio City, known for their vibrant local scenes.
  • Attend local networking events and community gatherings, such as the Cleveland Flea or local Chamber of Commerce events, to meet potential customers and collaborators.
  • Ask your satisfied customers for referrals, and consider implementing a referral program that rewards them, leveraging Cleveland’s strong community spirit.

Engaging on Social Media

Social media can be a game-changer for businesses in Cleveland. Here’s the real deal:

  • Identify which platforms your target audience uses most and focus your efforts there, particularly on Facebook and Instagram, which are popular among locals.
  • Share engaging content that showcases your personality and connects with the local community, such as posts about local artists or events at the Cleveland Cultural Gardens.
  • Use local hashtags to increase your visibility, connecting with the Cleveland community through tags like #ThisIsCleveland or #CLE.
  • Run targeted ads to reach specific demographics in Cleveland, focusing on local interests and events.

Running a local business in Cleveland is no small feat, but with the right strategies in place, you can attract more customers and grow your brand. Stay persistent, keep learning, and leverage your community connections. With Cleveland’s supportive atmosphere and vibrant culture, you've got this!
