Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward and can be done in just a few steps. Follow these actionable instructions to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the official website and click on 'Manage now' to begin the setup process.
  • Enter Your Business Information: Accurately input your business name and address. This is crucial for local searches; you want to ensure customers can easily find you, whether they’re near the Mountlake Terrace Transit Center or shopping at the local QFC.
  • Select the Right Category: Choose the correct business category that best describes what you offer. This helps Google display your business in relevant searches. For example, if you run a café, select 'Café' or 'Coffee Shop.'
  • Fill Out Contact Information: Include a phone number and a link to your website. This allows potential customers to reach out or browse your offerings before they visit.
  • Verify Your Business: Verification methods include receiving a postcard from Google or a phone call. Follow the instructions to confirm your business location and ensure you show up in local listings.
  • Add Visual Appeal: Once verified, upload high-quality photos of your business, products, or services. A snapshot of your inviting interior or delicious meals can entice customers to choose you over others. Don’t forget to update your hours of operation to reflect any changes, especially during holidays or special events.
