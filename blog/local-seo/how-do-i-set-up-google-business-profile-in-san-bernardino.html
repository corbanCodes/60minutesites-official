Creating a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to making your business stand out: a complete and optimized Google Business Profile. Trust me on this one, it's easier than it sounds! Here’s what to focus on:

  • Claim your listing: Make sure you officially claim your business to have control over its information.
  • Fill in all details: Add your business name, address, phone number, and website. Ensure that your address is accurate, especially since San Bernardino has various neighborhoods, like Delmann Heights and Muscoy.
  • Choose the right categories: Pick categories that accurately describe your business, such as 'Mexican Restaurant' or 'Auto Repair Service,' which are popular searches among locals.
  • Add photos: High-quality images of your products, services, and storefront engage customers. Consider including images of your business in the context of local landmarks to attract locals.
  • Write a compelling description: Tell your story and what makes your business unique. Highlight any community involvement or local partnerships, as San Bernardino residents appreciate businesses that give back to the community.
  • Keep information up-to-date: Regularly update your hours, services, and promotions, especially during holidays or events like the San Bernardino County Fair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to set up my Google Business Profile?

Q: Can I edit my profile later?

Absolutely! You can update your profile anytime to reflect changes in your business, such as new services or extended hours during the holiday season.

Yes! It's completely free, which is fantastic for small businesses looking to grow in a competitive market like San Bernardino.
