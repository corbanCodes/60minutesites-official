Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is not just a nice-to-have; it’s an essential step for businesses in South Jordan. Here’s why you shouldn’t overlook this opportunity to connect with potential customers:

  • Improves visibility in local searches: When locals search for services, your business appears prominently in Google search results and Google Maps, making it easier for them to find you.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile displays crucial details such as business hours, location, and contact information, ensuring that customers have everything they need at their fingertips.
  • Engages with customer reviews: Customer feedback is invaluable. A Google Business Profile lets you respond to reviews and questions, fostering trust and building relationships with your clientele.
  • Offers insights: You’ll gain access to analytics that reveal how customers find your listing, what actions they take, and how you can refine your approach to attract even more visitors.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Establishing your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process. Follow these detailed steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Navigate to the site and sign in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, it’s easy to create a new account.
  • Click on 'Manage now': Begin by entering your business name. Make sure it matches your signage and branding for consistency.
  • Fill out your business details: Include your complete address, phone number, and select the category that best fits your business. If you run a coffee shop, for example, choose ‘Coffee Shop’ to ensure you appear in relevant searches.
  • Verify your business: Google will require verification. You can opt for a postcard sent to your business address, a phone call, or an email. This step is crucial, as it confirms your business's legitimacy.
  • Complete your profile: Add high-quality photos of your products or services, your business hours, and a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. A welcoming image of your coffee shop’s interior can entice customers to visit.
  • Regularly update your profile: Keep your profile fresh by posting updates, promotions, and new photos. For instance, if you introduce a new seasonal drink, share it on your profile to attract attention.

How Locals in South Jordan Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals in South Jordan search for businesses can provide you with an invaluable edge. Here are some insights to consider:

  • Google Maps is a go-to: Many residents turn to Google Maps when looking for nearby services, especially for food, retail, and home services. Ensure your business is accurately listed on Maps for maximum visibility.
  • Search phrases matter: South Jordan locals often search with phrases like 'best coffee near me' or 'plumber in South Jordan.' Optimize your profile with relevant keywords to ensure you appear in these searches.
  • Community recommendations: Recommendations from neighbors on social media platforms like Facebook can greatly influence decisions. Engage with local groups and encourage satisfied customers to share their experiences.
  • Mobile-friendly is a must: With an increase in mobile searches, ensure that your website linked to your Google Business Profile is mobile-friendly. A smooth browsing experience can keep potential customers from clicking away.
