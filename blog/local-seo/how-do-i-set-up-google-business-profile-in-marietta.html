How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can significantly increase your visibility. Here’s how to do it:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now.' This will take you to the dashboard where you can start the setup process.
  • Sign in with a Google account or create one if you don’t have it yet. Having a dedicated account for your business can help you manage your profile more effectively.
  • Enter your business name and address. Make sure to use the exact address so locals can find you easily. This is particularly important if you’re located near popular landmarks like the Marietta Cobb Museum of Art or the Big Chicken.
  • Choose the appropriate business category to help potential customers find you based on their search. For example, if you run a bakery, select 'Bakery' rather than just 'Food' to ensure you reach the right audience.
  • Add your phone number and website URL for easy contact. Consider using a local area code to foster a sense of community and trust.
  • Verify your business through the mailing process, phone call, or email, depending on what Google offers. This step is crucial, as it confirms that you are the legitimate owner of the business and allows you to access all features of the profile.
  • Once verified, enhance your profile with photos, business hours, and a detailed description. Include pictures of your products, your storefront, and even behind-the-scenes shots to give potential customers a feel for your business.

How Locals in Marietta Search for Businesses

Understanding how Marietta residents search for businesses can help you tailor your Google Business Profile for better results. Here are some insights:

  • Many locals look for nearby businesses using phrases like 'best coffee near me' or 'restaurants in downtown Marietta.' Consider using these phrases in your profile description or updates to help improve your visibility.
  • Seasonal events, like the Marietta Cobb Museum of Art exhibitions or the annual Marietta Streetfest, often prompt searches for related services, so keep your profile updated with relevant content. If you offer seasonal promotions or specials, be sure to highlight them to attract event-goers.
  • Community engagement is key; locals often turn to reviews and recommendations from friends or on social media, so encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback on your profile. You might even consider creating a small incentive for reviews, such as a discount on their next purchase.
  • Mobile searches are prevalent, especially when residents are on the go or exploring Marietta Square, so ensure your profile is optimized for mobile users. This means having clear contact information and up-to-date hours, as well as making it easy for users to find directions to your location.
