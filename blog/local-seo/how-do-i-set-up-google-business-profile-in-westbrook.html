Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile is like a digital storefront, helping potential customers find you when they search online. In a vibrant community like Westbrook, where local businesses thrive, having an optimized profile is crucial. Here are some key reasons to set up and optimize your profile:

  • Boosts visibility in local searches: When someone searches for services in Westbrook, a well-optimized profile increases your chances of appearing in the local pack, which is the list of businesses that shows up in the map section of Google search results.
  • Gives customers up-to-date information: Your profile provides essential details such as business hours, contact information, and location. This is particularly important in a close-knit community where people appreciate knowing when they can drop by.
  • Showcases photos, reviews, and services: High-quality images of your products or services make your business more appealing. Moreover, positive customer reviews can enhance your credibility and encourage new customers to visit.
  • Helps you stand out from competitors: Westbrook has a variety of businesses competing for attention. A well-crafted profile can highlight what makes your business unique, attracting more customers to your door.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Ready to get started? Follow these steps to set up your Google Business Profile:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile page and click 'Manage now.' This will direct you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure the address is precise, especially in a tight-knit community like Westbrook where local landmarks can help customers find you. For instance, if you are near the Westbrook Community Center, mention that in your description.
  • Select the appropriate category: Choose a category that best describes your business. For example, if you run a coffee shop, select 'Coffee Shop' to help Google understand what services you offer.
  • Add your phone number and website: Include your contact details prominently. If you don’t have a website, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and professionally, ensuring you have an online presence that matches your business ethos.
  • Verify your business: Google may send a postcard to your business address with a verification code. This step is crucial as it confirms your business location and authenticity.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, take the time to fill out your profile thoroughly. Include accurate business hours, a compelling description that reflects your brand, and high-quality photos that showcase your offerings. Engage a professional photographer if necessary to capture the essence of your business.

How Locals in Westbrook Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents in Westbrook search for businesses can give you an edge. Here’s what you should know:

  • Google searches for quick access: Many locals rely on Google searches to find services. This means your profile needs to be appealing and informative to catch their attention.
  • Common search phrases: Residents often use phrases like 'best restaurants in Westbrook' or 'local shops near me.' Think about incorporating these keywords into your business description and posts.
  • Word of mouth: Even in the digital age, recommendations from friends and family in Westbrook carry weight. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on your profile, as these can significantly influence new customers.
  • Local landmarks and events: Seasonal events, like the Westbrook Riverfest, and local landmarks, such as the Presumpscot River, can influence searches. Consider creating content or promotions that tie into these events to attract more visitors.
