Getting Started with Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process, but there are a few essential steps to ensure you do it right. Here’s how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Head over to the Google Business Profile page and sign in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, creating a Google account is quick and easy.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be sure to include your full business name and a precise address. If you're located near well-known Johnston landmarks, such as the Johnston Commons or the scenic Beaver Creek Park, mention these to help with local SEO.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best represents your services. This helps Google show your business to the right audience. For instance, if you run a local café, select 'Café' or 'Coffee Shop' to increase your chances of being found by coffee lovers in the area.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL: A phone number is essential for customer inquiries. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites, which can help you build a professional-looking website quickly and efficiently, tailored to the needs of your business.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a verification code to your listed address via postcard. This step is crucial as it confirms your business location and helps boost your profile's authenticity.
  • Upload high-quality photos: Attractive visuals can significantly enhance your profile. Include images of your storefront, products, and even happy customers (with their permission). A well-lit, inviting photo of your café’s interior can entice potential visitors.

Optimizing Your Profile

Once your Google Business Profile is set up, you'll want to optimize it for better visibility and engagement. Here are some actionable tips:

  • Keep your business information up to date: Regularly check your profile to ensure your hours of operation, services offered, and contact information are accurate. Seasonal changes, special holiday hours, or new service offerings should be updated promptly to avoid customer confusion.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews: Positive reviews can significantly impact your business’s credibility. After a purchase or service, kindly ask your customers to leave feedback. Responding to reviews, whether positive or negative, shows that you value customer input and can enhance your profile’s trustworthiness.
  • Post updates about special offers, events, or new products: Keeping your audience informed about promotions or events can drive traffic to your business. For instance, if you’re hosting a community event at your café, share this news on your profile to attract local patrons.
  • Utilize the Q&A feature: This feature allows potential customers to ask questions directly on your profile. Monitor this section regularly and provide prompt answers to common queries about your business, which can enhance customer satisfaction and reduce confusion.

How Locals in Johnston Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Johnston search for local businesses can give you a leg up. Here are some insights that will help you tailor your Google Business Profile effectively:

  • Google Maps is a go-to resource: Many locals depend on Google Maps to find nearby services, especially when searching for eateries or shops. Ensure your business is listed correctly and appears on the map.
  • Keyword relevance: Locals often use specific keywords related to Johnston, such as 'family-friendly restaurants near Johnston Town Center' or 'best coffee shops by Beaver Creek'. Incorporating these keywords into your profile can enhance searchability.
  • Word-of-mouth is powerful: The Johnston community thrives on personal recommendations. Maintaining a robust online presence through your Google Business Profile can amplify these conversations and encourage more locals to talk about your business positively.
  • Mobile optimization is crucial: Given the rising trend of mobile searches, ensure that your profile is optimized for mobile viewing. This means your images should load quickly, and your information should be easy to navigate on smartphones, as many residents will search for businesses on the go.
