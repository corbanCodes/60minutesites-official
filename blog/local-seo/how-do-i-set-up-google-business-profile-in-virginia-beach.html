What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating a strong Google Business Profile is straightforward if you know what to focus on. Here’s how to make yours shine:

  • Claim your business: Start by verifying your business ownership on Google. This is essential for establishing your presence in a competitive market like Virginia Beach.
  • Keep your information up to date: Ensure your address, phone number, and hours of operation are accurate. In a city with a high tourist turnover, updating seasonal hours can attract summer visitors looking for local services.
  • Add high-quality photos: Pictures of your products, services, and location help attract more customers. For Virginia Beach, consider including shots of your business with the ocean or local attractions in the background to appeal to both locals and tourists.
  • Write a compelling business description: Clearly explain what you offer, emphasizing unique aspects that make your business suitable for the vibrant local culture.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Positive reviews boost your visibility and credibility. In a community-focused area like Virginia Beach, word-of-mouth and online reviews are critical.
  • Utilize posts: Share updates, offers, or events to keep your audience engaged and informed. Highlighting local events or collaborations can resonate well with the community.

How Virginia Beach Locals Search

In Virginia Beach, locals love supporting businesses that are part of their community. Here’s the secret to understanding how they search:

  • When residents are looking for a service, they often turn to Google first. They might search for 'best coffee shop near me' or 'top-rated plumbers in Virginia Beach.' Given the city's popularity as a vacation destination, many searches are also aimed at finding services that cater to tourists.
  • Word-of-mouth is still strong here; if someone has a great experience at a local business, they’ll tell their friends and family, who then may search online to find that same business. This is especially true in neighborhoods like Hilltop and Croatan, where community ties run deep.
  • Community events and local social media groups play a big role in discovery. When you show up in local conversations, that can lead to more online searches for your business. Participating in events like the Neptune Festival can help increase visibility.
  • The best part is that once locals find you, they’re likely to return and recommend you to others, creating a vibrant cycle of support. The strong sense of community in Virginia Beach encourages repeat business and referrals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

You can claim your profile by visiting google.com/business and following the prompts to verify your business details.

Q: Can I update my business hours?

Yes, you can update your hours anytime through your Google Business Profile dashboard, which is crucial for accommodating seasonal tourism.

Q: How important are customer reviews?

Customer reviews are crucial as they enhance your credibility and improve your visibility in search results, especially in a community that values local recommendations.

Q: Do I need to pay for a Google Business Profile?

No, it's completely free to create and manage your Google Business Profile, making it an accessible marketing tool for small businesses in Virginia Beach.
