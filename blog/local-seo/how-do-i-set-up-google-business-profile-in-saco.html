How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile isn't just simple; it's an essential step in connecting with your community. Here's how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Head over to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Manage Now.' This is your gateway to managing your business's online presence.
  • Sign in or create an account: If you already have a Google account, simply sign in. If not, setting one up is quick and straightforward. A Google Account will not only help with your business profile but also with other Google services.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key here. Ensure your address is precise, especially if you're located near notable landmarks such as the Saco River, the scenic Saco Bay, or the beloved Saco Museum. This helps Google associate your business with local searches.
  • Select your business category: Choose a category that best describes your business. This will help Google connect you with potential customers searching for your type of service—whether it's a cozy café, a boutique, or a service provider.
  • Add your phone number and website: Make it easy for customers to reach you. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites, which specializes in helping local businesses create professional websites quickly and effectively.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a verification code to your business address or give you the option to verify by phone. This step is crucial to ensure that you are the rightful owner of the business.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, take the time to fill out your profile with comprehensive details—hours of operation, quality photos of your business, and a list of services or products offered. Don’t forget to highlight any specialties or unique offerings that set you apart in Saco.

How Locals in Saco Search for Businesses

Understanding the search habits of locals can provide a significant advantage for your business. In Saco, residents and visitors often seek:

  • Businesses with a strong local presence: Many locals prefer supporting businesses that are part of their community. Establish your presence by participating in local events or collaborating with nearby businesses, especially those along Saco Main Street or around popular attractions like the Saco Riverwalk.
  • Positive reviews and ratings: Online reputation matters immensely. Encourage your satisfied customers to leave glowing reviews. You can even create a small incentive, like a discount on their next purchase, for customers who take the time to review your business.
  • Detailed information: Thorough information is essential—especially for those visiting Saco for events or attractions such as Funtown Splashtown USA. Clearly state your operating hours, and consider including holiday hours or special events. Make sure to keep this information updated.
  • Engaging photos: Visual content is powerful. Invest some time in capturing high-quality images that represent your products, services, and the ambiance of your business. Consider including images of your team, your workspace, or even your customers enjoying your offerings. This creates a personal connection with potential clients.

By aligning your Google Business Profile with these local search behaviors, you can significantly boost your chances of attracting more customers in Saco. Remember, the goal is to make it easy for locals to find and choose you over competitors.
