How Do I Get More Customers in Eugene?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Eugene, Oregon, is all about standing out in a vibrant local market. Known for its rich cultural scene, outdoor recreational opportunities, and commitment to sustainability, Eugene provides a unique backdrop for businesses. With a thriving community that values unique services, you have plenty of opportunities to connect with potential clients.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront, and it needs to impress. Here's what actually works:

  • Ensure it’s easy to navigate, reflecting the artistic spirit of Eugene.
  • Optimize for mobile users; many are on their phones while enjoying the local parks or coffee shops.
  • Include clear calls to action (e.g., 'Contact Us', 'Get a Quote') that resonate with Eugene's friendly vibe.
  • Showcase your services and any special offers, particularly those that align with the local culture, like eco-friendly options.
  • Add customer testimonials to build trust, highlighting success stories from fellow Eugene residents.

Utilize Google My Business

Claiming and optimizing your Google My Business listing is crucial. Here’s how:

  • Make sure your contact info is up to date, including a local phone number and address in the heart of Eugene.
  • Use high-quality images of your work or products, perhaps featuring iconic local landmarks like Skinner Butte or the University of Oregon campus.
  • Post regular updates and special promotions that align with local events, such as the Oregon Country Fair or local farmers' markets.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews; they can boost your visibility and help you stand out in Eugene's competitive market.

Leverage Customer Reviews and Referrals

I've seen this work time and time again; word-of-mouth is powerful:

  • Always ask for reviews after a job well done, especially from clients who appreciate Eugene's community-focused values.
  • Offer incentives for referrals; a small discount can go a long way in a city where community connections matter.
  • Feature positive reviews prominently on your website and social media, telling stories that resonate with Eugene's community spirit.

Engage on Social Media

Social media can be your best friend if used correctly:

  • Choose platforms where your audience hangs out; Facebook and Instagram are great for visual content, particularly to showcase Eugene’s local art and nature.
  • Post regularly, sharing behind-the-scenes content, promotions, or customer stories that highlight your connection to the Eugene community.
  • Engage with your followers through comments and messages; building that community ties back to Eugene's strong sense of local identity.

Network Locally

Don’t underestimate the power of networking in a city like Eugene:

  • Attend local business events and workshops, such as those hosted by the Eugene Chamber of Commerce, to meet other entrepreneurs.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business groups that focus on collaboration and community support.
  • Partner with other local businesses to cross-promote your services, tapping into Eugene's network of supportive, community-oriented entrepreneurs.

Remember, the hustle of running a local business is real, but with the right strategies, you can attract more customers and thrive in Eugene. Stay persistent and keep experimenting with what resonates with your audience!
