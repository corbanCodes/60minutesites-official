Why a Google Business Profile is Essential for Your Ardmore Business

Setting up a Google Business Profile is one of the most effective ways to enhance your online presence and connect with your community. Here’s how to ensure your profile shines and attracts local customers:

  • Claim your business: Start by verifying your business on Google. This adds credibility and allows you to manage your information effectively. To claim your business, visit the Google My Business website, sign in with your Google account, and follow the prompts. This process typically involves receiving a verification code by mail, phone, or email, ensuring that you are the rightful owner of the business.
  • Complete all information: Fill out every section of your profile meticulously, including your address, phone number, business hours, and website. The more complete your profile, the better it performs in searches. Don’t forget to specify if you offer delivery or curbside pickup, as this can make a big difference to your potential customers.
  • Add photos: High-quality images of your products, services, or storefront can make a significant impact. People love visuals, and a vibrant image can entice them to choose your business over others. Consider hiring a local photographer or using a good camera to capture the essence of your business, whether it’s a steaming cup of coffee or a beautifully arranged storefront.
  • Encourage reviews: Positive reviews can boost your reputation and attract new customers. Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on your profile, and make it easy by providing links. Respond to reviews, both positive and critical, as it shows that you value customer feedback and are engaged with your clientele. Building rapport in this way can foster trust and loyalty.
  • Utilize posts: Share regular updates about your business, including special offers, new products, or events. These posts can keep your customers informed and engaged. For instance, if you're hosting a local art show or offering a limited-time discount, share these details through your profile's post feature to generate excitement.

How Locals in Ardmore Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search can help you tailor your Google Business Profile to meet their needs effectively. Here are some valuable insights:

  • Use of local landmarks: Ardmore residents often include local landmarks or neighborhoods when searching for services. For example, they might search for "best pizza near the Ardmore Public Library" or "coffee shops close to the Ardmore Music Hall." By mentioning these local landmarks in your business description and posts, you can improve your visibility in these specific searches.
  • Mobile searches: Many people use their smartphones while on the go to find nearby services. Ensure your business information is accurate, up-to-date, and mobile-friendly. This includes having a responsive website that loads quickly on mobile devices, as well as clear directions to your location.
  • Community-focused terms: Residents may search for businesses that emphasize community involvement, like "family-owned businesses in Ardmore" or "local artisans near me." Highlighting your community ties and involvement in local events can help draw in those searching with these terms. Consider collaborating with other local businesses for joint promotions or community events.
  • Event-driven searches: During local events or festivals, searches may spike for nearby services or products. Participate in Ardmore's seasonal events, like the Ardmore Festival of the Arts, and make sure your profile reflects any special offerings or extended hours during these times. This proactive approach can capture additional traffic and showcase your business as an integral part of the community.
