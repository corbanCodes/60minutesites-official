How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is simpler than you might think. Follow these steps to ensure you’re making a powerful first impression:

  • Create or claim your business listing on Google: Visit Google My Business and either create a new profile or claim an existing one. If you’re starting fresh, ensure that your business name matches what customers will be searching for.
  • Fill in all the essential information: Accurately enter your name, address, phone number, and website URL. Make sure your address reflects your location in San Gabriel, especially if you’re near popular spots like the San Gabriel Mission or the shopping district on Valley Boulevard.
  • Add high-quality photos: Showcase your business through vibrant photos. Include images of your storefront, interior, team members, and products or services. This not only makes your profile visually appealing but also helps potential customers feel more connected to your business.
  • Write a clear and concise description: Highlight what makes your business unique and describe your services. For example, if you run a local bakery, mention your specialty pastries or any traditional recipes that resonate with the community.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews: Reviews are crucial for building trust. Prompt happy customers to share their experiences and make it a habit to respond to all reviews, whether positive or constructive. This engagement shows that you value feedback and care about your customers.
  • Keep your hours of operation updated: Ensure that your hours reflect any seasonal changes or special events, especially during local festivals or holidays when foot traffic may increase. San Gabriel is known for its Lunar New Year celebrations, so consider adjusting your hours to accommodate shoppers.
  • Utilize posts: Regularly share updates, promotions, or events that are relevant to your business. For instance, if you’re hosting a tasting event or a workshop, create a post to attract attendees and encourage them to engage with your brand.

How Locals in San Gabriel Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents search for businesses in San Gabriel is key to tailoring your Google Business Profile. Here’s what you should know:

  • Search phrases matter: Many locals search using phrases like “best tacos in San Gabriel” or “nearby coffee shops.” Incorporate these keywords into your business description and posts to enhance your visibility.
  • Google Maps is widely used: Residents rely heavily on Google Maps to find businesses close to them. Ensure your location data is accurate and consider using Google Street View to give potential customers a virtual tour of your vicinity.
  • Word of mouth is powerful: Recommendations shared on social media can significantly boost your visibility. Engage with local influencers or community groups to promote your business and share your story.
  • Seasonal needs drive searches: Be proactive in promoting timely offers relevant to local events or holidays, such as back-to-school specials or summer sales. This can attract more customers looking for specific products or services.
  • Mobile searches dominate: Most searches are conducted on mobile devices. Make sure your contact information is accessible and your profile loads quickly on smartphones. Consider linking to a mobile-friendly website that offers a seamless experience.
