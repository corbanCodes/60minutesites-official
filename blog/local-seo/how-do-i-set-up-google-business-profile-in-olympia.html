Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is easier than you might think. Follow these steps to ensure your business is set up for success:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile page: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile page. Sign in with your Google account, or create one if you don’t have it yet. This account will be crucial for managing your business information.
  • Click on 'Manage now': Once logged in, click on 'Manage now' to initiate the setup process. You’ll be prompted to enter your business name, so take a moment to ensure it’s spelled correctly and matches your branding.
  • Select or add your business: If your business appears in the dropdown menu, select it. If not, click on 'Add your business.' Make sure to include any relevant business variations (like abbreviations) that customers might use when searching.
  • Choose the appropriate category: Selecting the right category is crucial for visibility. Think about your main offerings—are you a 'family-friendly restaurant' or a 'boutique coffee shop'? This helps Google connect you with potential customers searching for specific services.
  • Fill out essential information: Provide your business address, phone number, and website URL. Make sure your address is accurate, as this will help customers find your location easily, especially if you're near popular Olympia landmarks like the Hands On Children's Museum.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business, which can be done via phone, email, or postcard. Choose the method that’s most convenient for you, and follow the instructions carefully to complete this step.
  • Add photos and a compelling business description: Once verified, make your profile more attractive by uploading high-quality photos of your business, products, or services. Write a detailed business description that highlights what makes you unique. This is your chance to tell the story of your business and what it offers to the Olympia community.

How Locals in Olympia Search for Businesses

Understanding how Olympia residents search for businesses can give you a leg up. Here are some key insights into local search behavior:

  • Mobile searches are common: Many locals prefer to look for businesses on their phones while exploring downtown or visiting popular spots like the Washington State Capitol. This means your profile needs to be mobile-friendly and easily accessible.
  • Location-specific search terms: Search terms often include specific locations, like 'coffee shop near Percival Landing' or 'best taco truck in West Olympia.' Think about how customers might phrase their searches and incorporate those keywords into your profile.
  • The importance of reviews: Reviews and ratings play a crucial role; locals trust businesses with strong, positive feedback. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on your Google Business Profile to build credibility and attract more foot traffic.
  • Highlight unique experiences: Using phrases like 'family-friendly' or 'outdoor seating' can help capture the attention of those looking for specific experiences. If your establishment has unique offerings, like live music events or pet-friendly patios, make sure to mention those in your description.
