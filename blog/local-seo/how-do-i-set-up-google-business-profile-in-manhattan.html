Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential because it allows your business to show up in local searches and on Google Maps, which is where many people start their search for services. Here are some key benefits:

  • Increased visibility: Your business appears in local search results when people are looking for what you offer. For example, if a resident types in 'best pizza in Manhattan, KS,' your profile can pop up, showcasing your menu and location.
  • Customer engagement: You can respond to reviews, answer questions, and post updates that keep your audience engaged. This interaction not only builds trust but can also encourage more customers to choose your business over competitors.
  • Insights: Google provides valuable data on how customers find your profile and what actions they take, such as calling your number or visiting your website. This information allows you to tailor your marketing strategies effectively.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit Google Business Profile: Go to the Google Business Profile page and click 'Manage now.' This will lead you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: If you don’t have a physical storefront, select the service area option. Make sure your address is accurate; this helps customers find you easily.
  • Choose the relevant category: Selecting the right category for your business helps Google understand what you do. For instance, if you run a bakery, choose 'Bakery' or 'Café' to attract the right audience.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: Ensure these details are correct, as they are crucial for potential customers trying to reach you.
  • Verify your business: You will need to verify your business through the mail or phone call method as prompted. This step is important to confirm your ownership and enhance your profile’s credibility.
  • Optimize your profile: Add high-quality photos of your business, products, or services, as well as your business hours and a description that highlights what makes your business unique. For example, if you offer locally sourced ingredients or a family recipe, be sure to mention that!

How Locals in Manhattan Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Manhattan search can help you tailor your profile effectively. Here’s what you should know:

  • Local keywords: Residents often search for specific terms like 'best coffee in Manhattan, KS' or 'affordable plumbing services near me.' Incorporating these keywords into your business description can enhance your visibility in searches.
  • Community engagement: Locals value businesses that participate in community events or support local causes. For example, if you sponsor a local sports team or participate in the annual Little Apple Festival, highlight these activities in your profile. This connection fosters loyalty and trust with your customers.
  • Mobile searches: Many users are searching on mobile devices while on the go. Ensure your profile is optimized for mobile viewing, which means keeping your information concise and easy to read on smaller screens.
