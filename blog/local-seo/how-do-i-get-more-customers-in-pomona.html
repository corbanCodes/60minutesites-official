How Do I Get More Customers in Pomona?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you're a small business in Pomona, California, you know how important it is to stand out and attract customers. Nestled in the heart of Los Angeles County, Pomona is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and community-oriented spirit. With the right mix of online and offline strategies, you can elevate your business and thrive in this vibrant community that boasts attractions like the Fairplex, home of the LA County Fair, and the Pomona Arts Colony, a hub for local artists and creatives.

Build a Professional Website

Your website is the foundation for getting customers online. It should be user-friendly and mobile-optimized. Here’s what actually works:

  • Clearly outline your services and pricing, tailored to the needs of Pomona residents and businesses.
  • Use high-quality images that showcase your work in relevant local settings.
  • Include a blog to share tips and updates related to your industry, especially those that resonate with the Pomona community.
  • Make sure your contact information is easy to find, including local phone numbers and addresses.
  • Optimize for local SEO to improve visibility in search engines, focusing on keywords relevant to Pomona.

Leverage Google My Business

Claiming and optimizing your Google My Business listing can significantly boost your local presence. The truth is, many customers search for services right on Google. Here’s how to make the most of it:

  • Keep your business information up-to-date, including hours of operation and service offerings that cater specifically to Pomona residents.
  • Add photos to make your listing visually appealing, highlighting local landmarks or events.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, particularly those who are part of Pomona’s close-knit community.
  • Respond to reviews, both positive and negative, to show you care and are engaged with your customer base.

Harness the Power of Reviews

Online reviews can make or break a local business. Encourage your customers to share their experiences. Time and again, I've seen businesses thrive with a solid review strategy:

  • Ask for reviews after every job or service, especially after participating in local events or fairs.
  • Share positive testimonials on your website and social media to showcase your reputation within the Pomona community.
  • Offer incentives for customers who leave reviews, like discounts on future services or exclusive promotions.

Tap Into Referrals and Networking

Word-of-mouth is still one of the most powerful marketing tools. Build relationships in your community:

  • Attend local networking events and mixers, such as those hosted by the Pomona Chamber of Commerce.
  • Partner with complementary businesses for cross-promotions, especially those located near popular spots like the Pomona Fairplex or the Downtown Pomona Historic District.
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers who bring in new clients, utilizing the communal spirit of Pomona.

Engage on Social Media

Social media is a great way to connect with your audience and showcase your brand. Here’s what I’ve seen work:

  • Post regularly and engage with followers, sharing local news and events that relate to your business.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content to build trust, highlighting your connection to the Pomona community.
  • Use local hashtags to reach your Pomona audience, such as #PomonaCA and #PomonaSmallBiz.
  • Run contests or giveaways to increase your following, encouraging community participation.

Running a local business in Pomona is no small feat, but with the right strategies, you can attract more customers and grow. Stay persistent, build your network, and don’t be afraid to innovate! Embrace the unique characteristics of Pomona, and let them guide your business approach.
