How Do I Get More Customers in Gilbert?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Gilbert, Arizona can feel challenging, but with the right strategies, you can make a real impact. The community is supportive, and there are plenty of opportunities if you know where to look. Known for its family-friendly atmosphere and outdoor activities, Gilbert is one of the fastest-growing towns in the U.S., with a population exceeding 260,000 as of 2023.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. Here’s what actually works:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly and fast to cater to the high percentage of mobile users in Gilbert.
  • Optimize for local SEO by using keywords like 'Gilbert [your service]' throughout your site, focusing on local attractions and events.
  • Add clear calls-to-action (CTAs) directing visitors on what to do next, such as signing up for a community event or newsletter.
  • Include a blog to share valuable content related to your services, local news, or tips for residents, attracting more visitors.
  • Set up Google My Business to improve visibility in local searches, ensuring your business appears in the local three-pack and Google Maps.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews

Reviews can make or break your business in Gilbert. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google and Yelp, where local shoppers often seek recommendations.
  • Respond to reviews, both positive and negative, to show you care and are engaged with your customers.
  • Showcase testimonials on your website and social media to build trust, especially highlighting those from local residents.
  • Consider offering incentives for reviews – just ensure it’s compliant with platforms' guidelines and maintains authenticity.

Networking and Community Engagement

Building relationships is key in a tight-knit community like Gilbert. Here’s how to do it:

  • Attend local networking events and community fairs, such as the monthly Gilbert Farmers Market, to meet potential customers and other business owners.
  • Partner with complementary local businesses for cross-promotions, such as a local coffee shop collaborating with a bakery.
  • Join local business organizations or chambers of commerce to access support and resources tailored to Gilbert’s unique market.
  • Engage in community service or sponsor local events like the Gilbert Days Festival to increase your visibility and goodwill.

Leveraging Social Media

Social media is a powerful tool for reaching more customers. Here’s what you should focus on:

  • Choose platforms that fit your audience; Facebook and Instagram are particularly popular in Gilbert, where many residents share local experiences.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content, customer stories, and promotions to engage your audience, showcasing your business’s connection to the community.
  • Run targeted ads to reach specific demographics in Gilbert, utilizing Facebook’s geo-targeting tools to ensure your message resonates with local consumers.
  • Use local hashtags, such as #GilbertAZ or #GilbertLocal, to increase visibility among community members.

Getting customers in Gilbert is all about building relationships and establishing a strong online presence. Stay persistent, engage with the community, and leverage local events to watch your business thrive!
