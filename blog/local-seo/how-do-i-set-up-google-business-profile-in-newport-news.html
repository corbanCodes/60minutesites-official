What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret: an optimized Google Business Profile can significantly boost your visibility. To make it stand out in a competitive market like Newport News, focus on these key elements:

  • Complete your profile: Fill in your business name, address, phone number, and website. Ensure that your address reflects the distinct neighborhoods of Newport News, such as Downtown, the Historic District, or near the Newport News Shipbuilding.
  • Choose the right categories: Select categories that accurately describe your business. For example, if you're near the Virginia Living Museum or the Mariners' Museum, make sure to include relevant categories that attract tourists and locals alike.
  • Add photos: High-quality images of your products, services, or store help attract customers. Showcase your business's unique aspects, such as outdoor seating if you run a café or the ambiance of your retail space.
  • Update your hours: Make sure your hours of operation are accurate to avoid disappointing customers, especially during local events like the Newport News Fall Festival of Folklife.
  • Respond to reviews: Engage with customers by replying to their reviews, whether they're positive or negative. This interaction can significantly enhance your reputation in a community that values personal connections.

Building a good reputation in the community can drive more customers through your doors, especially as locals participate in community initiatives or events hosted at places like the Peninsula Fine Arts Center.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a Google Business Profile?

Go to the Google Business Profile website, sign in or create a Google account, and follow the prompts to set up your business. Make sure to include local details that resonate with Newport News residents.

Q: Can I manage my Google Business Profile from my phone?

Yes, there's a Google My Business app that lets you manage your profile on the go! This is especially handy during local events when business hours may change.

Q: How long does it take for my profile to appear in search results?

Once you've set it up and verified your business, it can appear in search results almost immediately. However, engaging with the community through events can enhance visibility and drive traffic to your profile.
