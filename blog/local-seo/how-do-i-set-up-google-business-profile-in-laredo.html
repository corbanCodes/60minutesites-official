Creating Your Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting started: It's all about providing accurate and detailed information. Follow these steps to set up your profile:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click 'Manage now'.
  • Enter your business name and address. Ensure it's consistent with how it appears on your storefront; for example, if you're located near the historic San Agustin Plaza, mention that in your description.
  • Select your business category. This helps Google understand what you do, such as 'Mexican Restaurant' or 'Retail Shop'.
  • Add your phone number and website link. This is crucial for reaching out to customers, especially those who are searching online while enjoying local attractions like the Laredo International Bridge.
  • Verify your business. Google will send a postcard to your address with a verification code. Trust me on this one, this step is essential!
  • Fill out your profile with photos of your business, products, and services. Great visuals can make a big difference, particularly in a city known for its vibrant festivals and community events.

Optimizing Your Profile for Maximum Visibility

Once your profile is set up, it's time to optimize it so you stand out in Laredo. Here’s how:

  • Keep your information up to date, especially your hours of operation. Consider local events like the Laredo National Fair and adjust your hours accordingly to capture the increased foot traffic.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews. Responding to reviews shows you care about your customers and builds trust. In Laredo, many locals value recommendations from friends and family.
  • Use Google Posts to share updates, promotions, or events. Highlighting local celebrations or special offers can keep your audience engaged and informed.
  • Utilize the Q&A section to answer common customer questions. This can save you time and help potential customers decide to choose you! Address common queries about parking availability near the Laredo Energy Arena, for example.
  • Add special attributes, like ‘Women-Led’ or ‘LGBTQ-Friendly’ if applicable. In a diverse city like Laredo, this can attract more customers who value these qualities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take for my profile to show up on Google?

Typically, it can take a few days after verification for your business to start appearing in search results.

Q: Can I manage my Google Business Profile from my phone?

Yes! Google has a mobile app that makes it easy to manage your profile on the go, perfect for busy Laredo business owners.

Q: What if I move my business?

You can update your address directly in your Google Business Profile to reflect the new location. If you’re moving to a more bustling area, like downtown Laredo, make sure to highlight the benefits of the new location.
