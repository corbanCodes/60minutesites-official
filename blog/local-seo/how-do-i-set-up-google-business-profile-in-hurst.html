Why a Google Business Profile is Essential

A Google Business Profile is more than just a listing; it’s your digital storefront. Here’s why you need one:

  • Increases visibility in local searches: When potential customers search for services or products you offer, a well-optimized profile ensures your business appears at the top of local search results.
  • Allows potential customers to find your location and contact information easily: Your profile provides crucial information like your address, phone number, and even directions, ensuring that customers can find you without hassle.
  • Enables customer reviews, helping establish trust: Positive reviews can significantly influence a customer's decision. Your profile collects customer feedback, showcasing your reputation and reliability.
  • Provides insights into how customers find your business online: Understanding how many people view your profile and what actions they take can help you refine your marketing strategies.
  • Offers a platform to post updates and special offers directly to your community: Engaging with your audience through posts about new products, services, or events keeps your business top-of-mind for locals.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your profile set up is straightforward. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click 'Manage now': This is your starting point. Make sure you’re logged into the Google account you want to associate with your business.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key. Ensure that your business name matches what you use elsewhere online, and that your address is precise to avoid confusion.
  • Select the appropriate category for your business: This helps Google match your business with relevant searches. For example, if you run a coffee shop, choose ‘Coffee Shop’ instead of a generic category.
  • Fill in your phone number and website: If you have a website, linking it enhances your credibility. A local number also helps customers feel connected.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your address, or you may have other options such as phone verification. This step is crucial for establishing your business’s legitimacy.
  • Once verified, complete your profile: Add high-quality photos of your business, detailed business hours, and a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique.

  • Community events and activities: Hurst is a community-oriented place, and locals often search for events at places like the Hurst Community Park. Keeping your profile updated with your participation in local activities can increase visibility and engagement.
