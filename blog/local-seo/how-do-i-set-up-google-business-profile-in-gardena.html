Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Your Google Business Profile is your online storefront. It's how potential customers find you when searching for services or products nearby. Here’s why it’s essential:

  • Increases local visibility in Google searches: When locals search for services in Gardena, your business can appear in local search results and Google Maps, making it easier for them to discover you.
  • Provides essential information: Customers can quickly find your hours, location, and contact details, which is especially important for those on-the-go looking for immediate services.
  • Allows you to share updates: Engage your audience by posting updates about your business, sharing photos of new products, or announcing special offers that attract customers.
  • Enables customer reviews: Positive reviews build your reputation and can influence potential customers' decisions, making it critical to encourage satisfied customers to share their experiences.

With Gardena being home to a diverse population and various local events, a Google Business Profile ensures that your business is front and center when potential customers are searching for what you offer.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your Google Business Profile set up is easier than you might think. Just follow these steps:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by navigating to the Google Business Profile homepage and click on ‘Manage now.’ This will set you on your way to enhancing your online presence.
  • Sign in with your Google account: If you don’t have one, creating a new Google account is quick and straightforward. This account will be essential for managing your profile.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be sure to select the correct category for your business, whether it’s a cozy café near the Gardena High School or a trusted auto repair shop in the downtown area.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL: If you already have a website, include the URL to direct customers to more information. If not, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create a professional website that complements your profile.
  • Verify your business: Google will typically send a verification postcard to your business address. This postcard will contain a code that you need to enter on your profile to confirm that you are the rightful owner of the business.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding business hours, high-quality photos, and a compelling description of your services or products. Consider highlighting any unique aspects of your business that cater to the Gardena community.

By taking these steps, you’re not just setting up a profile; you’re laying the groundwork for building connections with local customers.

How Locals in Gardena Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Gardena look for businesses can help you optimize your profile. Locals often search for:

  • Specific services: Think about the phrases people might type, such as ‘best tacos in Gardena’ or ‘affordable plumbing services near me.’ By including relevant keywords in your profile description and posts, you can improve your chances of showing up in those searches.
  • Business hours: Residents want to know if you’re open, especially when they are planning their visits to local favorites like the Gardena Willows Wetland Preserve or the Alondra Park.
  • Directions and contact information: Ensure your address and phone number are accurate so customers can easily find and reach you without hassle.
  • Reviews and ratings: Many locals turn to reviews to gauge the quality of service. Encourage your happy customers to leave reviews on your Google Business Profile to help boost your reputation and attract new clientele.

By tailoring your profile based on these search behaviors, you can attract more foot traffic and phone calls. Engaging with your audience through posts and responding to reviews will further enhance your visibility and connection to the Gardena community.
