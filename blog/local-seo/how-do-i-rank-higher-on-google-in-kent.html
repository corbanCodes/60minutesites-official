Tips for Boosting Your Local SEO in Kent

The good news is there are practical steps you can take to improve your local SEO right here in Kent:

  • Claim your Google My Business listing: Make sure your info is accurate, and add photos of your business. Highlight your proximity to local landmarks, such as the Kent Valley Ice Centre.
  • Use local keywords: Incorporate phrases like 'Kent coffee shop' or 'best pizza in Kent' on your website and in blog posts, and consider mentioning local events or attractions.
  • Get listed in local directories: Sites like Yelp and Yellow Pages can give you added visibility, especially if you're located near popular spots like the Green River Natural Resources Area.
  • Gather reviews: Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on Google and Yelp to boost your credibility. Consider setting up an incentivized review program to motivate customers.
  • Optimize for mobile: Ensure your website is mobile-friendly since a lot of local searches happen on smartphones. Given Kent's active lifestyle, many residents search for businesses while on the go.
