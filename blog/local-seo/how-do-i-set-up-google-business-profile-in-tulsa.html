Creating Your Google Business Profile

Getting started is pretty straightforward. Here’s what you need to do:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now'.
  • Sign in with your Google account or create a new one.
  • Enter your business name and address; being specific about your location in Tulsa can help locals find you better.
  • Choose your business category. This helps Google understand what you do; consider using local categories like 'Tulsa florist' or 'Downtown Tulsa café'.
  • Add your phone number and website (if you have one). A local area code can enhance trust among customers.
  • Verify your business through the postcard Google sends you or by phone.

The best part is, once your profile is verified, you can start optimizing it!

Optimizing Your Profile

Now that you've got your profile set up, let’s make it shine:

  • Fill out every section completely, especially your business hours and services. Tulsans often search for businesses open late or on weekends.
  • Use high-quality images of your products or your business space; consider showcasing local landmarks or events that resonate with the community. Pictures can speak volumes!
  • Write a compelling business description that includes keywords people might search for, like 'best barbecue in Tulsa' or 'affordable HVAC services Tulsa'.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews. Positive reviews can help you rank higher, especially in a city where locals trust neighbor recommendations.
  • Keep your information up-to-date. If you change your hours or move locations, make sure to update your profile right away.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to verify my Google Business Profile?

Usually, it takes about 1-2 weeks for Google to send you a verification postcard, but you can also verify by phone in some cases.

Q: Can I manage my Google Business Profile from my phone?

Absolutely! Google has a mobile app that makes it easy to manage your profile on the go, which is perfect for busy Tulsa entrepreneurs.

Q: What should I do if my business information changes?

Just log in to your Google Business Profile and update your information. Keeping it current is key, especially in a dynamic city like Tulsa where events and local happenings can affect your business hours.
