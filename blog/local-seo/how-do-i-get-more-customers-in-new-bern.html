How Do I Get More Customers in New Bern?

5 min read

As a business owner in New Bern, you're likely aware of the untapped potential that this charming coastal town holds. Nestled between the Neuse and Trent Rivers, New Bern offers a unique blend of history, culture, and community spirit. However, attracting local customers can sometimes feel like a daunting task. If you're looking to increase foot traffic and boost online engagement, I’m here to share some effective strategies that will not only help you reach more customers but also enhance your business's visibility in this tight-knit community.

Effective Online Strategies

Your online presence is fundamental in drawing in customers in today's tech-savvy world. Here are some specific, actionable steps you can take:

  • Create a user-friendly website: Your website should be easy to navigate and mobile-friendly. Include essential details such as your business hours, location, and a clear description of your products or services. Adding a blog to share local stories or tips related to your business can also increase your visibility in search engines.
  • Utilize social media platforms: Platforms like Facebook and Instagram are perfect for showcasing your unique offerings. Share high-quality images, behind-the-scenes content, and customer testimonials. Engage with your audience by responding to comments and messages promptly. Consider creating events on Facebook to promote special sales or community gatherings.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Positive reviews on Google and Yelp can significantly enhance your business's credibility. After each transaction, gently ask satisfied customers to leave a review, and always respond to reviews—both positive and negative—to show that you value customer feedback.
  • Run targeted ads: Use Facebook Ads or Google Ads to reach specific demographics in New Bern. You can target users by location, interests, and behaviors to ensure your ads are seen by potential customers who are most likely to engage with your business.
  • Offer online promotions: Entice new customers with exclusive online discounts or limited-time offers. Create urgency by promoting special deals on social media or through email newsletters to encourage quick action.

Powerful Offline Strategies

While online marketing is essential, traditional methods can still have a profound impact on your local reach. Here are some ideas to consider:

  • Participate in local events: New Bern hosts numerous festivals and community events throughout the year, such as the Mumfest and the New Bern Farmers Market. Set up a booth or sponsor an activity to showcase your products and connect with potential customers face-to-face.
  • Join local business associations: Collaborating with other entrepreneurs can foster valuable relationships. Associations like the New Bern Area Chamber of Commerce offer networking opportunities, resources, and support that can help you grow your business and visibility.
  • Utilize local media: Don’t underestimate the power of traditional media. Reach out to local newspapers, radio stations, and community newsletters to promote special events, sales, or new product launches. This can attract attention from those who may not be active online.
  • Offer workshops or classes: Engage the community by hosting workshops or classes related to your business. For instance, if you run a bakery, consider offering a cupcake decorating class. This not only draws people in but also positions your business as an expert in your field.
  • Establish cross-promotions: Build relationships with neighboring businesses for cross-promotional opportunities. For example, if you own a coffee shop, partner with a local bookstore for a “Buy a Book, Get a Coffee Discount” promotion. This encourages customers to visit both establishments.

The New Bern Business Community

New Bern's business community is rich with opportunities for collaboration and support. By getting involved, you can tap into a wealth of resources that can help you thrive:

  • Join the New Bern Area Chamber of Commerce: This organization is a hub for local businesses, providing networking opportunities, workshops, and resources that can help you grow. Being a member also shows your commitment to the local economy.
  • Attend networking events: Look for events hosted by the Chamber or local business groups. These gatherings are perfect for meeting potential partners and customers, and they can lead to valuable referrals.
  • Participate in community initiatives: Get involved in local projects that aim to improve the community. Whether it's a cleanup day at Union Point Park or sponsoring a local sports team, your participation enhances your visibility and builds goodwill.
  • Support local charities: Consider sponsoring community events or donating a portion of your profits to local charities. This not only helps those in need but also portrays your business as a caring and responsible member of the community.
  • Share your expertise: Offer to speak at local events, schools, or workshops. Sharing your knowledge not only establishes you as an authority in your field but also builds trust with potential customers.
