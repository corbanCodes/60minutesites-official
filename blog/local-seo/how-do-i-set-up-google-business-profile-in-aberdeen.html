Why Set Up a Google Business Profile?

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for local visibility. It allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps, making it easier for potential customers to find you. Here are some key benefits:

  • Enhances your online presence: Your business will show up in local searches, making it easier for customers to discover you when they are nearby.
  • Provides important information: Customers can find crucial details like hours of operation, location, and contact information right away. This convenience can be the difference between a potential customer choosing you or a competitor.
  • Allows you to post updates and offers: You can keep your customers informed about special promotions, new products, or upcoming events, creating a sense of urgency and excitement.
  • Enables customer reviews: Positive reviews build trust and credibility. A strong profile with good ratings can influence customer decisions significantly.
  • Integrates with Google Maps: When potential customers search for services near them, your profile will appear prominently, especially if you are close to local landmarks like the Aberdeen Civic Theatre or the Dacotah Prairie Museum.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Visit the official site to begin the process of creating your profile.
  • Click on 'Manage now': Log in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, you’ll need to create an account.
  • Enter your business name and address: Check if your business already exists in the system. If it does, you can claim it. If not, select 'Add your business' and fill in the required information.
  • Verify your business: Google will ask you to verify ownership, which can be done via mail, phone, or email, depending on your preference. This step is crucial for ensuring that your business is legitimate.
  • Add opening hours: Specify your business hours, as this gives potential customers a clear understanding of when they can visit you.
  • Write a compelling business description: This is your chance to attract customers. Describe what makes your business unique, your values, and the services you offer.
  • Upload high-quality photos: Visuals are powerful. Include images of your products, staff, or the atmosphere of your business to create an inviting online presence.

How Locals in Aberdeen Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals in Aberdeen search can give you an edge. Residents often look for services nearby, especially when they need something right away. Here are the typical search behaviors:

  • Using specific terms: Residents frequently use terms like 'best coffee shop in Aberdeen' or 'plumber near me.' This highlights the importance of optimizing your profile with relevant keywords.
  • Searching for recommendations: Locals often turn to word-of-mouth and social media groups for recommendations. Engaging with your community on platforms like Facebook or Instagram can encourage these conversations.
  • Looking for good reviews: Customers typically check for businesses with positive reviews and recent updates on Google. Encourage satisfied customers to leave feedback on your profile.
  • Considering proximity to landmarks: Aberdeen locals may search for services near well-known places, such as the Aberdeen Civic Theatre or the Dacotah Prairie Museum. Ensure your profile mentions nearby landmarks to attract more local traffic.
