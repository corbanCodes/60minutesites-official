How Do I Get More Customers in Kent?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Getting customers in Kent, Washington, isn’t just about having a great product or service; it’s about being smart and strategic in your approach. The local market is competitive, but with the right tactics, you can stand out and attract more customers. With a population of over 130,000, Kent is the sixth-largest city in Washington State and boasts a diverse demographic that can be tapped into for various business opportunities.

Building a Strong Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. Here’s what actually works:

  • Make sure your website is mobile-friendly; many people search on their phones, especially in a tech-savvy city like Kent.
  • Optimize your website for local SEO to rank higher in search results, considering the keywords that locals might use.
  • Include clear calls-to-action (CTAs) on your site, guiding visitors on what to do next, such as visiting local events or promotions.
  • Use high-quality images and content that reflect your brand’s personality, showcasing local landmarks such as the Kent Station or the Green River Trail.
  • Start a blog to share valuable information about Kent's community events or local tips to boost your SEO.

Leveraging Google My Business

Google My Business is a game-changer for local visibility. Here’s how to maximize it:

  • Claim and verify your business listing; it’s free and essential for local SEO.
  • Keep your business information up-to-date: hours, location, and services, especially during local events like the Kent Cornucopia Days.
  • Post regularly about promotions, events, or special offers related to local happenings.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews; respond to them to show you care and to foster a sense of community.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Reviews can make or break your business. Here’s what I’ve seen work time and time again:

  • Ask satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on Google and Yelp, especially after they experience local events you might host.
  • Create a referral program that rewards customers for bringing in new business, perhaps offering discounts on services popular among Kent residents.
  • Share testimonials on your website and social media to build trust, especially those highlighting your involvement in local initiatives.

Getting Social with Your Community

Social media is a powerful tool for connecting with potential customers. Here’s how you can use it effectively:

  • Choose the right platforms where your target audience hangs out, such as Facebook and Instagram, which are popular among Kent locals.
  • Share engaging content, including behind-the-scenes looks at your business, customer stories, and your involvement in community events like the Kent International Festival.
  • Use local hashtags to increase your visibility within the Kent area and engage with local influencers.
  • Run contests or promotions that encourage sharing and engagement, tapping into the community spirit of Kent.

Networking and Community Engagement

Networking is crucial, especially in a tight-knit community like Kent. Here’s how to make it happen:

  • Attend local business events, fairs, or farmer’s markets to connect with potential customers; the Kent Farmers Market is a great place for visibility.
  • Join local business associations or chambers of commerce, such as the Kent Chamber of Commerce, for networking opportunities.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions or events, particularly those that align with Kent’s community-focused culture.

Running a local business in Kent can be challenging, but with the right strategies, you can attract more customers and build a loyal community. The diverse demographic, rich community events, and local support provide a robust ecosystem for businesses. Stay persistent, keep refining your approach, and don’t hesitate to lean on your neighbors for support.
