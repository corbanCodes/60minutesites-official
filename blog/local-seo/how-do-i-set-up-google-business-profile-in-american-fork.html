Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is your digital storefront. It’s what locals see first when they search for businesses like yours. Here’s why it’s crucial:

  • Enhances visibility on Google Search and Maps: A well-optimized GBP ensures that your business appears prominently in local searches, especially when people are looking for services or products in American Fork. If someone is searching for 'best pizza in American Fork', you want to be among the top results.
  • Builds credibility with customer reviews: Positive reviews help establish your reputation and build trust with potential customers. Encouraging satisfied customers to leave reviews can significantly impact your visibility and credibility.
  • Provides essential information like hours, location, and services: Make sure your profile includes accurate business hours, a full address, and a detailed list of services or products. This information is vital for customers planning their visits, especially during local events like the American Fork Festival.
  • Allows you to post updates and special offers: Use your GBP to share promotions, new menu items, or upcoming events. This keeps your customers informed and engaged, encouraging them to choose your business over competitors.

Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your GBP is easier than you think. Follow these steps to set it up correctly:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website and click ‘Manage now.’ This will direct you to the necessary setup page.
  • Sign in with your Google account (or create one if you don’t have it). Having a Google account not only helps with GBP but also allows you to access various Google services.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is vital here, as locals in American Fork rely on this information. Double-check for typos or discrepancies to avoid confusion.
  • Select the appropriate category for your business: Whether it’s a restaurant, service provider, or retail store, choosing the right category helps Google match your business with relevant searches.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites for a quick and easy setup. They specialize in creating professional-looking websites tailored for local businesses.
  • Verify your business: Google typically sends a postcard to your business address with a verification code. This step is crucial, as it confirms your business location and helps improve your profile's credibility.
  • Once verified, fill out more details: Add business hours, upload high-quality photos showcasing your products or services, and consider including a profile picture that reflects your brand. Engaging visuals can significantly attract more customers.

Understanding how residents in American Fork look for services can significantly enhance your profile's effectiveness:
