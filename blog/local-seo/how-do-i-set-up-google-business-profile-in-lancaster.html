Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Here’s how you can do it:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now'.
  • Enter your business name and address. Make sure everything is accurate! For instance, if your business is near the Lancaster Museum of Art and History or the Antelope Valley Fairgrounds, include those landmarks for better visibility.
  • Choose the right business category so people can find you easily. Lancaster has a variety of sectors including retail, dining, and services, so be specific.
  • Add your phone number and website link, if you have one.
  • Upload high-quality photos of your business—this can really catch people's eyes. Showcase your unique offerings, especially if you’re in a competitive area like Avenue K or the West Lancaster Shopping Center.
  • Verify your business through the postcard that Google sends you or by phone.

Here's the secret: Once you’re verified, you can start managing your profile and keeping it up to date!

Optimizing Your Profile

Now that your profile is set up, let’s make it shine. Optimization is key to getting more local customers. Here’s what to focus on:

  • Fill out every section completely, including business hours and services offered. For example, if you're near the Lancaster Marketplace, highlight your proximity to this shopping hub.
  • Use keywords relevant to your business in your description. Think about what locals might type in when searching, like 'best pizza in Lancaster' or 'affordable HVAC services'.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews and respond to them—this builds trust. Given Lancaster's community spirit, personal interactions can lead to word-of-mouth referrals.
  • Post updates regularly, like special promotions or events. It keeps your profile fresh! Consider local events like the Lancaster Performing Arts Center performances to tie into your promotions.
  • Use Google Posts to share news or offers directly on your profile.

The best part is that all this helps your business show up in local searches, which is what you want!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take for my profile to be verified?

It usually takes about 1-2 weeks for Google to send you a verification postcard, but sometimes it can be faster.

Q: Can I change my business information later?

Yes, you can update your business information anytime through your Google Business Profile dashboard.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical location?

You can still create a profile by selecting the option that says you provide services at customer locations instead of a storefront. This is especially beneficial for mobile businesses servicing the Lancaster area.
