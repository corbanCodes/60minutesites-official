Tips for Improving Local SEO in Virginia Beach

Look, I get it. Navigating local SEO can feel overwhelming, but it’s not rocket science. Here are some practical tips to help your business rank higher in Virginia Beach searches:

  • Optimize your Google My Business listing: Make sure all your info is accurate—hours, address, phone number—and include great photos of your business, especially if you're near popular spots like the Virginia Beach Fishing Pier or the Adventure Park at Virginia Aquarium.
  • Use local keywords: Think about terms people might use to find your services, like 'best coffee in Virginia Beach' or 'family-friendly restaurants near the Boardwalk.' Don’t forget to include seasonal keywords, as summer tourism peaks bring more visitors looking for local dining and entertainment.
  • Get reviews: Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews on Google and Yelp. Responding to reviews shows you care and helps your ranking. Highlight your unique offerings, like fresh seafood or eco-friendly products, to attract attention.
  • Create local content: Write blog posts about Virginia Beach events or spotlights on local attractions, such as the annual Neptune Festival or the Virginia Beach Neptune statue. This builds community and keeps your site fresh.
  • Build local backlinks: Connect with other Virginia Beach businesses and see if they’ll link to your site. Local chambers of commerce, like the Virginia Beach Chamber of Commerce, are great places to start.
