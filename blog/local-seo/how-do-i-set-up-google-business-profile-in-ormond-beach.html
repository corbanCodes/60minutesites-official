Getting Started with Google Business Profile

Creating your Google Business Profile is a straightforward yet essential step for your Ormond Beach business. Follow these practical steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Navigate to the official site and click 'Manage now' to begin your journey.
  • Sign in: Use your existing Google account or create a new one. Having a Google account not only helps you manage your business profile but also gives you access to various Google services that can benefit your business.
  • Enter your business name and category: Choose a name that matches your brand identity and add a category that accurately reflects your offerings, such as 'Italian Restaurant' or 'Spa Services.' This helps Google understand what you do and improves your visibility in relevant searches.
  • Fill in your business location: Provide an exact address so locals can easily find you. Make sure to consider nearby landmarks, like the Ormond Memorial Art Museum or the beautiful Tomoka State Park, as these can enhance your profile's visibility.
  • Add your contact information: Include a phone number and a link to your website. This makes it easy for potential customers to reach out and learn more about your services.
  • Verify your business: Verification can involve receiving a postcard at your business address or a phone call from Google. This step is crucial as it confirms that you are the rightful owner of the business.
  • Enhance your profile: Once verified, take the time to fill out your profile thoroughly. Add high-quality photos of your products, services, staff, and the interior or exterior of your business. Include your business hours and write a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique, like your commitment to local ingredients or your friendly customer service.

How Locals in Ormond Beach Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Ormond Beach search for services can give your business a significant advantage. Here are some key strategies locals typically use:

  • Using specific keywords: Residents often search with specific phrases like 'best seafood restaurant in Ormond Beach' or 'trusted HVAC services near me.' Incorporating these keywords into your business description can help you appear in relevant searches.
  • Searching near popular landmarks: Locals frequently look for businesses in proximity to well-known sites such as the Ormond Beach Scenic Loop or the historic Casements. By mentioning these landmarks in your profile and optimizing your location settings, you can attract more customers.
  • Utilizing Google Maps: Many people check Google Maps for businesses within walking distance of their homes or favorite spots. Ensure your address is accurate and that you have marked your location correctly on the map.
  • Reading reviews and ratings: Local customers often rely on reviews to gauge the quality of services. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews on your Google Business Profile, as this can significantly influence potential customers' decisions.
