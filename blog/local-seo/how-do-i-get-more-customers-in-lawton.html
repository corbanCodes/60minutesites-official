Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Establishing a compelling online presence is crucial for small businesses looking to thrive in today's market. Here are some actionable strategies to help you get noticed and connect with local customers:

  • Create a user-friendly website with 60 Minute Sites: Your website should serve as a digital storefront, effectively showcasing your products or services. Make it visually appealing and easy to navigate. Consider including high-quality images, clear calls-to-action, and customer testimonials. Ensure your contact information is easily accessible, and think about integrating an online booking system if applicable to your business.
  • Engage on social media platforms: Facebook and Instagram are popular among Lawton residents, making them ideal platforms for connection. Share engaging content, such as behind-the-scenes looks at your business, customer spotlights, and promotional posts. Use localized hashtags to increase visibility and consider hosting contests or giveaways to encourage interaction.
  • Utilize local SEO techniques: Optimize your website for local search by incorporating keywords related to Lawton into your content. Create a Google My Business profile to enhance your visibility in search results and make it easier for potential customers to find your location, hours, and contact information.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Happy customers are often willing to share their experiences. Ask them to leave reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp. A positive review can significantly influence potential customers' decisions. Consider offering a small incentive for leaving a review, such as a discount on their next purchase.
  • Run targeted online ads: Utilize Facebook Ads or Google Ads to reach specific demographics in Lawton, tailoring your ads to the interests and online behaviors of your target audience. You can promote special offers, new products, or events happening at your business, ensuring that your message reaches the right people at the right time.

Offline Strategies to Gain Local Customers

While digital marketing is essential, traditional marketing methods still hold value in building connections within the community. Here are some effective offline strategies to implement:

  • Participate in local events: Engage with residents by attending events like the Lawton Farmers Market or local festivals. Set up a booth to showcase your products or services, distribute samples, or offer exclusive event-only discounts. This not only increases your visibility but also allows you to build personal connections with potential customers.
  • Collaborate with other businesses: Partner with fellow small businesses for cross-promotional opportunities. For instance, if you own a coffee shop, consider teaming up with a local bakery to create a special combo deal. This not only benefits both businesses but also creates a sense of community among local entrepreneurs.
  • Distribute flyers and business cards: Identify high-traffic areas in Lawton, such as popular coffee shops, community centers, or gyms, and ask if you can leave flyers or business cards. Make sure these materials are visually appealing and include a compelling reason for people to visit your business.
  • Offer in-store promotions: Create enticing promotions or loyalty programs that reward repeat customers. For example, a 'Buy 5, Get 1 Free' offer can encourage customers to return. Display these promotions prominently in-store and share them on your social media platforms.
  • Engage in community involvement: Sponsor local events, sports teams, or charities. Not only does this improve your visibility, but it also demonstrates your commitment to the Lawton community. For instance, sponsoring a local youth sports team can create good will and establish your business as a community-minded entity.

Engaging with the Lawton Business Community

The Lawton business community is dynamic and supportive, presenting invaluable opportunities for small business owners. Here are ways you can actively engage with this vibrant network:

  • Join the Lawton Chamber of Commerce: Membership provides access to a wealth of resources, networking opportunities, and events that help you connect with other local business leaders. Attend meetings, participate in workshops, and leverage the connections you make to grow your own business.
  • Attend local networking events: Look for events organized by local business groups or chambers. These gatherings are fantastic for exchanging ideas, learning from others, and fostering collaborations that can lead to mutually beneficial partnerships.
  • Participate in community initiatives: Engage in projects that aim to improve the community, such as clean-up days or educational workshops. This involvement not only enhances your business’s reputation but also helps you build relationships with other community-minded individuals.
  • Seek mentorship: Connect with experienced business owners who can offer insights and guidance. Conversely, consider mentoring newer business owners to share your knowledge and expertise. Building these relationships can lead to invaluable support and collaboration.
  • Utilize local resources: The Lawton Economic Development Corporation offers various resources and support for local businesses. Take advantage of their programs, workshops, and funding opportunities to navigate challenges and seize growth opportunities.

Attracting more customers in Lawton hinges on a balanced approach that combines both online and offline strategies.
