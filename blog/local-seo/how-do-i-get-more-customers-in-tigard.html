How Do I Get More Customers in Tigard?

Have you ever wondered how the charming small businesses in Tigard manage to keep their doors open and thrive in a competitive landscape? The key often lies in a blend of smart strategies, both online and offline, that help them attract and retain customers while fostering a sense of community. Whether you're running a quaint café in the bustling downtown area or a unique boutique in the Tigard Town Center, understanding your customers and their preferences is essential for growth.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Building a strong online presence is crucial for small businesses in Tigard. Here are some effective strategies to consider:

  • Optimize your website for local searches: Make sure your website includes relevant keywords related to your business and location. For example, if you own a bakery, use terms like "Tigard bakery" or "fresh pastries in Tigard". A Google My Business listing can also improve your visibility and help local customers find you more easily.
  • Use social media platforms: Platforms like Instagram and Facebook can be powerful tools for showcasing your products and connecting with the community. Post high-quality images of your offerings, share customer testimonials, and engage with your followers through polls or questions. Highlight events happening in and around Tigard, such as local art walks or food festivals, to create a sense of community.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Satisfied customers are often willing to share their experiences. Encourage them to leave positive reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp. You can do this by providing excellent service and following up with a friendly email asking for feedback. Consider offering a small discount on their next purchase as a thank-you for their review.
  • Run targeted ads: Social media platforms allow for highly targeted advertising. Use these features to reach potential customers based on demographics, interests, and location. For instance, if you own a fitness studio, target ads to local residents interested in health and wellness.
  • Create engaging content: Start a blog or YouTube channel that highlights your business and its role in the local community. Share stories about the origins of your products, tips related to your industry, or features on local suppliers. This not only establishes your expertise but also builds a connection with your audience. You could even collaborate with local influencers to expand your reach.

Offline Strategies to Increase Foot Traffic

While online presence is essential, don't underestimate the power of face-to-face interactions. Here are some offline strategies that can help:

  • Participate in local events: Engage with the Tigard community by setting up a booth at local events and farmers' markets. This allows you to showcase your products directly to the community while also building relationships with potential customers. Events like the Tigard Festival of Balloons can provide excellent exposure.
  • Offer promotions for first-time visitors: Create enticing promotions or discounts for first-time customers. This could be a percentage off their first purchase or a free sample of your product. Making a great first impression can lead to repeat business.
  • Build partnerships: Collaborate with other local businesses to cross-promote each other’s offerings. For example, if you own a coffee shop, partner with a bakery to offer a combined discount. This not only fosters goodwill among businesses but also broadens your customer base.
  • Utilize direct mail campaigns: Identify specific Tigard neighborhoods and design eye-catching postcards or flyers to introduce your business. Highlight special offers and invite potential customers to visit your location. Personal touches, like handwritten notes or local landmarks, can increase engagement.
  • Host community events: Create workshops, classes, or events that invite the community into your space. Whether it’s a cooking class at a restaurant or a craft night at a boutique, these events can attract new customers and create lasting relationships.

The Tigard Business Community

The Tigard business community is vibrant and supportive, making it easier for small businesses to thrive. By tapping into various resources, you can elevate your business:

  • Join the Tigard Chamber of Commerce: Becoming a member opens doors to networking opportunities with other local business owners and access to valuable resources such as workshops, seminars, and promotional events.
  • Attend local meetups: Actively participate in networking events where you can share ideas and collaborate with fellow entrepreneurs. These gatherings can lead to partnerships, mentorship opportunities, and support from like-minded individuals.
  • Engage with advocacy groups: Connect with local advocacy groups focused on promoting small businesses. These organizations often provide resources, grants, and lobbying efforts to support local economic growth.
  • Utilize community events: Participate in events like the Tigard Festival of Balloons or the annual Tigard Farmers Market. These gatherings attract locals and visitors alike, offering a prime opportunity to showcase your business and engage with the community.

Tigard offers numerous opportunities for small businesses to grow and flourish. By implementing a combination of online and offline strategies, you'll be well on your way to attracting more customers.
