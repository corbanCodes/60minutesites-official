Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Establishing a presence on Google is vital for your business's success in Ashland. Here’s why:

  • Increases Visibility: When locals search for services, having a Google Business Profile ensures your business appears prominently in search results and on Google Maps. For instance, if someone types "best coffee in Ashland," your shop could be among the first they see.
  • Provides Essential Information: Customers can easily find crucial details like hours of operation, location, and contact information. This is especially important for those who may be walking around downtown and looking for a quick bite to eat or a place to shop.
  • Share Updates and Promotions: Use your profile to post updates about special offers, new products, or events you’re hosting. Imagine promoting a weekend market or a seasonal sale that attracts foot traffic.
  • Encourages Customer Reviews: Reviews from satisfied customers can enhance your credibility and attract new clients. When locals see positive feedback about your business, they’re more likely to choose you over competitors.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is straightforward and can be done in just a few steps. Here’s how to do it:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile Website: Go to the Google Business Profile website and sign in with your existing Google account or create a new one. If you already use Gmail, you can use that account.
  • Enter Your Business Name: Input your business name exactly as it appears in the real world. This helps ensure that customers can find you easily. Choose the appropriate category that best describes your business to improve search relevance.
  • Add Your Business Location: Enter your physical address accurately. This is crucial for helping locals find you, especially if you're located near popular spots like the Ashland Theatre or Lithia Park.
  • Provide Contact Information: Include your phone number and website URL. This not only helps customers reach you easily but also allows them to check out your offerings online.
  • Verify Your Business: Choose a verification method, such as a phone call, postcard, or email. Verification establishes your business’s authenticity and is a key step in making your profile live.
  • Enhance Your Profile: Once verified, enrich your profile with high-quality photos of your products or services. A well-written business description, highlighting unique features or local connections, can draw in potential customers. Make sure to include keywords that resonate with your audience, such as "family-owned" or "locally sourced ingredients."

How Locals in Ashland Search for Businesses

Understanding how people in Ashland search for businesses can help you tailor your Google Business Profile to better meet their needs:

  • Specific Searches: Many locals search for specific products or services, such as "best pizza in Ashland" or "hair salons near me." Ensure your business description includes relevant keywords that align with these searches.
  • Word of Mouth: Locals often seek recommendations from friends and family. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews, as positive feedback can significantly influence prospective clients.
  • Community Support: People in Ashland take pride in supporting local businesses. Highlighting your community ties, such as sourcing ingredients from nearby farms or participating in local events, can resonate well with customers.
  • Keyword Optimization: Consider incorporating local keywords into your business description. Phrases like "Ashland artisan" or "downtown Ashland" can help you show up in relevant searches, attracting more local customers.
