Why a Google Business Profile Matters

A Google Business Profile is essential for getting noticed in your local area. It helps your business appear in search results and on Google Maps. Here’s why you should set one up:

  • Increases online visibility: With so much competition, having a Google Business Profile can put your business front and center when potential customers search for services or products you offer. In a place like Manhattan Beach, where foot traffic is common, being easily found online can translate to increased walk-ins.
  • Provides essential info: Your profile displays crucial information such as business hours, location, and contact details. For instance, if you're running a seasonal ice cream shop, your hours might change with the seasons, and keeping this updated can help customers know when to visit.
  • Encourages customer reviews: Reviews can significantly influence purchasing decisions. Encouraging satisfied customers to leave positive feedback on your profile can enhance your reputation. Consider offering a small discount on their next purchase for a review, creating a win-win situation.
  • Engages your audience: Use your profile to post updates about new products, upcoming events, or special promotions. For instance, if you’re hosting a surfboard demo day at the beach, sharing this information on your profile can draw in the local surfing community.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile page to begin the setup process.
  • Sign in: Use your existing Google account or create a new one tailored for your business. This account will be your hub for managing your online presence.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure to input your business name exactly as it appears in the real world. If you operate a coffee shop called 'Manhattan Beach Brews,' don't forget the location to avoid confusion.
  • Choose the correct category: Selecting the right category helps Google match your business with relevant searches. For example, if you run a yoga studio, be sure to select 'Yoga Studio' to reach those searching for yoga classes in the area.
  • Verify your business: Verification is crucial for credibility. This often involves receiving a postcard from Google at your business address. Follow the instructions provided to complete the verification process.
  • Add photos, descriptions, and business hours: High-quality images of your products or services can make a significant impact. Write a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. If you’re a local restaurant, mention your signature dishes or any special culinary traditions you uphold.

How Locals in Manhattan Beach Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents in Manhattan Beach look for businesses can help you tailor your Google Business Profile effectively. Here are some common behaviors:

  • Search Keywords: Many locals search for businesses using keywords like 'best coffee in Manhattan Beach' or 'surf shops near me.' To tap into this, include relevant keywords in your business description and posts.
  • Use of Google Maps: Residents often rely on Google Maps to find nearby restaurants or services. Ensure your location is accurately marked on the map so that visitors can easily find you while strolling along the beach or shopping downtown.
  • Reviews and Ratings: People frequently check reviews before deciding where to go. Engage with reviews by responding to customers, showing that you value their feedback and fostering a sense of community.
  • Local Events and Promotions: Local events, like the Manhattan Beach Farmers Market or summer concerts in the park, can provide excellent opportunities to promote your business. Consider creating special offers that coincide with these events to attract more customers.
