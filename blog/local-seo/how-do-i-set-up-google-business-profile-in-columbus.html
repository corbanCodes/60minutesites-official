What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating a standout Google Business Profile is key to attracting local customers in Columbus. Here’s the secret to optimizing yours:

  • Complete every section: Fill in your business name, address, phone number, website, and hours of operation. Columbus has a variety of neighborhoods, so ensure your location is clear for locals.
  • Use high-quality photos: Showcase your products, services, and your business location. Pictures of your storefront on 13th Street or your signature dish can really make an impression.
  • Write a compelling description: Tell your story and explain what makes your business unique in Columbus. Highlight any special connections to local events, like the annual Riverfest or the Columbus Symphony Orchestra.
  • Choose the right categories: Make sure you select the most relevant categories for your business. Columbus is home to diverse industries, from healthcare to manufacturing, so tailor your categories accordingly.
  • Encourage reviews: Ask your happy customers to leave positive reviews to boost your credibility. Positive feedback from local patrons can greatly influence other Columbus residents looking for services.

How Columbus Locals Search

In Columbus, people often rely on both word-of-mouth and online searches to discover local businesses. Residents frequently share recommendations on social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram, especially for new eateries or shops. When searching for services, they may use keywords specific to the area, such as 'best coffee shop in Midtown' or 'affordable plumbing services near Fort Benning.' Trust me on this one, locals love supporting businesses that feel connected to the community. So, if you're a coffee shop, restaurant, or service provider, make sure you're showing up in those local searches. The best part is that when your Google Business Profile is fully optimized, you’ll be more likely to pop up in those searches, making it easier for locals to choose you over the competition!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to set up a Google Business Profile?

It can take just a few minutes to set up, but optimizing it with photos and descriptions may take a little longer. Consider adding local landmarks or events in your description to resonate more with Columbus residents.

Q: Can I manage my Google Business Profile from my phone?

Absolutely! Google has a mobile app that makes it easy to manage your profile on the go, perfect for busy entrepreneurs in Columbus.

Q: What should I do if my business information changes?

Just log into your Google Business Profile and update your information; it's super simple! Keeping your hours and location current is especially important, as Columbus hosts many tourists and events that can affect foot traffic.
