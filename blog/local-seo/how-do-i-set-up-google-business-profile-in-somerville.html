Why Set Up Your Google Business Profile?

A Google Business Profile is like your digital storefront. It's where potential customers find key information about your business. Here's why it's crucial:

  • Improves local visibility in searches: Your business will appear in local search results, making it easier for customers to find you when they need your services. This is particularly important in a tight-knit community like Somerville, where local support can make or break a business.
  • Provides essential details: Your profile offers vital information such as operating hours, location, and contact details. This ensures that prospective customers can easily reach you, reducing the chances of losing potential foot traffic.
  • Allows you to share updates: Keep your customers informed about your latest offerings, events, or promotions. For example, if you're hosting a special event at your café in Union Square, you can update your profile to draw in the crowds.
  • Enhances credibility: Customer reviews can significantly influence potential buyers. A well-maintained profile with positive feedback can boost your credibility and encourage others to choose your business over competitors.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile isn't complicated if you follow these steps:

  • Go to Google Business Profile: Visit the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Start Now.' This will take you to the setup page where the journey begins.
  • Sign in: Use your Google account to sign in. If you don’t have one, creating an account is straightforward and should only take a few minutes.
  • Add your business: Enter your business name and address, ensuring it matches what you have on your website and other listings. Consistency is key to improving your search rankings.
  • Choose your business category: Select a category that best fits your business type—this helps Google show your profile to the right customers. For example, if you run a bakery, choose ‘Bakery’ instead of a more generic term like ‘Food’ to attract the right audience.
  • Verify your business: You can verify through a postcard, phone, or email, depending on your business type. Verification is essential as it confirms that you are the owner of the business, which builds trust with potential customers.
  • Complete your profile: Add photos, business hours, and a description to attract customers. High-quality images of your products or the interior of your establishment can entice potential visitors. Don’t forget to include links to your website and social media pages to enhance connectivity.

How Locals in Somerville Search for Businesses

Understanding how Somerville locals search can give you an edge in this vibrant neighborhood. Many residents rely on their smartphones while on the go, especially around popular spots like Assembly Row or Magoun Square. Here are some insights:

  • Location-based searches: People often type in 'coffee shop near me' or 'best pizza in Somerville' when looking for places to eat or hang out. By optimizing your profile with keywords relevant to your offerings, you can improve your chances of appearing in these searches.
  • Word-of-mouth: Locals frequently ask for recommendations on community forums or social media, which makes positive reviews essential. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews and engage with them by responding to feedback, showing that you value their opinions.
  • Events and specials: Many Somerville residents check for local businesses that are promoting events or unique deals, especially during festivals or community gatherings. Make sure to update your profile about any seasonal promotions or special events, like the Somerville Arts Council’s ‘ArtBeat’ festival, to capture the attention of those looking to enjoy local attractions.
