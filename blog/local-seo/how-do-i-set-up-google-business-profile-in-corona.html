What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting noticed: your profile needs to be complete and accurate. Trust me on this one, every detail counts. Here's what you should focus on:

  • Business Name: Ensure it matches your real-world name.
  • Address: Make sure it's correct, so customers can find you easily; for example, if you’re located near the popular Dos Lagos shopping center, mention that.
  • Phone Number: List a local number to build trust, ensuring it reflects the 951 area code.
  • Website: Link to your business website, if you have one, especially if you have a storefront in busy areas like Main Street.
  • Business Hours: Keep these updated, especially during holidays or for local events like the Corona Arts Festival.
  • Photos: Include high-quality images of your business, products, and team to showcase the unique offerings in the heart of Corona.
  • Description: Write a brief, engaging description of what you offer, emphasizing how your services cater to the needs of the Corona community.
  • Categories: Choose the right categories to help Google understand what you do, such as 'Local Coffee Shop' or 'Family Restaurant.'

It's all about being present and making connections with your community, whether through local events, farmers' markets, or involvement in the Corona Chamber of Commerce.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Simply search for your business on Google, and if it appears, follow the prompts to claim it. You’ll need to verify your ownership by receiving a postcard or phone call.

Q: Can I update my profile later?

Absolutely! You can and should update your profile whenever your information changes, like new hours or services, especially during community events or holidays.

Q: What if my business doesn’t appear on Google?

If you can't find your business, you can create a new profile from scratch. Just make sure to follow the guidelines to ensure it meets local search requirements.

Q: How do I get reviews?

Encourage happy customers to leave reviews on your profile. You can ask them directly or send follow-up emails after their visit, perhaps even offering a small incentive for customers who share their experiences online.

Q: Is it free to use Google Business Profile?

Yes! It's completely free, making it a fantastic marketing tool for small businesses looking to connect with the growing population of Corona.
