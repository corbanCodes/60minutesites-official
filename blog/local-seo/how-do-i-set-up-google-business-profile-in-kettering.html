Setting up a Google Business Profile is essential for small businesses in Kettering, Ohio, to attract local customers. This free tool helps your business appear in local searches, making it easier for potential customers to find you.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to ensure your business stands out:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now.'
  • Sign in with your Google account or create a new one if necessary.
  • Enter your business name and address accurately.
  • Choose the correct business category to help Google understand your services.
  • Add a phone number and website link for customer inquiries.
  • Verify your business through the method provided by Google, usually via a postcard.
  • Complete your profile by adding business hours, photos, and a description.

How Locals in Kettering Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals in Kettering search for businesses can help tailor your Google Business Profile effectively. Here are some common search behaviors:

  • Many residents use Google Maps to find nearby services, making accurate location information crucial.
  • Local searches often include keywords like 'near me' or specific neighborhoods, so make sure to optimize your business description with relevant terms.
  • Reviews play a significant role; customers frequently check ratings before visiting a business.
  • Phone calls are common for local inquiries, so ensure your contact information is correct and up-to-date.
