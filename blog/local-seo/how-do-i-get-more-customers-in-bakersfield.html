How Do I Get More Customers in Bakersfield?

9 min read

In Bakersfield, California, small businesses are constantly looking for ways to attract more customers. With a unique blend of local charm and a growing economy, there are plenty of opportunities for savvy entrepreneurs to make their mark. Known for its rich agricultural history and as a hub for the oil industry, Bakersfield is also experiencing a cultural renaissance, making it an exciting place for new ventures.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a clean, user-friendly website that showcases your services and reflects the local culture, perhaps incorporating elements of Bakersfield's unique heritage.
  • Optimize your website for local SEO to rank higher in search results, using keywords like 'Bakersfield services' or 'local Bakersfield businesses'.
  • Create engaging content that speaks to your target audience, such as blogs about local events or how-to guides relevant to Bakersfield residents.
  • Make sure your site is mobile-friendly, as many customers browse on their phones, especially during events like the annual Kern County Fair.

Harnessing the Power of Google

Google is the go-to for most people looking for services. Here’s how to leverage it:

  • Claim your Google My Business listing and keep it updated with accurate information, including your business hours and location in relation to popular Bakersfield landmarks like the Bakersfield Museum of Art.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, which can significantly impact your visibility in local search results.
  • Use local keywords in your business description to attract local searches, such as 'services in Bakersfield' or 'Bakersfield [your service]'.

Encouraging Reviews and Referrals

Reviews matter more than you think. Here’s how to encourage them:

  • Ask happy customers for reviews after a service, particularly after local events where your business was showcased.
  • Offer a referral discount to incentivize word-of-mouth, encouraging customers to spread the word within their Bakersfield community.
  • Showcase testimonials on your website and social media, emphasizing your local roots to build trust.

Utilizing Social Media Effectively

Social media can be a game-changer for visibility. Here’s what you should do:

  • Choose platforms that align with your audience, like Facebook or Instagram, where local residents are active.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content and customer stories to connect on a personal level, perhaps highlighting your participation in local events like the Bakersfield Jazz Festival.
  • Use local hashtags to reach a wider audience in Bakersfield, such as #BakersfieldLocal or #SupportBakersfield.

Networking and Local Events

Getting involved in the community is key. Here’s how to make connections:

  • Attend local networking events and trade shows, such as the Bakersfield Business Expo, to meet other entrepreneurs.
  • Partner with other local businesses for promotions, especially during popular local events like the Bakersfield Christmas Parade.
  • Join local business organizations, like the Greater Bakersfield Chamber of Commerce, to expand your network and gain visibility.

The hustle of running a local business in Bakersfield is real, but with the right strategies, you can attract more customers and thrive. Stay persistent, engage with your community, and leverage the unique characteristics of Bakersfield, such as its strong agricultural ties and burgeoning cultural scene, to watch your business grow.
