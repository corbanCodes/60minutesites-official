What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Creating a standout Google Business Profile does take a bit of effort, but trust me on this one, it’s worth it. Here’s what you need to focus on:

  • Accurate business name and address: Make sure it matches what you use on your website and includes your street name, which can be as historical as Chapel Street or as trendy as Crown Street.
  • Business hours: Keep them updated so customers know when to visit, especially during the bustling lunch hours or evening events at local venues like the Shubert Theatre.
  • High-quality photos: Show off your products or services to attract attention; consider including images of your business's ambiance or dishes to entice food lovers among New Haven's many college students and residents.
  • Engaging business description: Use keywords that reflect what you do and who you serve; mention local landmarks such as Yale University, which can help in connecting with tourists and locals alike.
  • Customer reviews: Encourage happy customers to leave positive feedback to boost your credibility. Given New Haven's community-oriented culture, word-of-mouth can significantly impact your local visibility.

How New Haven Locals Search

New Haven locals love to discover businesses through both word-of-mouth and online searches. Picture this: someone is craving a slice of pizza from one of the city’s famed pizzerias or looking for a cozy bookstore. They’ll often ask friends or check Google to find the best spots nearby. It’s all about community here. People trust recommendations from their neighbors and are also savvy about searching online for quick answers. If your business shows up in these searches, you’re already ahead of the game. Make sure your Google Business Profile reflects the local vibe by using references to the city’s beloved events like the New Haven Jazz Festival or the International Festival of Arts & Ideas, and connects with the community spirit!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set up my Google Business Profile?

Go to the Google Business Profile website, click ‘Start Now,’ and follow the prompts to enter your business information. Don’t forget to verify your business through the mail or phone call to fully activate your profile.

Q: Can I change my business hours later?

Absolutely! You can update your hours anytime through your profile. This is especially important during holidays or events at nearby venues like the Yale Bowl or the New Haven Green.

Q: How do I get reviews?

Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews by simply asking them after a good experience, or you can send them a follow-up email with a link to your profile. Consider incentivizing feedback by offering discounts on future visits, which can be particularly effective in a place where local loyalty is valued.

Remember, setting up your Google Business Profile is just the first step. Keep it updated and engaging, and you’ll see how locals in New Haven will start finding and supporting your business more easily. You've got this!
