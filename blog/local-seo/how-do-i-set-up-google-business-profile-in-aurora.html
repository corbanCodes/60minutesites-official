What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to standing out: a well-optimized Google Business Profile can put your business on the map—literally. Follow these tips to make sure yours shines:

  • Complete every section: Fill in your business name, address, phone number, and website, ensuring you're listed accurately in a city known for its landmarks like the Paramount Theatre and the SciTech Hands On Museum.
  • Use high-quality images: Showcase your products, services, and even your storefront, especially if you're located near popular areas like the Fox River or the Aurora Riverwalk.
  • Write a compelling description: Tell your story and explain what makes you unique, drawing inspiration from Aurora’s rich history, which dates back to the 1830s.
  • Choose the right categories: Select categories that accurately describe your business, ensuring you align with local searches for popular categories such as dining, retail, or healthcare.
  • Update your hours: Make sure your operating hours are current to avoid customer confusion, especially during local events like the Aurora Farmers Market or the annual Festival of Lights.
  • Encourage reviews: Ask your satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, as Aurora residents often rely on community feedback when choosing where to shop or eat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Simply go to Google Business Profile, sign in with your Google account, and follow the prompts to claim your business.

Q: Can I change my business information later?

Absolutely! You can update your information anytime to keep it accurate.

Q: Do I need a physical location to use Google Business Profile?

Not necessarily! You can still set up a service area business if you provide services at customer locations, which is ideal for many local entrepreneurs in Aurora who serve the surrounding suburbs.
