Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile is often the first impression potential customers have of your business. It's not just about being listed; it's about being seen and chosen. A strong profile can help you:

  • Appear in local search results: When Burien residents are looking for services, having an optimized profile will increase your chances of being featured prominently in local search results. For example, if someone searches for 'best pizza in Burien,' a well-maintained profile can help you secure a top spot.
  • Showcase essential information: Your profile should clearly display your hours of operation, location, and contact information. This ensures customers can easily find you and know when to reach out. Make sure to update these details during holidays or special events.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Positive reviews not only build trust but also improve your ranking in search results. Encourage happy customers to leave feedback and respond graciously to any comments, whether positive or negative, to show you value their input.
  • Share engaging photos: High-quality images of your products, services, or even your store can attract attention. For example, if you run a bakery, posting pictures of fresh pastries and a cozy interior can entice potential customers to stop by.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your Google Business Profile set up is straightforward, but you want to do it right. Here’s how:

  • Visit Google Business Profile: Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now.' This will take you to the setup interface.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure it’s accurate and matches the name you use in your shop front. This consistency helps with searchability and recognition.
  • Select your business category: Choosing the right category is crucial as it helps Google show your business for relevant searches. For instance, if you own a pet grooming service, select 'Pet Groomer' to reach the right audience.
  • Fill out your contact information: Include your phone number and website so potential customers can easily reach you. Ensure your website link is active and leads to a user-friendly experience.
  • Add your business hours: Clearly state your opening and closing hours so customers know when to visit or call. Consider additional hours for holidays.
  • Upload high-quality photos: Invest time in taking professional-looking pictures that reflect your business environment and offerings. Pictures of your team at work or happy customers enjoying your services can foster a personal connection.
  • Verify your business: Once you’ve filled everything out, verify your business through the method Google provides, usually via mail or phone. This step is essential to making your profile live.

How Locals in Burien Search for Businesses

Understanding how people in Burien search for businesses can help you tailor your profile effectively. Here are some common search behaviors:

  • Locals use specific phrases: Residents often search for terms like 'best coffee near me' or 'family-friendly restaurants in Burien.' Incorporating these keywords into your business description can enhance your visibility.
  • Utilization of Google Maps: Many residents rely on Google Maps to find nearby services, meaning having accurate location details is essential. Make sure your address is correct and consider adding directions from local landmarks.
  • Word-of-mouth recommendations: Even in a digital age, word-of-mouth still carries weight. Encourage satisfied customers to share their experiences online. This can lead to more reviews and greater trust.
  • Incorporate local landmarks: Using local landmarks or neighborhoods in your description can enhance your profile's visibility. For example, mentioning that you are located near the Burien Community Center or across from the Burien Town Square can attract residents who are searching for nearby services.
