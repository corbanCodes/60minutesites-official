How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is not only simple but also an essential step toward enhancing your business's visibility. Follow these steps to make sure your business shines:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by going to the official Google Business Profile site. This is your launchpad for creating a profile that stands out.
  • Click 'Manage now' to start the setup process: This will take you to a page where you can begin entering your business details.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key here. Make sure your business name is exactly as it appears in the physical world, and provide a correct address so locals can find you easily. For instance, if you own a café on the corner of Garvey and Newmark, make sure that address is precise.
  • Select your business category: Choosing the right category is crucial for attracting the right customers. For example, if you run a sushi restaurant, select 'Sushi Restaurant' rather than just 'Restaurant' to improve your chances of being found by the right audience.
  • Fill in your contact information: Include a phone number and website URL, if available. This makes it easy for potential customers to reach you and learn more about your offerings. If you don't have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and efficiently.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your business address. This step is crucial as it confirms your business's legitimacy and allows it to appear in local search results.
  • Add photos of your business: Pictures speak volumes. Showcase high-quality images of your space, products, and team. If you're a local bakery, show off your delicious pastries or the cozy interior of your shop to entice potential customers.
  • Keep your profile updated: Regularly check and update your hours and offerings, especially during holidays or special events. If you plan a special promotion for the Lunar New Year, make sure that information is reflected in your profile.

How Locals in Monterey Park Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents search for businesses is key to your success in Monterey Park. Here are some insights into local search habits that can help you tailor your business strategies:

  • Google Maps is a go-to tool: Many locals use Google Maps to find nearby restaurants, shops, and services. Ensure your profile is optimized for visibility in map searches to attract foot traffic.
  • Mobile searches are common: People often search for businesses on their smartphones while they’re out and about, whether they’re looking for the best boba tea or the nearest car wash. Make sure your profile is mobile-friendly to cater to these users.
  • Keywords matter: Residents may search for specific terms like 'best dim sum in Monterey Park' or 'family-friendly parks near me.' Incorporate these local keywords into your business description to improve your chances of being found.
  • Reviews influence decisions: Locals are likely to check ratings and customer feedback before visiting a business. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, as this can significantly enhance your credibility and attract new patrons.
  • Community recommendations are powerful: Word of mouth and local social media groups play a big role in what businesses get noticed. Engage with local community groups on platforms like Facebook to promote your offerings and foster relationships with potential customers.
