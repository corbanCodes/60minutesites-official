How to Find a Salon in Denver

5 min read

Real talk: you’re ready for a fresh look, but finding the right salon in Denver can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. With so many options nestled in vibrant neighborhoods like LoHi, Cherry Creek, and Capitol Hill, it’s essential to know how to narrow it down to the perfect spot for your hair goals. Whether you're after a chic bob, vibrant color, or expert styling, Denver has a plethora of salons that cater to all tastes and preferences.

Start with Recommendations

Ask your friends, family, or coworkers where they go for their hair needs. Word-of-mouth is one of the best ways to find a salon you'll love. You can also look up reviews on platforms like Yelp or Google. Check for:

  • Overall ratings: Look for salons that consistently receive high scores, ideally above 4 stars. These ratings often reflect customer satisfaction and quality of service.
  • Detailed comments about experiences: Read through reviews to find insights about specific stylists and the services they provide. Pay attention to feedback regarding haircuts, color services, and customer service.
  • Photos of their work: Many salons post before-and-after photos, which can give you a visual sense of their capabilities and style.

Consider Your Hair Type and Style

Not every salon specializes in every type of hair. If you have curly hair, for example, seek out a salon known for working with texture. Research salons that cater to:

  • Curly or textured hair: Some salons focus specifically on curly hair techniques, such as the DevaCut method, which enhances natural curl patterns.
  • Color treatments: If you're looking for balayage, ombre, or vibrant colors, find salons that showcase these techniques in their portfolio.
  • Specialized cuts or styling: For intricate haircuts, bobs, or styles that require advanced techniques, look for stylists with specific training or accolades in those areas.

Explore the Neighborhoods

Denver is a city made up of vibrant neighborhoods, and each has its own charm. Take a stroll through areas like:

  • Capitol Hill: Known for its trendy, eclectic salons, Capitol Hill is home to creative spaces that often feature stylists who excel in modern, avant-garde looks.
  • Highlands: This neighborhood boasts chic, modern spaces, some of which are located in historic buildings, providing a unique atmosphere for your salon experience.
  • Washington Park: With a cozy, community vibe, this area has salons that focus on personalized service and building long-term relationships with clients.

Visiting salons in person can also help you gauge the atmosphere and customer service before making a decision. Pay attention to how staff interact with clients and the overall cleanliness and organization of the space.

Check Social Media and Websites

Most salons today have a social media presence. Check their Instagram or Facebook pages to see their latest work. Look for:

  • Before-and-after photos: These images can illustrate the salon's skill level and the types of transformations they specialize in.
  • Stylist portfolios: Many stylists showcase their work on social media, allowing you to get a sense of their unique style and expertise.
  • Client testimonials: Reading about other clients' experiences can provide additional assurance about the quality of service.

A well-maintained website can also provide you with valuable information about services and pricing. Look for blogs or articles that discuss hair care tips or trends, as these often reflect a salon's expertise and dedication to the craft.

Schedule a Consultation

Once you have a shortlist, consider booking a consultation. This is a great way to meet the stylist and discuss your hair goals without committing to a full appointment. During this time, pay attention to:

  • The stylist's approach to your hair: A good stylist will listen to your preferences and provide insightful suggestions based on your hair type and lifestyle.
  • The salon's atmosphere: Make sure you feel comfortable in the space. A welcoming environment can significantly enhance your overall experience.
  • How comfortable you feel: A consultation is also about finding someone you trust. If you feel at ease and confident in the stylist's abilities, that's a great sign.

Finding the right salon in Denver doesn’t have to be overwhelming. With some research and a little exploration, you can discover a place that feels just right for you. Whether you’re looking to change your style completely or just maintain your look, a skilled stylist can make all the difference.
