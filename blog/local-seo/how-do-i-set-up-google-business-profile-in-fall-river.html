How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can be completed in just a few steps. Follow this easy guide to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Click on 'Manage Now' to begin.
  • Sign in: Use your existing Google account or create a new one. This account will help you manage other Google services as well.
  • Enter your business name and address: Accuracy is key here. Be specific, as this will help potential customers find you easily, especially if you're located on a busy street like South Main Street.
  • Select your business category: Choose the category that best fits your offerings. For instance, if you run a diner, select 'Restaurant' or 'Diner' to help Google understand what you provide.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: Ensure that your contact information is correct. If you don’t have a website yet, 60 Minute Sites can assist you in creating a professional one that aligns with your brand.
  • Verify your business: Google will send a postcard to your business address with a verification code to confirm your location. This step is crucial for ensuring your profile is visible to locals.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos, your business hours, and a compelling description that tells your story. Highlight what makes your business unique, perhaps mentioning your long-standing history in the community or your commitment to local sourcing.

How Locals in Fall River Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents in Fall River search for businesses can significantly improve your visibility and attract more local customers. Here are some common search behaviors:

  • Business reviews: Customers frequently look for reviews to assess quality and trustworthiness. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, as this can greatly influence potential customers.
  • Directions: Many residents prefer to know how to get to your location. Clear directions can be a deciding factor for those who are ready to visit.
  • Special offers or events: Fall River hosts various local festivals, such as Fall River Celebrates America, where locals seek out special deals or events. Use your profile to promote any unique offerings during these times.
