Why Google Business Profile Matters

Your Google Business Profile is like your digital storefront. It's often the first thing potential customers see when they search for businesses like yours. Here’s why it’s essential:

  • Improves visibility on Google Search and Maps: When you set up your profile correctly, your business becomes searchable on Google Maps and appears in local search results. This is especially important in Clovis, where people frequently rely on their smartphones to find nearby services, whether it’s a cozy coffee shop in Old Town or a reliable mechanic off Shaw Avenue.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile displays crucial information like business hours, location, and a link to your website. Ensure you keep this information updated, especially during holidays or special events. Clovis has many local festivals, so if your hours change for an event, make sure to reflect that!
  • Encourages customer reviews: Reviews can make or break a local business's reputation. When customers leave positive feedback on your profile, it builds trust and credibility. Encourage your customers to share their experiences, and don’t forget to respond to reviews, whether they are positive or negative. Engaging with your customers shows that you value their input.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Navigate to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Manage Now.' This will direct you to the setup process.
  • Sign in with your Google account: If you already have a Google account for your business, sign in. If not, take a moment to create one. Having a dedicated business account can help keep your personal and professional lives separate.
  • Enter your business name: Make sure to enter your business name exactly as it appears in the real world. This consistency helps with brand recognition.
  • Choose the correct category: Selecting the right category for your business is vital. Clovis has a diverse array of businesses, from boutique shops in the Sierra Vista neighborhood to family-owned restaurants. Choose a primary category that best describes your business, and consider adding secondary categories to broaden your reach.
  • Fill in your location information: Provide accurate address details and specify your service areas if applicable. If you’re a mobile service, like a lawn care provider, make sure to list all areas you serve.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: Make it easy for potential customers to reach you. If you don't have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and efficiently, showcasing your services and offerings.
  • Verify your business: Google usually sends a postcard with a verification code to your business address. This step is crucial for ensuring that your business is authentic and trustworthy.
  • Complete your profile: Enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos, your business hours, and a detailed description. Visuals are particularly important—consider adding images of your storefront, products, or happy customers enjoying your services around Clovis.

How Locals in Clovis Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents of Clovis look for businesses can help you tailor your profile. Here’s what they often do:

  • Search for specific services: Clovis residents frequently search for specific services, such as 'best coffee in Clovis' or 'plumbing near me.' Tailoring your profile to include relevant keywords can help you show up in these searches.
  • Use Google Maps: Many locals utilize Google Maps to find nearby businesses with good reviews. Make sure your profile is optimized and encourages reviews to increase your chances of being featured in these searches.
  • Look for local events or promotions: Clovis hosts a variety of local events, from farmers markets to arts festivals. If your business is involved in any local happenings, make sure to mention this on your profile. You could also offer special promotions connected to these events to attract more customers.

Being aware of these habits can help you optimize your profile with the right keywords and offers tailored to the Clovis community. Consider including local landmarks or community highlights in your business description to resonate with locals.
