Why is a Google Business Profile Important?

Having a Google Business Profile helps you connect with customers right when they are searching for services or products you offer. It boosts your visibility on Google Search and Maps, making it easier for locals to find your business. Here's why it's important:

  • Enhances local SEO: By optimizing your profile with relevant keywords, you can improve your chances of ranking higher in search results. For instance, including phrases like 'Lake Oswego bakery' can draw in more targeted traffic.
  • Provides a platform to share essential info: With your profile, you can display your operating hours, location, and contact information prominently. Make sure to keep this information updated, especially during holidays or special events.
  • Enables customer reviews: Customer feedback is invaluable. Positive reviews build trust and credibility, encouraging potential customers to choose your business over competitors. Engage with reviews by thanking customers for positive feedback and addressing any concerns raised in negative reviews.
  • Offers insights: Google provides data on how customers find and interact with your listing. This information can help you tailor your marketing strategies, such as focusing on peak search times or adjusting your service offerings based on popular demand.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your Google Business Profile up and running is easier than you might think. Just follow these steps:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by going to the Google Business Profile website and clicking on 'Start Now.' This will guide you through the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Be sure to include your location in Lake Oswego. If your business is near popular spots like Lake Oswego Village or along the picturesque Oswego Lake, make sure to highlight that in your address.
  • Select your business category: This helps Google connect you with relevant searches. For example, if you run a coffee shop, choose 'Coffee Shop' as your category, which will help coffee lovers in the area find you more easily.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: If you don't have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one easily. They specialize in helping local businesses establish a strong online presence quickly and efficiently.
  • Verify your business: You can verify your business through the mail, phone, or email. This step is crucial as it helps to confirm that your business is legitimate, which enhances customer trust.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, fill out your profile completely. Include your hours of operation, services offered, and high-quality photos of your business. Consider adding images of your storefront, products, and even customer interactions to make your profile more engaging.

How Locals in Lake Oswego Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search for businesses can give you a leg up in attracting customers. Here are some common behaviors:

  • Using Google Maps: Many residents use Google Maps to find nearby shops or restaurants while out and about. Make sure your business is marked correctly on the map for easy visibility.
  • Specific searches: People often search with specific terms, such as 'coffee near me' or 'best sushi in Lake Oswego.' By optimizing your profile with these keywords, you'll increase your chances of appearing in relevant searches.
  • Customer reviews matter: Local reviews play a big role in decision-making; customers often look for businesses with high ratings and recent feedback. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews and consider responding to all reviews to show that you value customer input.
  • Community engagement: Local events or Instagram hashtags might inspire searches, so staying connected to local happenings can be beneficial. Participate in community events, sponsor local sports teams, or collaborate with other businesses in Lake Oswego to increase your visibility.
