Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started with your Google Business Profile is easier than you think. Here’s a simple breakdown of what to do:

  • Claim your business on Google My Business if you haven't already.
  • Fill out all the essential information: business name, address, phone number, and website. Make sure your address is accurate, especially since Lewisville spans several neighborhoods like Old Town, Vista Ridge, and Castle Hills.
  • Choose the right business category to help Google understand what you do. For example, if you’re a restaurant, specify if you serve Italian, Mexican, or American cuisine.
  • Upload high-quality photos of your business, products, and team. Consider showcasing your storefront against the backdrop of Lewisville's scenic Lewisville Lake or the bustling shopping areas.
  • Write a compelling business description that highlights what makes you special, whether you offer unique services or are a staple in the community.
  • Keep your hours updated, especially around the holidays when events like the Lewisville Western Days Festival draw more visitors.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews; they make a huge difference!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Simply go to Google My Business, search for your business, and follow the prompts to claim it. Make sure to have documentation ready to verify your business location in Lewisville.

Q: Can I update my profile later?

Absolutely! You can make changes to your profile anytime to keep your information current. This is especially important during events like the Lewisville Farmers Market.

Q: How important are customer reviews?

They are crucial! Positive reviews can greatly improve your visibility and credibility in local searches. Consider responding to reviews to engage with your customers and show that you value their feedback.

Q: What should I do if my business hours change?

Just log into your profile and update your hours to reflect any changes. Be sure to account for seasonal variations or special holiday hours.

Q: Is it free to set up a Google Business Profile?

Yes! Creating and managing your profile is completely free, which is a fantastic opportunity for small businesses in Lewisville to enhance their online presence.
