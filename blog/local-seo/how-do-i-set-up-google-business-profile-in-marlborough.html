Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is a straightforward process that can significantly boost your visibility in local searches. Here’s how you can get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Start by going to the Google Business Profile page and click on 'Manage now.' This will set the foundation for your online presence.
  • Sign in or create a Google account: If you already have a Google account, log in. If not, take a few moments to create one. A Google account is essential for managing your business profile and accessing various Google tools.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure this information matches your official business documentation. For example, if your café is located on Main Street, provide the exact details to help customers find you easily.
  • Select your business category: Choose the category that best represents what you offer. This could be anything from 'Italian Restaurant' to 'Local Bookstore.' Selecting the right category will help your business show up in relevant searches.
  • Add your contact details: Include your phone number and website URL. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using 60 Minute Sites to create one quickly and easily, allowing you to establish a professional online presence without the hassle.
  • Verify your business: Google will send you a verification code through mail, phone, or email to confirm your business location. This step is crucial, as it helps prevent fraudulent listings.
  • Complete your profile: After verification, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos of your products, services, or storefront. Include your hours of operation and a detailed description of what you offer, using keywords that potential customers might search for.

How Locals in Marlborough Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents in Marlborough search for businesses can significantly enhance your strategy. Here are some local search trends to consider:

  • Utilization of Google Maps: Many residents rely on Google Maps to find nearby services, especially when searching for dining options, cafes, or retail shops. Make sure your business is listed so locals can easily find directions to your door.
  • Common search phrases: Keywords like 'best places to eat in Marlborough' or 'Marlborough hair salons' are frequently searched when locals seek recommendations. Incorporating these phrases into your profile description can improve your visibility.
  • The importance of reviews: Marlborough residents place a strong emphasis on reading reviews before choosing a business. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback on your profile. Consider responding to reviews to show that you value customer input and are engaged with your community.
  • Awareness of community events: Local events, such as the Marlborough Farmers' Market or annual festivals, often create spikes in searches. Keep your Google Business Profile updated with any special offers or events you're participating in, as this can attract more foot traffic during these busy times.
