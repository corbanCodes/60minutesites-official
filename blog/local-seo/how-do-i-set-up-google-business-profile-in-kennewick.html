Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is crucial for your visibility in the local market. It helps potential customers find you when they’re searching for services or products you offer. Here’s why you should set one up:

  • Increased visibility on Google Search and Maps: When locals search for businesses like yours, a well-optimized profile can ensure you appear at the top of search results and on Google Maps.
  • Ability to share essential information: You can easily provide your business hours, address, contact details, and website link, ensuring that customers have all the information they need at their fingertips.
  • Customer reviews enhance your reputation: Positive reviews not only boost your credibility but also influence potential customers’ decisions. Responding to reviews, whether positive or negative, shows that you value customer feedback.
  • Easy integration with Google Ads for promotion: Once your profile is set up, you can seamlessly run Google Ads campaigns to reach even more local customers.

Steps to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your Google Business Profile is straightforward. Here’s how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Head over to the official site and click ‘Manage now’ to get started.
  • Sign in with your Google account: If you already have a Google account, simply log in. If not, creating a new one is quick and free.
  • Enter your business name and address: Ensure you use your real business name as it appears in the community. This helps with brand recognition, especially in neighborhoods like Southridge or Downtown Kennewick.
  • Choose the appropriate business category: Selecting the right category is essential for appearing in relevant searches. For instance, if you own a coffee shop, select ‘Coffee Shop,’ and consider adding subcategories like ‘Cafe’ or ‘Espresso Bar’ if applicable.
  • Provide accurate contact information: Include your phone number and website. Make sure these details are correct so customers can easily reach you.
  • Verify your business: This verification process may involve receiving a postcard with a code or a phone call. This step is crucial, as only verified businesses will appear in search results.
  • Complete your profile: After verification, fill in your profile with your business hours, high-quality photos, and a compelling description that highlights what makes your business unique. If you have a cozy ambiance, delicious menu items, or exceptional customer service, showcase that!

How Locals in Kennewick Search for Businesses

Understanding how locals search can help you tailor your Google Business Profile effectively. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Search habits: Many residents use Google Maps to find nearby services. They often search for terms like ‘best pizza near me’ or ‘plumbers in Kennewick.’ Ensure your keywords reflect what locals are looking for.
  • Importance of visuals: Locals appreciate seeing photos that showcase the ambiance of your business, particularly for dining or retail. Consider adding images of your storefront, interior, and popular menu items or products.
  • Leverage reviews: Positive reviews play a huge role in decision-making. Encourage satisfied customers to leave feedback and consider incentivizing reviews by offering discounts or loyalty rewards. Responding to these reviews also fosters community engagement.
  • Mobile searches: Many people in the Kennewick area also search for businesses on their mobile devices while on-the-go, so ensure your information is up-to-date and easily accessible. Consider using call-to-action buttons like ‘Call Now’ or ‘Get Directions’ to facilitate quick connections.
