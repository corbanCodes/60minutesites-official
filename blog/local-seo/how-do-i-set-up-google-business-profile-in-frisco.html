Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting started: First, create your profile by going to Google Business Profile and claiming your business. Once you’re in, you’ll want to focus on these key elements:

  • Business Name: Make sure it matches the name on your storefront, especially in a bustling area like the Frisco Square.
  • Address: Be precise so people can find you easily; include landmarks like the Toyota Stadium or The Star, which are well-known in the area.
  • Phone Number: Use a local number to encourage calls from nearby customers, as Frisco residents prefer to support local businesses.
  • Business Hours: Keep these updated, especially for holidays and special events like the Frisco Freedom Fest.
  • Categories: Choose categories that best describe your business. This helps Google understand what you do and match you with searchers looking for your services; for example, if you’re a restaurant, specify if you offer Tex-Mex cuisine or Asian fusion.
  • Photos: Upload high-quality images of your business, products, or services. A picture's worth a thousand words! Highlight your unique offerings, like the artisanal coffee from your café or the craftsmanship of your handmade goods.

The best part is that you can manage and update all this information right from your Google account, which is essential for keeping up with the fast-paced growth in Frisco.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to set up my Google Business Profile?

It can take just a few minutes to set up, but verifying your business may take a couple of days, particularly if you’re located in a high-traffic area like the Stonebriar Centre.

Q: Can I update my profile later?

Absolutely! You can update your profile anytime to reflect changes in hours, services, or promotions. This is especially important during events like the Frisco Arts Walk or the annual Frisco Fair, when foot traffic might increase.

Q: Is it really important to get reviews?

Yes! Reviews can significantly influence potential customers and improve your ranking in local searches. In Frisco, many residents rely on reviews to choose where to shop or dine.

Q: How do I respond to customer reviews?

You can respond directly on your Google Business Profile, which shows that you value customer feedback and are engaged with your community.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical location?

You can still set up a profile by choosing the option for service-area businesses, which is ideal for many entrepreneurs in Frisco who operate from home or offer mobile services.
