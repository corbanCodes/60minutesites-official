How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Shreveport?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Shreveport is a vibrant city with a rich history and a strong sense of community. As a small business owner here, you know how important it is to stand out in our local market. Let’s dive into some practical ways to boost your local SEO and get more customers through your doors.

Optimize Your Google My Business Listing

Your Google My Business (GMB) listing is like your business's digital storefront. Here’s how to make it shine:

  • Claim and verify your GMB listing.
  • Fill out every section: business hours, phone number, website, and services.
  • Use high-quality images of your products or storefront, especially highlighting local charm.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews, and make sure to respond to them—both good and bad.

Use Local Keywords Strategically

Keywords are how people find you, so let’s make them specific to Shreveport. Here’s what to do:

  • Research keywords like 'best coffee in Shreveport' or 'Shreveport plumbing services.'
  • Integrate these keywords naturally into your website content, blog posts, and product descriptions.
  • Don’t forget to include local landmarks or neighborhoods, like mentioning your shop is near the Louisiana Boardwalk.

Create Local Content

Content is king, especially when it's relevant to your local audience. Consider these tips:

  • Start a blog that talks about local events, tips, or news relevant to your industry.
  • Create guides, such as 'Top 10 Places to Eat in Shreveport' if you run a restaurant, or 'How to Prepare for Mardi Gras in Shreveport' if you're in retail.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for joint promotions and feature each other on your websites.

Build Local Backlinks

Getting other local businesses and organizations to link to your site can boost your credibility. Here’s how:

  • Partner with local charities or events and get featured on their websites.
  • Join local business associations and get listed on their member pages.
  • Provide testimonials for other local businesses and request a backlink in return.

Improving your local SEO in Shreveport doesn’t have to be overwhelming. Start with these practical tips, and watch your business grow. Remember, it’s all about connecting with our community. If you need help or have questions, don’t hesitate to reach out!
