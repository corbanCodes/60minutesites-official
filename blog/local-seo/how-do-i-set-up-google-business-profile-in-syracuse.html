Creating a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting your business noticed: a well-optimized Google Business Profile! Follow these steps to make sure you shine:

  • Claim your listing on Google My Business.
  • Fill out every section completely; this includes your business name, address, phone number, and website. Ensure your address is specific to neighborhoods in Syracuse like Armory Square or the Near West Side.
  • Add high-quality photos of your business, products, or services, showcasing the unique character of Syracuse.
  • Write a clear and inviting business description that highlights what makes you unique, especially in the context of local culture and community.
  • Choose relevant categories that accurately describe your business; consider niche categories popular in Syracuse, such as 'local eateries', 'craft breweries', or 'art galleries'.
  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews, and make sure to respond to them, fostering a strong sense of community.
  • Keep your hours updated, especially during local events like the Great New York State Fair or Syracuse University football games.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Simply go to Google My Business and follow the steps to claim your business. You'll need to verify your ownership, usually through a postcard sent to your business address.

Q: Can I update my profile later?

Absolutely! You can update your information, photos, and posts anytime to keep your profile fresh and engaging. Regular updates can attract more attention, especially during Syracuse's vibrant seasonal events.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical storefront?

No problem! You can still create a profile by selecting the 'service area' option, allowing you to serve customers in your local area without a storefront. This is ideal for service providers in neighborhoods like Fayetteville or Liverpool.

Q: How can I get more reviews?

Ask your happy customers to leave a review. You can also send them a follow-up email or text with a direct link to your profile. Building relationships with local customers at events can also encourage them to share their experiences online.

Q: How long does it take for my profile to show up in searches?

It varies, but generally, once you complete your profile and verify it, you should start seeing it in local searches within a few days. Engaging with local influencers or participating in community events can speed up your visibility.
