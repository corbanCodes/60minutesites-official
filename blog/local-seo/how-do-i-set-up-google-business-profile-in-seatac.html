How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now.'
  • Sign in with your Google account or create a new one.
  • Enter your business name and address. Ensure the information is accurate and matches your business signage.
  • Select the appropriate business category to help Google understand what services you offer.
  • Add your business phone number and website URL.
  • Verify your business through the method provided by Google, which may include a postcard sent to your business address.
  • Once verified, complete your profile by adding business hours, photos, and a description of your services.
