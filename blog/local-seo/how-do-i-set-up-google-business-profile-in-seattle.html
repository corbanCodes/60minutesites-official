Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting started is easier than it seems. Here's how to set up a Google Business Profile that stands out:

  • Claim your business: Go to Google Business Profile and find your business. If it’s not listed, create a new profile. Seattle has a plethora of diverse businesses, so make sure yours is distinct.
  • Fill in the basics: Add your business name, address, and phone number. Make sure these details are consistent across all platforms. Seattle's neighborhoods, such as Capitol Hill, Ballard, and West Seattle, each attract different demographics, so tailor your information accordingly.
  • Choose the right category: This helps Google understand what you do, so pick the most relevant category for your business. For instance, if you’re a coffee shop, don’t just select ‘Cafe’—consider ‘Specialty Coffee’ to attract the coffee-loving Seattleites.
  • Add photos: Upload high-quality images of your products, services, or your storefront. Visuals grab attention! Showcase Seattle-inspired themes or local ingredients if applicable.
  • Write a compelling description: Use this space to tell your story, highlight what makes you unique, and include important keywords. Mention local landmarks or your connection to the Seattle community to resonate with potential customers.
  • Keep your hours updated: If you have special hours for holidays or events, make sure to keep them updated. Local customers appreciate this! Given Seattle’s bustling events like Bumbershoot or the Seattle International Film Festival, be sure to adjust your hours during such times.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I verify my business?

Google will send a verification code to your business address, or you may have options to verify via phone or email, depending on your business type. This process is essential for establishing credibility in a city known for its trust in local businesses.

Q: Can I manage multiple locations?

Yes! You can manage several locations from a single Google Business Profile account—helpful for businesses with branches in various Seattle neighborhoods.

Q: What if my business hours change?

Simply log in to your profile and update your hours. Keeping this information current is crucial for local customers, especially during Seattle's popular events and festivals.

Q: How can I encourage reviews?

Ask satisfied customers to leave a review. You can even provide them with a link to make it easy! In a city where community feedback is highly valued, positive reviews can significantly impact your business.
