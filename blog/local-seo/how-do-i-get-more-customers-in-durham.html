How Do I Get More Customers in Durham?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you're looking to grow your small business in Durham, North Carolina, you're entering a vibrant market rich with potential. Known for its unique blend of innovation and tradition, Durham is a city bustling with opportunities. With proper strategies, you can attract more customers and build a loyal clientele in this dynamic environment.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online in Durham's competitive landscape. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a clean, user-friendly website that showcases your services, alongside your location in neighborhoods like Trinity Park or Brightleaf Square.
  • Optimize your site for local SEO, incorporating keywords such as 'Durham', 'Research Triangle', and your specific service.
  • Create valuable content that answers common questions in your industry to establish authority, and consider incorporating local insights that resonate with Durham residents.
  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly; many customers will browse on their phones while exploring the city.
  • Set up Google My Business to improve your visibility in local searches, as many Durham locals rely on Google to find nearby services.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews

In Durham, online reviews can make or break your business. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Encourage happy customers to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, or Facebook, platforms widely used by locals.
  • Respond to all reviews, positive or negative, to show you care and engage with the Durham community.
  • Create a process for asking for reviews, perhaps after a successful service or purchase, and consider using local events as an opportunity to gather feedback.
  • Use testimonials on your website and social media to build trust with potential customers, especially highlighting local success stories.

Leveraging Referrals and Networking

Referrals are a goldmine, especially in a close-knit community like Durham. Here’s how to tap into that:

  • Offer referral discounts to existing customers who bring in new clients, fostering a community-oriented atmosphere.
  • Build relationships with other local businesses for cross-promotion, such as collaborating with nearby restaurants or shops in the Bull City.
  • Attend local events and networking meetups, like the monthly First Friday Art Walk or the Durham Farmers' Market, to connect with potential customers and partners.
  • Join local chambers of commerce or business groups, like the Greater Durham Chamber of Commerce, to expand your network and establish your business within the community.

Utilizing Social Media Effectively

Social media is a powerful tool for local businesses in Durham, but it’s all about how you use it. Here’s the deal:

  • Focus on platforms where your target audience hangs out, like Facebook or Instagram, and consider engaging with local influencers.
  • Share engaging content that showcases your personality and expertise; think behind-the-scenes, tips, and customer stories that highlight Durham's culture.
  • Run targeted ads to reach local customers based on interests and geography, focusing on the diverse demographics found in areas like downtown and the surrounding neighborhoods.
  • Engage with your followers. Respond to comments and messages promptly to foster a sense of community and connection.

Running a local business in Durham is no easy feat, but with determination and the right strategies, you can thrive. Keep pushing forward, stay engaged with your community, and don’t hesitate to adapt your approach as you learn what works best for you in this vibrant and supportive city.
