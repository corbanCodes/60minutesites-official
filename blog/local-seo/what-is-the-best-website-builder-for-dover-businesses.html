The Local Business Scene in Dover

Dover boasts a lively and diverse local business landscape, deeply intertwined with its rich history and vibrant culture. From the picturesque shops near The Green, where community members gather, to the delightful eateries surrounding the Delaware State Capitol, small businesses are the heart of this thriving community. Here are some essential highlights:

  • Retail: The Dover Mall, a cornerstone of local shopping, features a mix of national retailers and unique local boutiques, giving residents a variety of shopping options. Don't miss local favorites like the charming shops on State Street, where you can find one-of-a-kind gifts and handmade crafts.
  • Culinary Delights: The food scene in Dover is both diverse and delicious. You can indulge in everything from farm-to-table restaurants like The Brick Works, which emphasizes local ingredients, to cozy coffee shops like The Greenery where you can unwind with friends over artisanal brews and pastries.
  • Community Events: Community spirit runs high in Dover, with regular events such as the Dover Days Festival celebrating local history and culture. These events not only foster relationships among residents but also encourage support for local businesses by showcasing their offerings.
  • Support for Local Artisans: Many businesses in Dover collaborate with local artisans, creating a vibrant marketplace that highlights the creativity and talent within the community. For example, local craft fairs often feature handmade goods from Dover artisans, providing them with a platform to showcase their work and connect with customers.
