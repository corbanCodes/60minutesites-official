How Do I Rank Higher on Google in Amarillo?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

Hey there, fellow Amarillo business owner! If you're looking to improve your local SEO and get more visibility in our great Texas Panhandle city, you're in the right place. Amarillo is not just a hub for commerce but also a vibrant cultural center with a rich history. Let’s dive into some practical strategies that can help you stand out in Amarillo's bustling market.

Optimize Your Google Business Profile

First things first, have you claimed your Google Business Profile yet? If not, do it now! This is your golden ticket to local visibility.

  • Make sure your business name, address, and phone number are accurate and consistent. Amarillo has a variety of neighborhoods, from the historic districts to the vibrant Westgate area, so make sure your location is clear.
  • Add high-quality photos of your products, services, and even your storefront, especially if you're located near iconic spots like the Cadillac Ranch or the American Quarter Horse Hall of Fame.
  • Encourage happy customers to leave positive reviews; they can really boost your ranking!

Local Keywords Matter

Here's the thing: using local keywords is crucial for getting found by Amarillo residents searching for your services.

  • Think about what your potential customers are typing into Google. For example, 'best barbecue in Amarillo' or 'Amarillo hair salons.' With local favorites like The Big Texan Steak Ranch, you can even create content that compares your offerings to well-known local spots.
  • Incorporate these keywords naturally into your website content, blog posts, and even social media. Mention local attractions like Palo Duro Canyon for businesses related to outdoor activities.
  • Don’t forget to mention local landmarks or neighborhoods relevant to your business, like the vibrant Historic District.

Leverage Local Links

Building relationships with other local businesses can work wonders for your SEO.

  • Partner with other Amarillo businesses for events or promotions and ask them to link back to your site. Collaborate with local events like the Amarillo Music Festival to gain exposure.
  • Consider sponsoring local events, such as the annual Panhandle-Plains Historical Museum exhibitions or participating in community fairs, which can also lead to valuable backlinks.
  • Don’t shy away from reaching out to local bloggers or media — getting featured in the Amarillo Globe-News can really help your visibility.

Create Local Content

Offering valuable content is a great way to establish your authority and attract local customers.

  • Write blog posts about local events, seasonal activities, or even tips related to your industry that cater to Amarillo residents. Highlight events like the Tri-State Fair & Rodeo to engage your audience.
  • Share stories about how your business supports the community or highlights local culture, like the annual Amarillo Livestock Show.
  • Use social media to engage with local trends and showcase your business's personality. Participate in city-wide initiatives or local art exhibitions to get noticed.
