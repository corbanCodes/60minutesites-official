Online Strategies

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront, and it needs to impress. Here’s what actually works:

  • Invest in a clean, user-friendly website that loads fast to cater to the tech-savvy population of Lewisville.
  • Optimize for local SEO to ensure you show up in Lewisville search results, especially when people search for services related to the nearby Lewisville Lake or the bustling Vista Ridge Mall.
  • List your business on Google My Business and keep it updated to take advantage of the city's growing online presence.
  • Create valuable content (like blogs) that answers your customers' questions, especially about local events or attractions.
  • Engage with your audience on social media; post regularly about local happenings, community events, and showcase your services to connect with residents.

Offline Strategies

Don’t underestimate the power of face-to-face connections. Here are some offline strategies that can drive customers to your door:

  • Attend local networking events or join the Lewisville Chamber of Commerce, which hosts various events to help local businesses connect.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses for cross-promotions, especially those located around the popular Old Town Lewisville area, which attracts many visitors.
  • Ask satisfied customers for referrals and offer incentives for them; word-of-mouth is particularly effective in tight-knit communities.
  • Participate in community events or sponsor local sports teams, such as those associated with the Lewisville Parks and Recreation Department, to increase visibility.
  • Distribute flyers or coupons in high-traffic areas around town, like near the Lewisville Town Square or the bustling shopping centers.
