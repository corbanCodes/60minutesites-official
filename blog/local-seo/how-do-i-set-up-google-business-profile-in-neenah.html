Setting up a Google Business Profile is essential for small businesses in Neenah, Wisconsin, to enhance their online visibility. This free tool allows you to manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps, making it easier for local customers to find you.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website.
  • Click on 'Manage now' to sign in or create a Google account.
  • Enter your business name and click 'Next.'
  • Choose your business category to help Google understand what you offer.
  • Add your location if you have a physical storefront or service area.
  • Provide your contact details, including phone number and website.
  • Verify your business through the method Google provides, which typically involves receiving a postcard at your business address.
  • Once verified, complete your profile with photos, business hours, and a detailed description.
