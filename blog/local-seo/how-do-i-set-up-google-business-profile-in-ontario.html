What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to getting the most out of your Google Business Profile: it's all about providing accurate and detailed information. Trust me on this one, the more complete your profile, the better your chances of standing out. Here are some must-have elements:

  • Business name: Keep it consistent with your actual business name.
  • Address: Make sure it's accurate; this helps with local searches, particularly important in a city where neighborhoods like Ontario Ranch and the historic downtown area are well-defined.
  • Phone number: Use a local number to establish trust with your community, as many residents prefer supporting businesses that are part of their locality.
  • Business hours: Clearly state when you’re open to avoid customer frustration, especially during busy local events like the Ontario Festival of the Arts.
  • Categories: Choose the right categories that describe your business, whether you're in retail, food service, or professional services.
  • Photos: Upload high-quality images of your products, services, or storefront to attract customers; Ontario's scenic parks and community events can inspire great visuals.
  • Reviews: Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive reviews; they really do matter! In Ontario, locals often rely on reviews to make decisions on where to shop or eat.
