Why Your Google Business Profile Matters

Having a Google Business Profile is crucial for connecting with customers in Hilliard. This free tool acts as your online storefront, making it easy for locals to discover your business. Here’s why it’s important:

  • Improves your visibility on Google Search and Maps: When potential customers search for businesses like yours in Hilliard, having a well-optimized profile ensures you appear in the search results, especially when they're looking for services in close proximity.
  • Provides essential information: Your profile displays vital details such as your address, phone number, hours of operation, and website link. For instance, if a family is heading to the Hilliard Family Aquatic Center and needs a nearby restaurant, they can easily find your details.
  • Allows you to post updates, offers, and events: Keep your community informed by sharing special promotions or upcoming events, such as a local craft fair or holiday sales. Engaging posts can draw new customers into your store.
  • Lets customers leave reviews: Customer reviews significantly enhance your credibility. In a close-knit community like Hilliard, word-of-mouth is powerful; positive feedback from satisfied customers can quickly spread and bring in new business.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these simple steps to get started:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website: Start by visiting the Google Business Profile site and click 'Manage Now.' This will lead you to the setup process.
  • Enter your business name and address: Make sure it matches exactly what’s on your storefront. For example, if you own a coffee shop called 'Hilliard Brew,' ensure that your listing reflects that name and the exact address on your signage.
  • Select the appropriate business category: Choose a category that accurately describes your business. For instance, if you own a bakery, select 'Bakery' to help Google understand the type of services you offer.
  • Add your contact phone number and website URL: Ensure this information is correct so potential customers can reach you without hassle. If you have a unique URL, like hillardbrew.com, include that.
  • Verify your business: You can verify your business through a postcard, phone call, or email. Google will send a verification code, which you must enter to confirm your business's legitimacy.
  • Complete your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding high-quality photos of your products or services, detailed business hours, and a compelling description that includes keywords your customers might use when searching.
