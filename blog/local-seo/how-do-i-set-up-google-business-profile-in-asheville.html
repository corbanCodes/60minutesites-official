Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for connecting with potential customers right here in Asheville. Here are a few compelling reasons why:

  • Boosts Your Visibility: When someone searches for a product or service on Google, having a business profile increases your chances of appearing in local search results and on Google Maps. This is particularly important in Asheville, where tourists often look for local favorites while exploring.
  • Provides Essential Information: Your profile allows you to display important information such as business hours, location, and contact details. For example, if you're a late-night eatery in the River Arts District, showcasing your extended hours can attract hungry patrons after a long day of exploring.
  • Enhances Credibility through Reviews: Customer reviews are a powerful way to build trust. A well-maintained Google Business Profile allows customers to leave feedback, and responding to these reviews can show that you value their opinions. This is especially important in a community like Asheville, where word-of-mouth is vital.
  • Showcases Your Unique Offerings: Use your profile to highlight what makes your business special by adding photos and posts. Whether you’re a local brewery with seasonal craft beers or a boutique with locally sourced products, vibrant images can draw in potential customers.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these detailed steps to make the most of your Google Business Profile:

  • Sign in to Google Business Profile: Use your existing Google account or create a new one. This account will be the gateway to all your business management needs.
  • Add Your Business: Enter your business name and address. Make sure it matches your physical location in Asheville, as this will help you show up in local searches. If you’re a food truck, include your regular locations or areas where you operate.
  • Select Your Business Category: Choose the category that best describes your business. Be as specific as possible; for instance, if you own a vegan café, select 'Vegan Restaurant' instead of just 'Restaurant' to target the right audience.
  • Fill in Your Details: Provide your business hours, contact information, and a brief description that captures the essence of your offerings. Consider mentioning nearby landmarks or popular attractions, such as the Blue Ridge Parkway or the Asheville Art Museum, to attract visitors.
  • Verify Your Business: Google may send a postcard to your business address or call you to verify your information. This step is crucial for confirming that you are the owner and helps improve your profile’s trustworthiness.

How Locals in Asheville Search for Businesses

Understanding how Asheville locals search for businesses can help you tailor your profile effectively. Many residents look for:

  • Neighborhood-Specific Keywords: Terms like 'best coffee in West Asheville' or 'family-friendly restaurants near Biltmore' are common. Consider using these keywords in your profile description to increase your visibility.
  • Visual Content: Locals often prefer businesses that showcase appealing photos of their offerings and ambiance. Invest in professional photography to highlight your unique space or delicious dishes, especially if you’re located near popular spots like the Grove Arcade.
  • Community Engagement: Residents appreciate businesses that actively participate in local events or support Asheville initiatives. Highlight any collaborations with local artists or sponsorship of community events on your profile to foster a sense of connection with potential customers.
