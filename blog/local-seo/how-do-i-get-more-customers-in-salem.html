How Do I Get More Customers in Salem?

9 min read

Get a Professional Local SEO Website in 60 Minutes →

If you’re running a small business in Salem, Oregon, you know the hustle to attract customers is real. Fortunately, there are effective strategies that can help you stand out in this vibrant community and grow your client base. With its rich history, proximity to natural attractions, and a supportive local population, Salem offers a unique backdrop for entrepreneurial success.

Build a Professional Website

The truth is, your website is the foundation of getting customers online. It’s your digital storefront and must represent your brand well. Here’s what actually works:

  • Ensure it’s user-friendly and mobile-responsive to cater to the on-the-go residents of Salem.
  • Include clear calls to action that guide visitors on how to engage with your business.
  • Optimize for local SEO with keywords relevant to Salem, such as "Salem Oregon services" or "local Salem businesses."
  • Showcase customer testimonials and your services prominently to build trust among prospective clients.
  • Keep your content updated to improve search rankings and reflect seasonal offerings, especially given Salem's seasonal events and festivals.

Leverage Google My Business

Google My Business (GMB) is essential for local visibility. I've seen this work time and time again:

  • Claim and verify your GMB listing, ensuring it is complete with your business name, address, and phone number.
  • Regularly update your business hours and services, especially during local events like the Oregon State Fair or the Salem Art Fair.
  • Post updates, offers, and events to engage your audience, taking advantage of Salem’s community-centric calendar.
  • Encourage customers to leave reviews, and respond to them promptly to foster a sense of community.

Harness the Power of Reviews and Referrals

Word of mouth is still one of the most powerful marketing tools. Here's how to make it work for you:

  • Provide exceptional service that customers want to talk about, especially in a town where personal recommendations carry significant weight.
  • Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, and local platforms.
  • Set up a referral program to reward customers for bringing in new clients, creating a network of loyal supporters who thrive on community engagement.

Get Active on Social Media

Social media is a great way to connect with your local community. Here are some practical tips:

  • Choose platforms where your target audience hangs out (Facebook, Instagram), and consider local groups that focus on Salem happenings.
  • Share behind-the-scenes content to humanize your brand, showcasing your involvement in local events like the Salem Farmers Market.
  • Promote local events and collaborate with other Salem businesses to strengthen community ties.
  • Use targeted ads to reach potential customers in the Salem area, especially during peak shopping seasons.

Network and Get Involved Locally

Networking is crucial for building relationships and visibility in Salem. Consider these strategies:

  • Attend local business events and community gatherings, such as the annual Salem Arts Festival.
  • Join local business associations or chambers of commerce to connect with other entrepreneurs.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions that can benefit both parties.
  • Volunteer for community initiatives to raise your profile, tapping into Salem's strong community spirit.

As you navigate the challenges of running a small business in Salem, remember that building relationships and establishing a strong online presence are key. Stay persistent, keep trying new strategies, and soon you’ll see your customer base grow. With Salem's supportive community and rich cultural tapestry, your business has the potential to thrive.
