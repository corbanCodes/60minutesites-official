Understanding Local SEO

Local SEO is all about getting your business found by people in your area. Here’s how to make it work for you in New Orleans:

  • Claim your Google My Business listing and fill it out completely.
  • Use local keywords in your website content, like 'New Orleans coffee shop' or 'French Quarter tours'.
  • Optimize your website for mobile since a lot of locals search on their phones while on the go.
  • Build local backlinks by collaborating with other New Orleans businesses. Think about local bloggers or tourism sites.
  • Encourage online reviews from customers to boost your credibility.

Optimizing for Local Searches

Look, I get it—ranking high in local searches can feel overwhelming. But don’t worry; here are some practical tips:

  • Use local schema markup on your website to help search engines understand your business’s location.
  • Create location-specific pages if you have multiple locations, highlighting what makes each one unique.
  • Keep your NAP (Name, Address, Phone number) consistent across all platforms.
  • Participate in local events and festivals to increase your visibility and get your name out there.
  • Leverage social media to engage with the local community—post about local events or share stories about your business journey in New Orleans.
