How Do I Get More Customers in Ashland?

5 min read

If you're running a business in Ashland, Kentucky, you understand the importance of attracting the right customers. This charming town, nestled along the Ohio River and steeped in rich history, presents unique opportunities to connect with both local residents and visitors. With landmarks like the Paramount Arts Center and the picturesque Ashland Riverfront, there are countless ways to engage the community. Let’s explore some practical strategies to effectively boost your customer base, both online and offline, so your business can thrive in this vibrant environment.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Building a robust online presence is crucial for expanding your reach in Ashland. Here are some actionable strategies to consider:

  • Create a user-friendly website with 60 Minute Sites to showcase your products or services. Ensure your website is mobile-friendly, visually appealing, and easy to navigate. Incorporate high-quality images and clear calls-to-action that guide visitors to make purchases or contact you.
  • Utilize social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram to connect with your audience and promote specials. Share engaging content that reflects local culture, such as behind-the-scenes looks at your business or posts highlighting local events. Use targeted ads to reach specific demographics within Ashland.
  • Invest in local SEO to ensure your business appears in relevant searches, especially for those looking for services in Ashland. Optimize your website with keywords that include 'Ashland,' 'Kentucky,' and your specific offerings. Register on local listings and directories to improve visibility.
  • Encourage customer reviews and testimonials on platforms like Google My Business to enhance your credibility. Consider offering a small incentive for customers who leave positive reviews, like a discount on their next purchase, to increase participation.
  • Run targeted online ads to reach potential customers in specific demographics. Use platforms like Google Ads or Facebook Ads to create campaigns that focus on your ideal customer profile, honing in on interests and locations that align with your business.

Offline Strategies to Attract Customers

While online marketing is essential, the power of offline strategies should not be underestimated. Here are some effective approaches to consider:

  • Participate in local events and festivals to showcase your business and meet potential customers face-to-face. Events like the Ashland Bluegrass Festival or the annual Christmas parade provide a platform to engage with the community. Set up a booth, offer samples, or conduct demonstrations to draw in attendees.
  • Partner with other local businesses for cross-promotions or joint events. For example, collaborate with a nearby café to offer a discount for customers who visit both locations. This not only boosts your visibility but also fosters community spirit.
  • Offer promotional discounts or loyalty programs to encourage repeat visits. Implement a loyalty card system where customers receive a stamp for every purchase, leading to a reward after a certain number of visits, which can keep them coming back.
  • Distribute flyers or brochures in high-traffic areas like the Ashland Town Center or near local schools to increase awareness of your offerings. Make sure your materials are visually appealing and clearly convey your unique selling points.
  • Join a local organization or chamber of commerce to network and gain visibility in the Ashland community. Engaging with groups like the Ashland-Boyd County Chamber of Commerce can provide valuable networking opportunities and keep you informed about community events and resources.

Embrace the Ashland Business Community

Ashland boasts a vibrant business community that can be an incredible asset for small enterprises. Building relationships with other local business owners can lead to mutual support and increased referrals. Here’s how to engage:

  • Attend local networking events to meet other entrepreneurs and share ideas. Events hosted by local organizations often provide insights into the market and allow for collaboration opportunities.
  • Join community groups focused on supporting local businesses, such as the Ashland-Boyd County Chamber of Commerce. These groups often offer workshops, resources, and promotional opportunities that can significantly benefit your business.
  • Collaborate on events or promotions to create a sense of unity and attract more customers. Consider organizing a street fair or themed night where multiple businesses participate, offering special deals and activities.
  • Get involved in community service projects, which can enhance your reputation and connect you with new customers. Engaging in local charities or events not only gives back to the community but also showcases your business's commitment to local values.
  • Stay informed about local regulations and opportunities that could impact your business. Keep an eye on city council meetings or local news to stay ahead of potential changes that might affect your operations or marketing strategies.

Combining these online and offline strategies will effectively grow your customer base in Ashland.
