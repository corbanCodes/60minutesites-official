How Do I Get More Customers in Keene?

5 min read

Let's be real – attracting customers in Keene, New Hampshire, takes more effort than just opening your doors. With its unique charm and vibrant community, small businesses here have a special opportunity to connect with locals and visitors alike through both online strategies and traditional outreach. Keene is not just a picturesque New England town; it’s a hub of activity, with its iconic landmarks like the historic Keene State College and the bustling Central Square. This article will guide you through effective ways to reach new customers in this captivating locale.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

To tap into the digital world, small businesses in Keene can implement several effective online strategies:

  • Create a user-friendly website: Your website should not only be visually appealing but also easy to navigate. Include high-quality images of your products or services, clear descriptions, pricing, and a straightforward contact page. Consider adding a blog to share updates, tips, or local happenings that engage your audience.
  • Utilize social media platforms: Platforms like Facebook and Instagram are perfect for connecting with your audience. Share behind-the-scenes content, customer testimonials, and special promotions. Engaging with local hashtags can also increase your visibility. Additionally, consider partnering with local influencers who can showcase your business to their followers.
  • Invest in local SEO: Optimize your website for local searches by incorporating keywords that reflect your business and its location, such as “best coffee in Keene” or “Keene boutique clothing.” Register your business on Google My Business to appear in local search results and Google Maps, which is crucial for attracting foot traffic.
  • Encourage customer reviews: Customer reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp can significantly influence potential buyers. Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews by providing links via email after their purchase or by displaying QR codes in-store that link to your review page.
  • Offer promotions through email newsletters: Build an email list by offering a sign-up incentive, such as a discount on their next purchase. Regular newsletters can keep customers informed about new products, upcoming events, and exclusive offers, helping to foster loyalty.

Offline Strategies to Build Local Presence

Don’t underestimate the power of face-to-face interactions! Here are some offline strategies to consider:

  • Host or participate in local events: Events like the Keene Pumpkin Festival draw large crowds and offer a great platform for local businesses. Set up a booth to showcase your offerings or sponsor a local event to boost your visibility. Participation in farmers' markets can also connect you with health-conscious shoppers looking for local products.
  • Collaborate with nearby businesses: Team up with other local businesses for cross-promotions or joint events. For example, a coffee shop could partner with a bakery to offer a “coffee and pastry” deal. Collaborative efforts can double your reach and introduce your services to a wider audience.
  • Utilize flyers and posters: Place well-designed flyers and posters in high-traffic areas such as downtown Keene, community centers, and bulletin boards. Consider places like the Keene Public Library or the local YMCA, where potential customers gather.
  • Offer workshops or classes: Hosting workshops or classes related to your business can attract new customers. For instance, a craft store could offer a beginner’s painting class or a cooking store could host a cooking demonstration. This not only showcases your expertise but also creates a community around your brand.
  • Network with other local business owners: Building relationships with fellow entrepreneurs can lead to valuable insights and referrals. Attend local business association meetings, and don’t hesitate to reach out directly to fellow business owners to discuss potential collaborations.

The Keene Business Community

Keene boasts a supportive business community that thrives on collaboration. By connecting with local chambers of commerce and business associations, you can gain access to invaluable resources. Here’s how to make the most of this community:

  • Join local business networking groups: Groups such as the Greater Keene Chamber of Commerce provide opportunities to meet like-minded entrepreneurs and share best practices. Regular networking events can help you build relationships and referrals.
  • Attend community forums: Staying updated on local initiatives through community forums can open doors to new business opportunities and collaborations. These gatherings often allow you to voice your concerns and ideas, making you an active participant in your community.
  • Engage with local influencers: Building relationships with local bloggers and social media influencers can significantly boost your business’s visibility. Consider sending them samples of your products or inviting them to your events for coverage that can reach a broader audience.
  • Participate in community service projects: Engaging in community service not only enhances your visibility but also builds goodwill. Sponsor a local charity event or volunteer at community clean-up days to show your commitment to Keene.
  • Support fellow businesses: By referring customers to other local businesses or collaborating on events, you can foster a strong network. This not only strengthens community ties but also creates a ripple effect of goodwill that can benefit everyone involved.

Whether you’re focusing on online or offline strategies, building your customer base in Keene is all about community engagement and visibility. Remember, 60 Minute Sites can help you create an attractive online presence that will draw in more customers. By combining your unique offerings with the rich culture of Keene, your business can thrive while contributing to this vibrant community.
