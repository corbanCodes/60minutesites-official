Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Creating a Google Business Profile helps your business show up in local searches, especially on Google Maps. Here’s why it matters:

  • Boosts Your Visibility: When locals search for services, your business will appear in the local pack, increasing the chances that they’ll choose you. For instance, if you own a bakery, when someone types in 'bakeries near me,' your profile will be prominently displayed.
  • Showcase Your Unique Offerings: You can add photos of your products, highlight special offers, and share your business hours. If you’re located near the Baldwin Borough Library, you might want to showcase a cozy reading nook in your café, inviting customers to enjoy a book with their coffee.
  • Build Trust Through Reviews: Customer reviews can significantly influence potential clients. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback, as Baldwin residents often rely on recommendations from their neighbors.
  • Gain Valuable Insights: Google provides analytics that show how customers find and interact with your listing, allowing you to tailor your marketing efforts effectively. You can see how many people clicked on your phone number or visited your website, giving you insights into what works.

Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Getting your profile up and running is straightforward. Follow these steps:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile Website: Head to business.google.com and sign in with your Google account. If you don’t have one, it’s easy to create a new account.
  • Enter Your Business Name: Make sure it matches how you present your business in Baldwin. If your business is known locally as 'Baldwin Pet Supplies,' don’t use abbreviations or variations.
  • Select Your Business Category: Choose a category that accurately reflects your services. For instance, if you run an Italian restaurant near the Baldwin Shopping Center, select 'Italian Restaurant' to ensure you attract the right customers.
  • Add Your Location: Provide your physical address so customers can find you easily. This is especially important for businesses that rely on foot traffic.
  • Verify Your Business: Google will send you a postcard with a verification code. Follow the instructions carefully to complete the process. Some businesses may also have the option to verify by phone or email, so check for those alternatives.

How Locals in Baldwin Search for Businesses

Understanding how residents search can help you tailor your profile. Here’s what to keep in mind:

  • Use Localized Queries: Many locals type in specific queries like 'best pizza near me' or 'plumbers in Baldwin.' Consider incorporating these phrases in your business description or posts.
  • Leverage Local Landmarks: Local landmarks, such as the Baldwin Borough Library or the community center, often feature in searches. Mentioning these landmarks in your posts or descriptions can help attract customers who are nearby.
  • Focus on Reviews: Review ratings can make or break a decision. Baldwin residents tend to trust recommendations from their community, so actively encourage your customers to leave reviews after their visits.
  • Ensure Mobile Friendliness: With many searches occurring on mobile devices, make sure your profile looks good on smartphones. A mobile-friendly profile is essential for attracting customers who are on the go.
