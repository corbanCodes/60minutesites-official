How Do I Get More Customers in Hattiesburg?

5 min read

If you've set up shop in Hattiesburg, you're likely already aware of the unique blend of Southern charm and local loyalty that makes this city such a vibrant place for business growth. With a rich history and a close-knit community, Hattiesburg offers incredible opportunities to attract and retain customers. So how can you harness this local magic to draw more people to your business? Let’s explore some specific strategies tailored for this lovely city.

Online Strategies to Attract Customers

Embracing digital marketing is essential for small businesses in Hattiesburg to thrive. Here are some detailed tactics you can implement:

  • Create a user-friendly website: Your website should not only showcase your products or services but also reflect the unique spirit of Hattiesburg. Use local imagery and testimonials to create a sense of familiarity. Ensure that it’s mobile-friendly, as many customers will search for local businesses on their phones.
  • Utilize social media: Platforms like Facebook and Instagram are powerful tools for connecting with the local community. Share stories about your business, highlight local partnerships, and engage with your audience through regular posts and updates. Consider running local-targeted ads to reach potential customers in Hattiesburg.
  • Implement search engine optimization (SEO): Work on local SEO by including Hattiesburg-related keywords in your website content. This might include phrases like “best coffee in Hattiesburg” or “Hattiesburg boutique.” Register your business with Google My Business to help it appear in local searches.
  • Engage with online reviews: Online reviews can significantly influence customer decisions. Encourage satisfied customers to leave positive feedback on platforms like Yelp and Google. Respond to reviews—both positive and negative—showing that you value customer opinions and are committed to improving their experience.
  • Consider email marketing: Build an email list by offering a discount or freebie in exchange for email sign-ups. Use this list to inform your customers about promotions, new product launches, or special events. Make your emails visually appealing and personalized to increase engagement.

Offline Strategies to Connect with Your Community

Don’t underestimate the power of direct, face-to-face connections in Hattiesburg. Here are some practical offline strategies to consider:

  • Participate in local events and festivals: Hattiesburg hosts numerous events throughout the year, such as the Hub City Beer Fest or the Hattiesburg Arts Council's annual events. Set up a booth or sponsor a local event to gain visibility and showcase your offerings to the community.
  • Collaborate with other local businesses: Form partnerships with neighboring businesses for cross-promotions. For instance, a local bakery could team up with a coffee shop to offer a special deal. This not only increases your reach but also fosters a sense of community.
  • Host workshops or informational sessions: Share your expertise by hosting free workshops or seminars. If you own a craft store, consider hosting a DIY project night. This not only brings foot traffic into your store but also positions you as a knowledgeable leader in your field.
  • Offer special discounts for first-time customers: Create enticing offers for newcomers to encourage them to visit your business. For example, offer a “first visit” discount or a free gift with their first purchase to make a lasting impression.
  • Join the Hattiesburg Chamber of Commerce: Becoming a member can provide invaluable networking opportunities. Attend meetings and events to connect with other local business owners and share insights. The Chamber often facilitates workshops that can help you sharpen your business skills.

Understanding the Hattiesburg Business Community

The Hattiesburg business community is not just comprised of individual ventures; it's a dynamic ecosystem full of support and collaboration. Engaging with local organizations and attending networking events can help you build meaningful relationships. Here are some key features of this vibrant community:

  • A focus on collaboration: Hattiesburg's small businesses often work together to promote mutual success. You’ll find that many local businesses are eager to support one another, facilitating a thriving partnership culture.
  • Supportive local residents: Hattiesburg residents take pride in shopping local. They appreciate the personal touch and unique offerings that local businesses provide. Make sure your marketing emphasizes the community-driven aspect of your business.
  • Access to resources: There are numerous resources available, such as business development workshops and mentorship programs through organizations like the Small Business Development Center (SBDC) at the University of Southern Mississippi. Take advantage of these opportunities to grow and refine your business strategies.
  • A thriving arts and culture scene: Hattiesburg is known for its rich arts and culture, from the Hattiesburg Cultural Center to the Saenger Theater. Connecting with local artists and cultural events can open doors for partnerships and customer engagement, creating a sense of community around your brand.

Building a customer base in Hattiesburg requires a thoughtful combination of online and offline efforts. By leveraging the unique aspects of this community and implementing these strategies, you can effectively reach your target audience.
