Why a Google Business Profile Matters

Creating a Google Business Profile is essential for any business in Walla Walla. It helps locals find you, learn about your services, and engage with your brand. Here's how to get started:

  • Visit the Google Business Profile website: Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now' to initiate the setup process.
  • Sign in or create an account: Use your existing Google account to sign in or create one if you don’t have it. This account will be your gateway to managing your business online.
  • Enter your business name: Make sure to use your official business name. This ensures consistency across platforms and helps locals recognize you.
  • Choose the appropriate category: Select a category that best fits your business. This helps Google show your profile to the right audience. For example, if you own a coffee shop, choose 'Coffee Shop' as your category.
  • Add your business address: Ensure your address is accurate and formatted correctly for local searches, as this is crucial for helping customers find you. Including nearby landmarks can enhance searchability.
  • Include your phone number and website link: This makes it easy for customers to reach you. Having an active website is an added bonus, as it provides more information about your services.
  • Upload high-quality photos: Showcase your business with attractive images of your space, products, or services. Pictures of your signature dishes or the inviting atmosphere of your establishment can entice potential customers.
  • Complete your profile: Fill in your business hours and any special services you offer, such as curbside pickup or delivery. Clear information helps customers know when and how to engage with your business.
  • Verify your business: Follow the verification process Google provides, which may involve receiving a postcard with a verification code at your business address. This step is crucial for legitimacy and helps build trust with potential customers.

How Locals in Walla Walla Search for Businesses

Walla Walla residents typically search for businesses with urgency and a focus on local relevance. They often use specific keywords influenced by the community's landmarks and favorites. To optimize your Google Business Profile for local searches, consider these points:

  • Incorporate local terms: Use phrases that resonate with Walla Walla residents, such as 'wine tasting near Pioneer Park' or 'restaurants close to the Whitman Mission'. This local flavor will improve your visibility in searches.
  • Encourage reviews: Actively seek reviews from your local clientele. Positive feedback can significantly boost your profile's visibility and credibility. Consider offering discounts or incentives to customers who leave reviews.
  • Keep your profile updated: Regularly update your profile with current promotions, events, or local happenings, such as the Walla Walla Fair or the annual Wine & Food Festival. This not only keeps your audience engaged but also signals to Google that your business is active.
  • Utilize Google Posts: Share news or updates that resonate with the local community. For instance, if you're hosting a special event or introducing a new menu item, create a Google Post to inform your audience directly through Google search results.
