How to Rank on Google Maps in St. Louis

5 min read

Look, I get it: you want your business to shine on Google Maps in St. Louis. It’s like having a neon sign that draws in foot traffic, but achieving that visibility requires a bit of strategy and dedication. Whether you’re nestled in the bustling Central West End or the vibrant Soulard district, your business can attract more customers with the right approach. Here’s how you can climb the ranks on Google Maps and stand out in this vibrant city.

Claim and Optimize Your Google Business Profile

The first step to ranking on Google Maps in St. Louis is to claim your Google Business Profile. This is your free listing where potential customers find key information about your business. Think of it as your online storefront. Here’s how to make it shine:

  • Go to Google Business Profile: Visit the Google Business Profile website and click 'Manage now' to start the claiming process.
  • Complete all necessary information: Fill in your business name, address, phone number, and website. Make sure these details are accurate to avoid losing potential customers.
  • Select the right categories: Choose categories that truly represent your business. For example, if you run a coffee shop in the Delmar Loop, select 'Coffee Shop' as your primary category but also consider adding 'Cafe' or 'Bakery' if applicable.
  • Add high-quality photos: Showcase your products or services with stunning images. People love visuals, so include pictures of your offerings, your storefront, and even your team to give a personal touch.
  • Include business hours: Clearly state your business hours and any special hours during holidays or local events, like the St. Louis Mardi Gras, to keep your customers informed.

Collect and Respond to Customer Reviews

Customer reviews are a crucial factor in enhancing your local ranking on Google Maps. Not only do they build trust, but they also signal to Google that your business is worth featuring. Here’s how to effectively manage your reviews:

  • Ask for reviews: Don’t hesitate to ask your satisfied customers to leave positive reviews. You can do this through follow-up emails, after a purchase, or even with a sign in your store.
  • Respond to all reviews: Whether positive or negative, respond promptly to all reviews. Thank customers for their kind words or address concerns with professionalism and a willingness to resolve issues.
  • Aim for a mix of reviews: It’s okay if not all your reviews are five stars. Potential customers appreciate authenticity, so a few less-than-perfect reviews can actually build credibility.
  • Encourage location-specific details: When asking for reviews, encourage customers to include specifics about their experiences, like mentioning the neighborhood or local landmarks nearby, which can help improve your local SEO.

Local SEO: Optimize for St. Louis Keywords

Utilizing local keywords specific to St. Louis can help your business gain visibility. This means weaving those terms into your content on your website, social media, and even your Google Business Profile. Here are some actionable strategies:

  • Incorporate local phrases: Use keywords like 'best pizza in St. Louis' or 'top coffee shop in the Delmar Loop' throughout your website and profiles. This helps Google understand your relevance to local searches.
  • Write blog posts: Create engaging content about local events, attractions, or topics that relate to your business. For example, if you own a bakery, consider writing about the best pastries to enjoy during the St. Louis Art Fair.
  • Use localized images: When posting images on your website, use alt text that incorporates local keywords. For instance, if you have a photo of your shop, use alt text like 'Delicious donuts at [Your Business Name] in St. Louis' to boost relevancy.
  • Maintain consistent NAP: Ensure your Name, Address, and Phone number are consistent across all platforms, including your website and social media, to help Google verify your business information.

Engage with Local Community and Online Networks

Engagement is key to building your brand's presence in the St. Louis area. The more connected you are to local events and community activities, the more visible your business will be. Here’s how to foster those connections:

  • Participate in local events: Get involved in St. Louis festivals, farmers' markets, or charity runs. These activities not only increase your visibility but also help you connect with your community.
  • Join local business groups: Social media platforms like Facebook often have groups dedicated to local businesses. Engage with fellow business owners and potential customers in these spaces.
  • Collaborate with other businesses: Consider cross-promotions with other local businesses. For instance, if you run a bookstore, partner with a local coffee shop for a 'Books and Brews' event.
  • Share local news: Keep your audience informed about St. Louis happenings by sharing local news and updates on your social media. This shows that you’re engaged with the community and keeps your content relevant.

Utilize Social Media to Boost Visibility

Social media is a powerful tool to drive traffic to your Google Maps listing. By posting regularly and engaging with your audience, you increase your chances of being discovered. Here are some ways to make the most of your social media presence:

  • Highlight unique offerings: Share posts that showcase what makes your business special. Whether it’s a signature dish or a unique service, let your followers know what sets you apart.
  • Use local hashtags: Incorporate hashtags like #StLouisEats or #ShopLocalSTL to reach a broader audience within the St. Louis community.
  • Encourage user-generated content: Ask customers to tag you in their posts when they visit your business. This not only promotes your brand but also creates a sense of community around your business.
  • Promote your Google Maps link: Include your Google Maps link in your social media profiles and posts to make it easy for potential customers to find you.

Getting your business to rank on Google Maps in St. Louis takes time and effort, but with the right strategies, you can elevate your visibility. Remember, every small step counts, from optimizing your Google Business Profile to engaging with your local community.
