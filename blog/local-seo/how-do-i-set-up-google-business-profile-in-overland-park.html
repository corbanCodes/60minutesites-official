What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here’s the secret: a well-optimized Google Business Profile can help your business stand out in Overland Park's competitive market. To get started, make sure you include these key elements:

  • Accurate business name: Use your official business name to ensure consistency.
  • Address and phone number: Ensure they are correct, especially since many locals rely on Google Maps to navigate the area.
  • Business category: Choose the most relevant category, such as 'family restaurant' or 'landscaping services,' so customers know what you offer.
  • Business hours: Keep these updated, especially for holidays and local events like the Overland Park Fall Festival.
  • Photos: Upload clear, high-quality images of your products, services, and location to attract attention; consider showcasing local landmarks or popular community events.
  • Customer reviews: Encourage happy customers to leave reviews, as they build trust with potential clients and can influence new customers who are looking for services in the area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I claim my Google Business Profile?

Simply search for your business on Google, and if it appears, you can claim it by following the prompts. Make sure to verify your business through the postcard that Google sends to your registered address.

Q: Can I update my profile anytime?

Yes! You can update details like hours, photos, and services at any time to keep your profile fresh, especially during busy seasons like the holidays or local events.

Q: How long does it take for changes to reflect?

Usually, changes appear almost instantly, but some updates, particularly for reviews or major changes, might take a few hours to show up publicly.

Q: What if I don’t have a physical location?

You can still create a profile by selecting the service area option and indicating where you serve your customers. Many service-based businesses in Overland Park operate this way.
