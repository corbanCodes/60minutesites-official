How Do I Get More Customers in Lakewood?

9 min read

Getting customers in Lakewood, Colorado can be a rewarding journey if you know where to focus your efforts. With a mix of local charm, beautiful parks, a vibrant arts scene, and growing business opportunities, it’s crucial to leverage both online and offline strategies to stand out in this dynamic community.

Building Your Online Presence

The truth is, a professional website is the foundation of getting customers online. If you don't have a solid site, you're missing out. Here's what actually works:

  • Make sure your website is user-friendly and mobile-responsive, as many residents rely on their phones for local information.
  • Use local SEO strategies to optimize for terms specific to Lakewood, such as 'Lakewood family restaurants' or 'Lakewood home services'.
  • Create valuable content that speaks to your audience's needs and interests, such as guides on local attractions like the Lakewood Cultural Center or the Belmar shopping district.
  • Claim and optimize your Google My Business listing to show up in local searches, ensuring your address and operating hours are correct.
  • Engage with potential customers through your blog and social media platforms, sharing news about local events, like the Lakewood Farmers Market or concerts at the Lakewood Heritage Center.

Harnessing the Power of Reviews

I've seen this work time and time again: positive reviews can be a game changer for local businesses. Here's how to get started:

  • Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook, especially after they visit local hotspots like the Lakewood Dog Park or the stunning Bear Creek Lake Park.
  • Respond to all reviews, both positive and negative, to show you care about customer feedback and are committed to improving your services.
  • Share testimonials on your website and social media to build trust, showcasing how your business supports the local Lakewood community.

Leveraging Referrals and Networking

Referrals are one of the most powerful ways to gain new customers. Here’s how to make it happen:

  • Build relationships with other local businesses for cross-referrals, especially those located in popular areas like the West Colfax Avenue business district.
  • Attend local networking events to meet potential customers and partners; consider joining the Lakewood Chamber of Commerce for access to resources and events.
  • Implement a referral program that rewards existing customers for bringing in new ones, potentially offering discounts for referrals that lead to sales.

Engaging with the Local Community

Getting involved in the Lakewood community can significantly boost your visibility. Here’s what to do:

  • Sponsor local events or sports teams, especially those at Lakewood High School or community centers, to gain exposure and goodwill.
  • Participate in farmers markets or fairs, such as the annual Lakewood Arts Festival, to showcase your offerings and connect directly with residents.
  • Collaborate with local organizations for community service projects, helping to foster a sense of community and brand recognition.

The hustle of running a local business in Lakewood is real, but with the right strategies in place, you can attract more customers and thrive in this community. Keep pushing forward and don’t hesitate to reach out for help along the way. Remember that leveraging local charm and community involvement will be key to your success in this beautiful city.
