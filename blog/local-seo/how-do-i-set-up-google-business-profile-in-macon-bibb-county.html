What Makes a Great Google Business Profile

Here's the secret to standing out on Google: an optimized profile. Trust me on this one; it’s not just about filling out forms. It’s about creating an inviting presence. To craft a great Google Business Profile, focus on these key areas:

  • Accurate business name, address, and phone number (NAP)
  • High-quality photos of your business and products, showcasing local flavor and community involvement
  • Engaging business description with relevant keywords that highlight your unique offerings, such as Southern cuisine or artisanal crafts
  • Regular updates with posts about promotions, events, or news, including local festivals like the Cherry Blossom Festival or special events at the Macon Centreplex
  • Customer reviews and ratings—encourage happy customers to leave feedback, as community recommendations weigh heavily in Macon-Bibb County.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set up my Google Business Profile?

Start by visiting the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now.' Follow the prompts to enter your business information, ensuring you include your location in relation to well-known spots like the Ocmulgee National Monument or the Allman Brothers Band Museum.

Q: Can I edit my profile after it's set up?

Absolutely! You can update your profile anytime to add new photos, change your hours, or post updates about local events or new services.

Q: How do I get more reviews?

Encourage your satisfied customers to leave a review by sending them a follow-up message or simply asking them in person, particularly after they’ve enjoyed a meal at your restaurant or a service at your salon.

Q: What if my business hours change?

Just log into your Google Business Profile and update your hours. Keeping this info current helps customers know when to visit you, especially during local events when foot traffic increases.
