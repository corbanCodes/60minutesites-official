Why You Need a Google Business Profile

Having a Google Business Profile is essential for your visibility in Erlanger. Here’s why it matters:

  • Helps potential customers find your business on Google Search and Maps: Local search results often prioritize businesses with a Google Business Profile. This means that when someone searches for services you offer, your business is more likely to appear at the top of the list.
  • Allows you to share important information: Your profile is a platform where you can provide essential details such as business hours, location, and the services you offer. This helps customers make informed decisions before they even contact you.
  • Enables customer reviews: Customer reviews are critical for building trust and credibility. Potential clients often read reviews to gauge the quality of your services, so positive feedback can significantly influence their choice.
  • Showcases your offerings: Uploading high-quality photos of your products or services can make your listing more appealing. For example, if you run a local bakery, photos of your delicious pastries can entice passersby, turning online interest into foot traffic.

How to Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Setting up your profile is straightforward. Follow these steps to ensure you get it right:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile page: Click on 'Manage now' to begin your setup.
  • Sign in to your Google account: If you don’t have a Google account, you can easily create one. This account will be central to managing your business profile.
  • Enter your business name and address: It's crucial to choose the correct category for your business, whether it's a restaurant, retail shop, or service provider. For instance, if you're a family-owned diner in Erlanger, make sure to categorize it accordingly to attract local diners.
  • Add your phone number and website URL: This is where customers can reach you. If you don’t have a website yet, 60 Minute Sites can help you build one quickly and efficiently, ensuring you establish an online presence that complements your Google Business Profile.
  • Verify your business: Google will require you to verify your business through a phone call, email, or postcard. This step is vital to confirm that you are the legitimate owner of the business listed.
  • Optimize your profile: Once verified, enhance your profile by adding photos, business hours, and a detailed description of your services. A well-optimized profile can set you apart from competitors and entice more clients to choose you.
