Setting Up Your Google Business Profile

Here’s the secret to getting started with your Google Business Profile:

  • Go to the Google Business Profile website and click on 'Manage now'.
  • Enter your business name and address. Make sure it’s accurate, as locals in neighborhoods like Sango and St. Bethlehem rely on this information.
  • Choose the right business category that best describes what you offer. This helps Google show your business to the right people.
  • Add your phone number and website. If you don’t have a website yet, consider using social media as a placeholder.
  • Fill out your business hours. Make sure they are up to date, especially around local events or holidays like Rivers and Spires Festival.
  • Add photos of your business. High-quality images of your products, services, or even your team can make a big difference in attracting customers.
  • Verify your business by following Google’s verification process, which usually involves receiving a postcard in the mail with a verification code.

Optimizing Your Google Business Profile

Once your profile is set up, it’s time to optimize it for maximum visibility:

  • Write a compelling business description. Share what makes your business unique and include relevant keywords, especially those that connect to the Clarksville market.
  • Encourage customer reviews. Ask satisfied customers to leave positive feedback, as this can significantly boost your ranking. Local businesses thrive on word-of-mouth, and reviews create trust.
  • Respond to reviews, both positive and negative, to show that you value customer feedback and are engaged with the community.
  • Keep your information updated. If there are changes in hours, services, or offerings, make sure they reflect on your profile, especially during busy seasons like back-to-school or holidays.
  • Utilize posts to share special offers, events, or news about your business. This keeps your profile engaging and fresh, particularly during local events like the Clarksville Downtown Market.

Q: How long does it take for my Google Business Profile to appear in search results?

It can take a few days after verification for your business to show up, so be patient!

Q: Can I change my business hours?

Absolutely! You can update your business hours anytime through your profile, which is especially useful during special events or holidays.
