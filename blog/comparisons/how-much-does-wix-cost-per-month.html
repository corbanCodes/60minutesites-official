How Much Does Wix Cost Per Month

5 min read

Have you ever wondered if the cost of building your dream website could feel as light as a feather or as heavy as a brick? The answer lies in understanding the financial landscape of Wix, a popular platform for those looking to create an online presence. Wix provides a powerful, user-friendly interface that allows anyone, from aspiring entrepreneurs to seasoned professionals, to design a stunning website without needing extensive technical expertise. The cost of using Wix can vary significantly based on the features you need and the size of your project. Let's dive into the details so you can make an informed decision.

Understanding Wix Pricing

Wix offers a variety of plans tailored to different needs and budgets, making it accessible for everyone. Here’s a more detailed breakdown:

  • Free Plan: This plan is great for those who want to try out Wix without any financial commitment. However, it comes with limited features and displays Wix branding on your site, which might not be ideal for a professional appearance.
  • Combo Plan: Priced at approximately $14/month, this plan is perfect for personal use or small projects. It removes Wix ads, giving your website a cleaner look, and includes 3GB of storage and 2GB of bandwidth, making it suitable for a simple blog or portfolio.
  • Unlimited Plan: At around $18/month, this plan is designed for entrepreneurs and freelancers. It offers unlimited bandwidth and 10GB of storage, providing ample resources for growing websites. It also includes additional features such as a site booster app for better SEO.
  • Business Basic: For about $23/month, this plan is crafted for small businesses looking to sell online. It allows for online payments and comes with 20GB of storage, along with features tailored for e-commerce, such as a product catalog and secure checkout.
  • Business Unlimited: Roughly $27/month, this plan is ideal for growing businesses. It offers unlimited storage and additional marketing tools like a logo maker and social media logo integration. This can significantly enhance your brand's online presence.
  • Business VIP: At about $49/month, the Business VIP plan includes all the features of the previous plans with some added perks, such as priority support. This level is suitable for larger businesses that need advanced functionalities like subscriptions and advanced analytics.

Additional Costs to Consider

While the base price is essential, you should also consider extra costs that might arise during the website building process:

  • Domain Registration: If you want a custom domain name (e.g., www.yourbusiness.com), you can expect to pay around $10-20 per year. Some plans include this cost, but it's important to check.
  • Premium Apps: Wix offers a plethora of apps to enhance your website’s functionality. Some of these are free, but many require a subscription. Depending on your needs, costs can range from a few dollars a month to over $20 for more advanced features.
  • Upgrades: As your website grows, you might find the need to upgrade your plan for additional storage or features. This could mean moving from the Combo to the Unlimited plan, which will increase your monthly expenses accordingly.

How to Decide on a Plan

Choosing the right plan depends on your specific needs. Here are some key considerations to help you make an informed choice:

  • Your Business Goals: Are you running a blog, an online store, or a portfolio? Understanding your primary objective will help narrow down the options. For instance, if you're an artist showcasing your work, the Combo Plan might suffice initially.
  • Website Traffic: Estimate how many visitors you expect on your site. If you're anticipating high traffic, choose a plan with more bandwidth to ensure smooth performance. The Unlimited Plan could be a good fit here.
  • Features Needed: Consider if you require e-commerce functionalities, advanced analytics, or marketing tools. If selling products online is a priority, the Business Basic or higher plans would be more suitable.

Understanding how much Wix costs per month can help you budget for your new website effectively.
