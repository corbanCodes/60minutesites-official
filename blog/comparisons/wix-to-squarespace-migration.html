How to Migrate from Wix to Squarespace

5 min read

Get a Professional Comparisons Website in 60 Minutes →

Let me share something important: switching website platforms can feel daunting, but it doesn't have to be. If you're looking to migrate from Wix to Squarespace, you're making a choice that can open up new design possibilities and better functionality for your site. Whether you're an artist wanting to showcase your portfolio, a small business owner aiming to elevate your online store, or a blogger looking for a more dynamic platform, Squarespace offers a world of opportunities that can help you achieve your goals.

Why Move from Wix to Squarespace?

Before diving into the migration process, let’s explore why you might want to make this switch:

  • More Advanced Design Options: Squarespace provides a stunning array of templates designed for various industries, from photography to e-commerce. Unlike Wix, which can sometimes feel templated, Squarespace’s designs are polished and modern, allowing for a unique expression of your brand.
  • Robust E-commerce Capabilities: If you're running a business, Squarespace's built-in e-commerce features are a game-changer. They offer tools for inventory management, product variants, and customizable checkout experiences, which make it easier for customers to shop on your site.
  • Better Blogging and Content Management: Squarespace’s blogging platform is intuitive and equipped with features like social sharing, scheduling posts, and beautiful layouts, making it a breeze to manage your content and engage with your audience.
  • Professional Aesthetic: Squarespace sites have a reputation for looking sleek and professional. This can be crucial in establishing trust with your audience and can significantly enhance your brand's perceived value.

Preparing for Your Migration

Transitioning your site requires a bit of prep work. Here’s what you should do before starting:

  • Backup Your Wix Site: Start by saving all crucial content from your Wix site. This includes copying text, downloading images, and exporting any videos. You can use the built-in Wix export tools to make this process smoother.
  • Catalog Your Current Pages: Make a comprehensive list of all the pages on your Wix site. This will serve as a roadmap for your new site, ensuring you don’t miss any essential content during migration.
  • Gather Important Data: If you have email subscribers, product lists, or customer data, compile them in a secure location. Consider exporting your email list as a CSV file to easily import into your new platform later.
  • Visualize Your New Site: Take time to sketch out or use digital tools to conceptualize how you want your Squarespace site to look. Consider what features and functionalities you want to include, such as galleries, forms, or ecommerce capabilities. Research Squarespace templates that resonate with your vision and align with your brand's aesthetic.

Step-by-Step Guide to Migrating

Now, let’s break down the steps you need to follow to complete the migration:

  • Sign Up for Squarespace: Begin by creating a Squarespace account. Choose a template that not only looks good but also fits the functionality you need. Spend some time exploring the templates available, as each one comes with unique features that may suit your specific requirements.
  • Recreate Your Pages: Using your list from the previous step, manually recreate your pages in Squarespace. Pay attention to layout and design elements that enhance the user experience based on how your Wix site was structured.
  • Transfer Content: Copy and paste text content from Wix to Squarespace. Ensure that the formatting remains intact; Squarespace offers rich text editing tools that can help you maintain the look and feel of your original content.
  • Upload Media: For images and videos, upload them directly to your new site. Take advantage of Squarespace’s media library to keep your files organized. Use high-quality images to enhance your site’s visual appeal.
  • Set Up Your Navigation: Create a navigation menu that mirrors your previous site for a seamless transition for your users. Ensure that it is intuitive and easy to navigate, allowing visitors to find what they need without frustration.
  • Blog Migration: If you have a blog, manually transfer blog posts or consider using Squarespace’s import tool for quicker migration. This tool can help streamline the process, but be sure to check each post for formatting and links afterwards.

Final Touches and Launch

Once everything is set up, follow these final steps:

  • Test Functionality: Before going live, check all links, forms, and functionalities to ensure everything works smoothly. Test your navigation, contact forms, and any interactive elements to guarantee a seamless user experience.
  • Optimize for SEO: Take this opportunity to optimize your site for search engines. Set up page titles and descriptions that reflect your content accurately. Don’t forget to add alt text to images, which not only helps with SEO but also improves accessibility.
  • Connect Your Domain: If you have an existing domain, follow Squarespace’s guidelines to connect it to your new site. This is a crucial step to ensure your audience can find you at the same web address.
  • Preview and Adjust: Use the preview function to see your site as visitors will. Make any last-minute adjustments to layout, text, and images. This is also a good time to ask a friend or colleague to review the site and provide feedback.

Migrating from Wix to Squarespace can enhance your online presence significantly. Embrace the change and the new possibilities it brings.
