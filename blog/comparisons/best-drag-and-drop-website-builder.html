  • Mobile responsiveness: In 2026, ensuring your site looks great on all devices is non-negotiable. A good builder should automatically optimize your site for mobile viewing, which is crucial since more users are browsing via smartphones. Squarespace excels in this area, offering responsive design templates.

Be sure to choose a builder that allows you to tweak colors, fonts, and layouts. For example, Weebly offers a range of templates that can be tailored to reflect your brand’s personality.

This is essential for streamlining your workflow. WordPress.com, for instance, has a vast library of plugins that can enhance your site's capabilities.
