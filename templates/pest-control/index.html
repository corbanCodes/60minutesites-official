Best Practices

What Makes a Great Pest Control Website

Pest control websites that generate leads share these essential elements.

Emergency Services

Prominently display 24/7 emergency contact options. Pest emergencies happen anytime and customers need to reach you immediately.

Service Areas

Clearly list the cities, counties, and zip codes you serve. Help visitors quickly confirm you service their location.

Pest Types

Detail all pests you handle: termites, bed bugs, rodents, ants, roaches, wasps, wildlife. Dedicated pages for each pest type improve SEO.

Licensing Display

Show your pest control license number, insurance, and certifications. Customers want to know they are hiring a legitimate, licensed operator.

Easy Scheduling

Online booking forms and click-to-call buttons make scheduling easy. Capture leads 24/7 even when you are out on service calls.

Customer Reviews

Display testimonials and link to Google reviews. Social proof builds trust for a service that requires entering customers homes.
