Best Practices

What Makes a Great Spa Website

Spa websites that convert visitors into clients share these essential elements.

Treatment Menu

Display your complete service menu with detailed descriptions, durations, and pricing. Help clients understand exactly what each treatment offers.

Online Booking

Enable 24/7 appointment scheduling. Integrate with booking platforms so clients can book their preferred treatment and time instantly.

Ambiance Photos

Showcase your serene environment with high-quality photos of treatment rooms, relaxation areas, and spa facilities. Let visitors feel the calm.

Spa Packages

Feature bundled treatment packages that increase average booking value. Highlight couples experiences, day spa packages, and seasonal specials.

Gift Cards

Offer digital gift card purchases for easy gifting. Gift cards are a major revenue stream for spas, especially during holidays.

Client Reviews

Display testimonials and reviews prominently. Social proof builds trust and helps potential clients choose your spa over competitors.

FAQ

Spa Website Questions

A spa website should include a detailed treatment menu with descriptions and pricing, online booking capabilities, high-quality ambiance photos showcasing your space, spa package offerings, gift card purchasing options, client reviews, team member profiles, hours of operation, and clear contact information. Our templates include all these features built-in.

Online booking is essential for modern spa businesses. Studies show 67% of clients prefer booking appointments online. It reduces phone calls, fills last-minute openings, and allows 24/7 booking even when your spa is closed. Our templates integrate with popular booking platforms like Calendly, Acuity, Square, and spa-specific systems.

Yes, displaying treatment prices builds trust and helps clients plan their visits. Transparent pricing reduces phone inquiry calls and attracts clients who are ready to book. Include starting prices for variable treatments (like "from $85") and highlight package deals to encourage higher-value bookings.

Use professional photography that captures your spa's ambiance - the soft lighting, serene colors, and peaceful environment. Include photos of treatment rooms, relaxation lounges, water features, and any unique elements. Our templates feature dedicated gallery sections and full-width image displays to immerse visitors in your spa's atmosphere.

Absolutely. Gift cards are a significant revenue stream for spas. Our templates include dedicated gift card sections and can integrate with platforms like Square, GiftUp, or your existing POS system. Make gift cards prominent on your site, especially before holidays and special occasions.
