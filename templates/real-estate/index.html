FAQ

Real Estate Website Questions

Common questions from agents and brokers about our website service.

Can I display my MLS listings on the site?

Our templates are designed to showcase your featured listings with beautiful galleries. For full IDX/MLS integration with property search, we can discuss add-on options or integrate with your preferred IDX provider.

Will the site work if I change brokerages?

Absolutely. Your website is yours regardless of brokerage affiliation. We simply update the branding and brokerage information. Your domain, content, and online presence move with you.

Can I have a team website with multiple agents?

Yes! Our templates support team structures with individual agent profiles, shared listings, and team branding. Whether you are a solo agent or team leader, we customize to your structure.

How do I update listings and sold properties?

Simply email us with updates and we handle the changes within 24 hours. Small text updates are included in your monthly fee. No technical skills required on your end.

Do you help with real estate SEO?

Every site includes SEO best practices: proper meta tags, local keywords, fast loading, mobile optimization, and structured data. This helps you rank for searches like "realtor in [your city]".

Can clients book showings or consultations online?

Yes! We integrate with Calendly or your preferred booking system. Visitors can schedule buyer consultations, listing appointments, or property showings directly from your site.
