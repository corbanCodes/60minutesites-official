Best Practices

What Makes a Great Painter Website

Painting contractor websites that generate leads share these essential elements.

Portfolio Gallery

Showcase completed projects with high-quality photos organized by type - interior, exterior, residential, and commercial painting work.

Before-After Photos

Nothing sells painting services like dramatic before-and-after transformations. Show the impact of your work with side-by-side comparisons.

Service Areas

Clearly list the cities, neighborhoods, and regions you serve. Help homeowners quickly confirm you work in their area.

Free Estimates

Prominent free estimate request forms on every page. Capture leads by making it easy for visitors to request quotes.

Mobile Responsive

Homeowners research painters on their phones. Your site must look great and work perfectly on every device.

Customer Reviews

Display testimonials and link to Google reviews. Social proof builds trust and helps homeowners choose your painting company.

FAQ

Painter Website Questions

A painting contractor website should include a homepage, services page detailing interior and exterior painting options, portfolio gallery with before-after photos, about page with your experience and team info, service areas listing where you work, and contact page with free estimate form. Additional pages for color consultation services, commercial painting, and specialty finishes help attract specific customer types.

Create a portfolio gallery with high-quality before-and-after photos of completed projects. Take photos in good lighting showing the full transformation. Organize by project type (interior, exterior, cabinets, commercial). Include details about paint brands used, colors chosen, and any challenges overcome. Our painter templates include built-in before-after comparison sliders that make transformations dramatic.

Absolutely. Free estimate requests are the primary way painting contractors generate leads online. Make your estimate form prominent on every page, ask for project details like square footage and room count, and allow photo uploads. Respond to estimate requests quickly - speed matters. Our templates include optimized estimate request forms designed to capture qualified leads.

List the cities, towns, and neighborhoods you serve prominently on your homepage and in a dedicated service area section. For larger service areas, consider individual pages for major cities you target. Include a map showing your coverage area. Clear service area information helps visitors quickly confirm you work in their location and improves local SEO.

Yes. Displaying logos of premium paint brands you use (Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, etc.) builds credibility and signals quality. Homeowners recognize these brands and associate them with professional results. Include information about why you choose specific paints and the benefits they provide for durability and finish quality.
