Best Practices

What Makes a Great Music Website

Music websites that build fanbases and get bookings share these essential elements.

Audio/Video Integration

Embed your tracks from Spotify, SoundCloud, or Bandcamp. Showcase music videos and live performances with seamless video players.

Event Calendar

Display upcoming shows, tours, and appearances in an easy-to-browse calendar. Let fans know where to see you live next.

Artist Bio

Tell your story with a compelling biography. Share your journey, influences, and what drives your music. Connect personally with fans.

Contact/Booking

Make it easy for venues, promoters, and collaborators to reach you. Professional booking forms that capture all the details you need.

Mobile Responsive

Fans discover music on their phones. Your site must look amazing and play media smoothly on every device and screen size.

Social Links

Connect all your platforms in one place. Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Spotify, and more. Make it easy for fans to follow you everywhere.
