Best Practices

What Makes a Great Single Page Website

Single page websites that convert visitors share these essential elements.

Clear Sections

Distinct, well-organized sections guide visitors through your story. Each section has a clear purpose and flows naturally into the next.

Smooth Scrolling

Seamless navigation between sections creates a polished experience. Click menu links that smoothly scroll to the right spot on the page.

Strong CTA

Strategic call-to-action buttons throughout the page. Multiple opportunities to convert without feeling pushy or overwhelming.

Fast Loading

Optimized images and clean code ensure your single page loads quickly. No waiting means lower bounce rates and higher conversions.

Mobile Perfect

Single page sites shine on mobile devices. Natural scrolling behavior matches how people use their phones every day.

Contact Form

Built-in contact form at the bottom of the page. Visitors can reach out immediately without navigating away from your content.

FAQ

Single Page Website Questions

A single page website contains all your content on one scrolling page instead of multiple separate pages. Visitors navigate by scrolling down or clicking navigation links that smoothly scroll to different sections. Single page sites are ideal for businesses with focused messaging, portfolios, events, and companies that want a clean, modern online presence.

Single page websites work best for businesses with focused offerings, freelancers, portfolios, events, product launches, and startups. They excel when you have a clear story to tell and a single conversion goal. Multi-page sites are better for content-heavy businesses, e-commerce stores, or companies with many distinct services. If your core message fits on one page without overwhelming visitors, single page is often more effective.

Single page websites can rank well for focused keywords, especially for local businesses targeting specific search terms. While multi-page sites offer more keyword targeting opportunities, a well-optimized single page site with proper headings, fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, and quality content performs well for targeted searches. We optimize every template for search engines.

Navigation on single page sites uses anchor links that smoothly scroll to different sections of the page. The navigation menu typically stays fixed at the top as you scroll, making it easy to jump between sections. This creates an intuitive experience that feels natural on both desktop and mobile devices.
