Best Practices

What Makes a Great Landing Page

High-converting landing pages share these essential elements that turn visitors into leads.

Clear Call-to-Action

One primary CTA that stands out visually and tells visitors exactly what action to take. No ambiguity, no confusion.

Single Focus

One offer, one message, one goal. Remove distractions and navigation that could lead visitors away from your conversion goal.

Lead Capture Form

Strategic form placement with minimal fields. Ask only for what you need. Every extra field reduces conversions.

Mobile Optimized

Over half of traffic comes from mobile devices. Your landing page must look perfect and convert on every screen size.

Fast Loading

Every second of load time costs conversions. Our landing pages load in under 3 seconds to keep visitors engaged.

Trust Elements

Testimonials, reviews, trust badges, and social proof that build credibility and reduce friction in the conversion process.

FAQ

Landing Page Questions

A landing page is a single-purpose page designed to drive one specific action, such as capturing leads, booking appointments, or making a sale. Unlike multi-page websites with navigation and multiple goals, landing pages remove distractions and focus visitors on a single call-to-action. This focused approach results in significantly higher conversion rates than general websites.

High-converting landing pages include a compelling headline that addresses the visitor's problem, a clear value proposition explaining the benefit, a single focused CTA that stands out visually, trust elements like testimonials and reviews, mobile-responsive design, fast loading speeds, and minimal navigation to keep visitors focused. Our templates include all these elements pre-built and optimized.

Effective lead capture uses simple forms asking only for essential information. The fewer fields, the higher your conversion rate. Place forms prominently above the fold and repeat them throughout the page. Offer something valuable in exchange like a free consultation, quote, estimate, or resource. Our templates include optimized form layouts designed for maximum conversions.

Absolutely. Landing pages are ideal for paid advertising campaigns because they match the specific offer in your ads and focus visitors on one conversion goal. This message match improves your Quality Score in Google Ads, reduces cost per click, and dramatically increases conversion rates compared to sending traffic to a general website homepage.
