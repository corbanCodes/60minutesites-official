Best Practices

What Makes a Great HVAC Website

HVAC websites that generate service calls share these essential elements.

Emergency Contact

Display your emergency phone number prominently in the header. When a furnace fails at midnight or AC dies in summer, customers need to reach you instantly.

Service Areas

Clearly define which cities and zip codes you serve. Help visitors quickly confirm you provide HVAC services in their area.

Seasonal Services

Highlight seasonal maintenance like AC tune-ups in spring and furnace inspections in fall. Promote maintenance plans to build recurring revenue.

Certifications

Display EPA certifications, NATE credentials, manufacturer authorizations, and state licenses. Trust badges convert visitors into customers.

Online Scheduling

Let customers book service appointments 24/7. Online scheduling dramatically increases conversions, especially for non-emergency maintenance.

Customer Reviews

Display testimonials and link to Google reviews. Social proof is essential for service businesses that enter customers' homes.
