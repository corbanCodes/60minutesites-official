Our Story

We started 60 Minute Sites because we saw a problem: small business owners were either paying thousands for custom websites that took months to build, or settling for clunky DIY builders that looked unprofessional.

There had to be a better way. So we built it.

Our approach is simple: we created 560+ professionally designed templates across 28 industries. When you choose one, we customize it with your content and launch it - all in about 60 minutes. You get a beautiful, professional website at a fraction of the cost and time of traditional web design.

No technical skills required. No waiting weeks for revisions. Just tell us about your business, and we handle the rest.
